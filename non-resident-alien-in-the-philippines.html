Even if they were not involved in the conspiracy themselves, their knowledge of the plot and failure to report it could still make them liable for the crime of Misprision of Treason.

B. Karlo and Chang Xiao can be charged with the crime of Treason, which is the act of betraying one's country by levying war against it or adhering to its enemies, giving them aid or comfort. The fact that they were conspiring to commit treason during a time of war with China would make their actions even more egregious and punishable under the law. If convicted, they could face severe penalties, including imprisonment or even death.

8. Bernardo, a Filipino citizen, and Dexter, a resident US Citizen in the Philippines, were able to overhear Karlo, also a Filipino citizen and Chang Xiao, a Chinese resident alien, who conspired to commit the crime of treason while the Philippines is in a state of war with China. Bernardo and Dexter did not disclose the information they heard to the proper authorities. A. What crime can be charged against Bernardo and Dexter? B. What crime can be charged against Karlo and Chang Xiao?


Answer:

A. Bernardo and Dexter can be charged with the crime of Misprision of Treason. This offense is committed when a person who has knowledge of the commission of treason does not disclose the same to the proper authorities as soon as possible.

B. Karlo and Chang Xiao can be charged with the crime of Conspiracy to Commit Treason. Conspiracy to commit treason happens when two or more persons come to an agreement to overthrow the government or to levy war against it. It does not require the actual commission of the crime. The mere act of agreeing to commit treason is punishable by law.

Taxable Net Income and Income Tax Due

Taxable net income is the amount of income that is subject to taxation after deducting allowable expenses and exemptions. Then the tax authorities use it to determine how much income tax a taxpayer owes. Income tax due is the actual amount of tax that a taxpayer owes to the government based on their taxable net income. It is calculated by applying the applicable tax rate to the taxable net income.
