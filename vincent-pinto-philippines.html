24. Vincent’s father is three times as old as Vincent. Five years ago he was 4 times as old as Vincent. How old is each of them now?Solution to the word problem?​


Answer:

Vincent's father is 45 and vincent is 15

Step-by-step explanation:

In the first sentence it says Vincent's father is three times as old as Vincent. In that sentence it says that Vincent's father is 3 times as old as Vincent but since we don't know Vincent's age we represent his age with a variable. So the present age of Vincent's father is 3x and Vincent's is x. The next sentence says 5 years ago meaning we have to subtract five from their present ages so it'll be 3x-5 and x-5. Then the sentence says that Vincent's father is 4 times as old as Vincent and now we equate:

3x - 5 = 4 ( x - 5)

3x - 5 = 4x - 20

20 - 5 = 4x - 3x

15 = x

Or simply x = 15

Then for Vincent's father it'll be

3x = 3 (15)

3x = 45
