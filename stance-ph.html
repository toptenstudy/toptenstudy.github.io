10. what is the preparatory position in Arnis? a. courtesy bow b.fighting stance c.forward stance d.backward stance​


Answer:

A. Courtesy bow

The Attention Stance or Courtesy Bow is commonly used in preparation for courtesy or “bowing” at commencement of sparring. Starting with the ready stance, move one foot forward until the knee and the toe are in line to each other. Both toes are pointing in front, the waist and the body is facing forward.
