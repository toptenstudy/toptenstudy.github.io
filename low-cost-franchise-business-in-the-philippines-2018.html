27. The taxpayer biegan his business in 2018. He did not register as a vat taxpayer because he did not anticipate that his gross sales for the year will exceed the threshold amount for vat. He had sales of 1,000.000 and cost 400,000 in 2018. How much was his business for the year?​


Answer:

To determine the business income for the year 2018, you can use the following formula:

Business Income = Gross Sales - Cost

where "Gross Sales" is the total revenue from the sale of goods or services, and "Cost" is the total expenses incurred in generating those sales.

In this case, the taxpayer's gross sales were 1,000,000 and the cost was 400,000. So, the business income for the year is:

Business Income = 1,000,000 - 400,000 = 600,000

So, the taxpayer's business income for the year 2018 is 600,000.

It's worth noting that even though the taxpayer didn't anticipate that their gross sales would exceed the threshold amount for VAT, if they did exceed the threshold they would have been required to register and pay VAT on their sales.
