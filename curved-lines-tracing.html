Explanation:

A track or tracing wheel (example in the picture), also known as a pattern wheel, pounce wheel, and dart wheel, is an instrument with many teeth on a wheel attached to a handle. Teeth can be serrated or smooth. It is used to transfer marks from sewing patterns onto fabric with or without the use of tracing paper, and can be used to make slotted perforations. Such marks may include pleats, darts, buttonholes, notches or placement lines for appliques or pockets.
