20. Dr. Lao, a physicist, and Dr. Bela-ong, a biologist, partnered for a research project sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology. Dr. Lao is four years older than Dr. Bela-ong. In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. How old is Dr. Bela-ong?


AGE-RELATED PROBLEMS

Problem:

Dr. Lao, a physicist, and Dr. Bela-ong, a biologist, partnered for a research project sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology. Dr. Lao is four years older than Dr. Bela-ong. In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. How old is Dr. Bela-ong?

Answer: Dr. Bela-ong is 36 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us gather the clues that we can use to answer this age related word problem:

Dr. Lao is 4 years older than Dr. Bela-ong. So let us say the the variable x - 4 represents Dr. Bela-ong's age while x represents Dr. Lao's age. These are their present ages.In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. This one is quite tricky but we need to analyze this one. After 8 years, Dr. Lao will be 8 times older than Dr. Bela-ong's age 30 years ago so: x  + 8  is Dr. Lao's age after 8 years and (x - 4) - 30 is Dr. Bela-ong's age 30 years ago. In an equation, we can express this as:x + 8 = 8[ x - 4 - 30 ]    => Combine like terms.x + 8 = 8[ x - 34]          => Distribute 8 to all terms inside the brackets.x + 8 = 8x - 272           => Transpose each terms to combine like terms.8x - x = 272 + 8           => Simplify.7x = 280                     => Divide both sides by 7 to isolate x.x = 40

So we already have the value of x which is Dr. Lao's age. By direct substitution, we can obtain Dr. Bela-ong's age:

Dr. Bela-ong's age = x - 4 = 40 - 4 = 36

Therefore, Dr. Bela-ong's age is 36 years old.

21. Janice is 14 years younger than his oldest cousin. the sum of their age is 56. how old is each of them now?​


[tex]\Large\color{aqua}\underline\mathbb{AGE \: PROBLEMS}[/tex]

Janice is 14 years younger than his oldest cousin. the sum of their age is 56. how old is each of them now?

[tex]\\[/tex]

Representation:

Let x be the age of Janice.Let y be the age of her oldest cousin.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Equation:

Janice (x) is (=) 14 years younger than his oldest cousin (y - 14)

x = y - 14

The sum of their age is 56.

x + y = 56

[tex]\\[/tex]

Equation:

[tex]\begin{cases}\sf{x = y - 14} \\ \sf{x + y = 56} \end{cases}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\color{indigo}\underline\mathbb{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

To solve using substitution, replace the variable x in the equation x + y = 56 to y - 14, since it is the value of x.

[tex]\\[/tex]

x = y - 14y - 14 + y = 562y - 14 = 562y = 56 + 142y = 70y = 70/2y = 35

[tex]\\[/tex]

The value of y is 35. Now substitute the first equation, which is x = y - 14. Replace the variable y in the equation x = y - 14 to 35, since it is the value of y, to solve for the value of x.

[tex]\\[/tex]

x = y - 14x = 35 - 14x = 21

The value of x is 21.

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\color{blue}\underline\mathbb{ANSWER:}[/tex]

Therefore, the age of Janice is 21 while her older cousin is 35 years old.
