1. diary of a senior high school​


Answer:

Monday, September 14th

Today was the first day of my senior year of high school! I was pretty nervous, but also excited to see what this year has in store for me. I started the day off by attending my first period math class, which went by pretty quickly. After that, I had my English class, which I'm already looking forward to because the teacher seems really friendly. After that, I had lunch with some of my friends, which was nice. We talked about how nervous we all were for the first day and what classes we were taking. After that, I had history and science classes, which were both really interesting. I'm really looking forward to learning more in these classes. Finally, I had my last period, which was a study hall. I'm glad I got to relax a bit before I headed home. Overall, it was a good first day of school and I'm looking forward to what this year has in store!

21. scope and delimitations of loitering in senior high school students​


Answer:

The scope of loitering in senior high school students refers to the extent or range of the issue being studied. In this case, it pertains to the act of lingering or staying in a particular area without any apparent purpose or reason among senior high school students. The scope may include the frequency of loitering, the locations where it commonly occurs, and the reasons why students engage in this behavior.

On the other hand, the delimitations of loitering in senior high school students refer to the boundaries or limitations of the study. These may include the specific senior high school level or age group being studied, the geographical location of the school, and the time frame of the study. The delimitations may also include the exclusion of other related behaviors such as truancy or skipping classes.

It is important to establish the scope and delimitations of the study to ensure that the research is focused and manageable. By doing so, the researcher can gather relevant and accurate data that can be used to address the issue of loitering among senior high school students effectively.

30. novelty of the research about the advantage and disadvantage of social media to the student in senior high school to the performance of the academic performance senior high school ​


The novelty of the research topic is that it focuses specifically on the impact of social media on the academic performance of senior high school students. While there have been many studies conducted on the general effects of social media on students, this research aims to delve deeper into how it specifically affects senior high school students.

The research problem of this topic could be:

1. What are the advantages and disadvantages of using social media among senior high school students in relation to their academic performance?

2. How does social media use affect the study habits and time management skills of senior high school students?

3. What are the factors that contribute to the positive and negative effects of social media on the academic performance of senior high school students?

The chosen research problem is important because it addresses a relevant issue among senior high school students, who are at a critical stage in their education. The results of the research could provide insights to educators and parents on how to properly regulate the use of social media to maximize its advantages and minimize its disadvantages in relation to academic performance.
