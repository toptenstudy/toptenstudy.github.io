8. Make a research about the important ions in our body and atmosphere. Draw a template that shows the type of ions and its importance​


The two types of ions are cation and anion.

Cation: Ion with a positive charge.

Anion : Ion with a negative charge.

_

There are specialized types of ions. Anions have more electrons than protons and so have a net negative charge. Cations have more protons than electrons and so have a net positive charge. Zwitterions are neutral and have both positive and negative charges at different locations throughout the molecule.

_

The reason why it is important that cations and anions should balance each other out in the human body is that they are found both inside the cells and outside the cells. For example, it is important that a balances must be maintained between the fluid surrounding a body cell and the fluid inside a body cell.
