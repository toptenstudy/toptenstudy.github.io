The order of cumulative adjectives is as follows: quantity, opinion, size, age, color, shape, origin, material and purpose.

Quantity

These adjectives answer the question “How much?” or “How many?” They can refer to specific numbers, like two or 31, or to more general amounts, like “whole” “half” “a lot” or “several.”

Opinion

Next in word order comes opinion adjectives, which express how we feel about something.

Size

This includes any number of descriptive size words, such as “large” “big” and “little.”

Age

This can refer to specific age adjectives, such as 16-year-old, or such words as “young” “old” “middle-aged” and so on.

Shape

This includes words like “long” “short” and “round” or words for specific shapes, like square.

Color

We normally use color adjectives to describe objects and animals.

Origin

Origin, ethnicity and religion.

Material

Material adjectives are usually nouns that act as adjectives when used to describe other nouns -- like metal, paper and silk.

Purpose

For purpose adjectives, we usually also use a noun as an adjective. “Folding” is a gerund – a kind of noun ending in -ing.

16. Talambuhay ni cecilio pedro


Si Cecilio Kwok Pedro ay isang negosyante sa Pilipinas na nakabalik sa itaas matapos ang kanyang pagkabigo noong 1985. Siya ang nagtatag ng Hapee toothpaste sa Pilipinas. Ang kanyang kumpanyang Lamoiyan Corporation ang unang local na kumpanya ng toothpaste sa Pilipinas. Dahil sa mas kilala ang ibang mga brand ng toothpase naniwala sya na ang pananampalataya ay isang paraan upang maging matagumpay sa kompetensya sa merkado. Naniniwala din sya na sa mundo ng negosyo hindi dapat nakadepende sa iisa o dadalawang parokyano lamang.

Buhay Bilang Supplier

Isa siyang mangangalakal na Filipino-Chinese na may ari ng kumpanyang kilala sa pagsupply ng aluminum na mga lalagyan ng toothpaste na ginagamit ng dalawang kilalang kumpanya ang Colgate-Palmolive at Philippine Refining Company (Close Up).  Pero kalaunan ay plastic na ang ginamit ng dalawang ito na nagtulak sa kanya upang isara nya ang kanyang kumpanya na Aluminum Container Inc, makalipas ang walong taon lamang.

Panibagong Buhay

Kilalang matyaga at madiskarte si Cecilio mula pa lamang nung kanyang kabataan. Sya ay nagsimulang magnegosyo noong sya at sampung taon pa lamang at naitatag nya ang unang kumpanya nya noong 1977. Dahil nga sa paglipat sa plastic na lalagyan ng mga malalaking kumpanya ng toothpaste, sinara nya ang Aluminum Container Inc noong 1985. Inisip nya na gamitin para sa epoxy ang kanyang mga alumium tubes pero kalaunan ay nagdesisyon syang subukan ang paggawa ng toothpaste. Dito na itinatag  ang Lamoiyan na syang naging unang local homegrown na tootpaste company dito sa Pilipinas noong 1987. Umutang pa sya sa mga Japanese ng nasa $20,000 para lang maitatag ang kanyang kumpanya.  

Ang Pagsibol ng Hapee

Sa una ay walang may kilala sa Hapee pero sa pamamagitan ng magandang marketing unti unti din itong nakilala. Mas nagbigay pansin sya sa mga bata at kinuha din niya bilang endorser si Lea Salonga, na noong mga panahong iyon ay sikat at may impluwensya sa mga tao. Nagkaroon din ng iba ibang mga flavor ang kangyang tootpaste upang mas makaakit ito ng target market nya na mga bata.

23. The food web shows _________________. * A. series of organisms in an environment B. series of food energy transfer C. series of consumers D. series of producers


answer:

B. series of food energy transfer

A food web is a detailed description of the species within a community and their relationships with each other; it shows how energy is transferred up food chains that are interlinked with other food chains.

explanation:

Food webs

Food chains give us a clear-cut picture of who eats whom. However, some problems come up when we try and use them to describe whole ecological communities.

For instance, an organism can sometimes eat multiple types of prey or be eaten by multiple predators, including ones at different trophic levels. This is what happens when you eat a hamburger patty! The cow is a primary consumer, and the lettuce leaf on the patty is a primary producer.

To represent these relationships more accurately, we can use a food web, a graph that shows all the trophic—eating-related—interactions between various species in an ecosystem. 27. Differentiate arithmetic series and geometric series​


Answer:

In the case of arithmetic series the sum is almost always of a finite number of terms. While in a geometric series, if the quotient is between −1 and 1, one can take the sum of all the infinitely many terms of the sequence

An arithmetic sequence is a sequence with the difference between two consecutive terms constant. The difference is called the common difference. A geometric sequence is a sequence with the ratio between two consecutive terms constant.
