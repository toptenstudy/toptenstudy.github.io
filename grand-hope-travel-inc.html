1. if a family is driving to grand canyon at 60 km/hour. how many meters do they travel in 1 minute​


They travel 1000 m

Further explanation  

Linear motion consists of 2: constant velocity motion with constant velocity and uniformly accelerated motion with constant acceleration  

At constant velocity motion:  

the speed of vo = v = constant  

acceleration = a = 0  

Δx = vt or x = xo + vt  

An equation of constant velocity motion  

d = v x t

d = distance, m

v = velocity, m/s

t = time, s

Given

velocity = 60 km/hr = 16.7 m/s

time = 1 minute = 60 s

Required

distance

Solution

Input the value :

d = 16.7 m/s x 60 s

d = 1002 m

or

1 minute = 1/60 hr, then :

d = 60 km/hr x 1/60 hr

d = 1 km = 1000 m

30. pangasinan is 191 kilometers from manila how many meters are you going to travel if you want to visit your grand mother in pangasinan


Answer:

The answer is 191,000 meters.

Explanation:

Meters of Travel From Manila to Pangasinan

In the given, it was stated that Pangasinan is 191 kilometers (km) away from Manila. It is important to note that for every kilometer, there are 1,000 meters (1 km is equal to 1,000 m).  If somebody is traveling from Manila to Pangasinan to visit his or her grandmother, it will take him or her approximately 191,000 meters (m) to get to the province. To be able to understand more, a solution and how the answer is generated will be explained below

How to Convert Kilometers to Meters?Determine the given:Distance from Manila to Pangasinan = 191 kmIn the metric system, it is established that for every 1 km, there are 1,000 m.Perform the solution:To get the answer, write the formula: 191 km x 1,000 m / 1 kmTo be able to get the right unit, write the unit to be cancelled under the possible unit. This is to cancel the same units which are inversely proportional to each other in the equation.Calculate the answers based on the formula: 191 km times 1,000 m divided by 1 km. Cancel the km units to be left with m. The answer is 191,000.To explain further, if 1 km is equal to 1,000 m, then 191 km must be equal to 191 multiples of 1,000 m.
