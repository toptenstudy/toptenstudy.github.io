29. what are the major differences in microsoft excel 2010 and microsoft excel 2016​


Explanation:

If it worked in Excel 2010, it will still work in Excel 2016. Excel 2016 uses the same formulas, and for the most part the same ribbon menu items. The training material you have is definitely still useful.

Excel 2016 does have some new features. Some of them require that you have a subscription copy (Office 365) rather than a retail box of Office.

New chart engine and new chart types. Excel can now produce waterfall, funnel, histogram, box & whisker, treemap, sunburst and stock charts. Some of the new chart types may require an Office 365 subscription.
