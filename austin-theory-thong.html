3. According to Austin’s Speech theory, illocutionary acts refer to one of the following ?​


Answer:

having a certain force

Explanation:

Austin says that in uttering a sentence the speaker per- forms an illocutionary act of having a certain force, which is different from the locutionary act of uttering the sentence, which is to have a meaning, and also from the perlocutionary act performed by uttering the sentence, which is to achieve certain effects.


4. what type of simple machine is kitchen thong


Answer:

Type 3 Lever

Explanation:

A pair of tongs in your kitchen is one of the most common examples of Type 3 Lever kind of simple machine. In such simple machines, the effort is displaced between the load and the fulcrum. Also, the applied effort is more than the size of the load

Answer:

Tongs are Class-3 Levers

Explanation:

They reduce the applied force and hence provide better control and manipulation. In these, the effort and the load are on the same side of the fulcrum, however, the effort is closer to the fulcrum so it demands more force to apply a lesser force.
