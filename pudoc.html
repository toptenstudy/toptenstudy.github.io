2. Music can lift you up

If you play an instrument, you have so many opportunities for expressing your mood. Even just playing around on the guitar can be therapeutic. You might choose to listen to rock, rap or a classical symphony. Studies show that listening to upbeat music really does affect your mood positively.

3. Writing as therapy

When I was a teen, I wanted to express some thoughts through poetry so I sent some poems to a publisher. Unfortunately, they were turned down. Writing enabled me to express emotional trauma and other frustrations. It was a safety valve. Even if you never write a story or poem, writing down your thoughts and feelings is great therapy.

It can stimulate a curiosity about night life in 19th century Paris, the social mores in vogue at the time, fashion, the life of Toulouse-Lautrec and his difficulties caused by his unusually short stature. The more we find out, the more we want to read and discover what life in Paris was like at the time.

24. analysis of soil samples from humid region reveals the presence of a high concentration of iron and clay. based on this soil, the soil would be classified as:a. pedocalb. pedalferc. laterited. bauxite​


LATERITE

Laterite is defined as a red rock or red soil deposit. Laterites arise as a result of the disintegration of several types of rocks under circumstances that produce aluminum and iron hydroxides.

COMPONENTS

Laterite soil is made up of clay and iron particles and is distinguished by its hardening capabilities, chemical composition, and structural evolution. It is porous and soft under high humidity, but hardens when exposed to high ambient temperatures on a constant basis.

Laterite soil is crimson to yellow in hue, has a reduced nitrogenphosphorus(po)tassiumlimemagnesia contentcontains 90-100% ironaluminumtitaniummanganese oxides.REGIONS WHERE LATERITE CAN BE FOUNDLaterite is a soil layer rich in the iron oxide that forms when a range of rocks weather under strong oxidizing and draining circumstances. It grows in humid climates such as the tropics and subtropics.Laterite soil is found mostly in the Western and Eastern Ghats. Particularly in Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Odisha, Madhya Pradesh, and Assam. This soil is high in iron oxide and (po)tassium but low in nitrogen, phosphorus, and calcium.Laterite soil is a rock-type soil that is rich in iron and aluminum and is thought to have developed in hot and humid tropical locations.
