Submissiveness refers to the quality or state of being submissive, which means to be willing to obey or yield to the authority, will or desires of another person or group. Submissiveness can be a trait or behavior that is learned and developed over time, and may be influenced by factors such as cultural and societal norms, upbringing, personality, and personal experiences.
