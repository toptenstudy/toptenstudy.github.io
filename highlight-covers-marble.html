9. Students use a balance to determine the mass of a marble is 7.5 grams. A graduated bottle contains 10.0 milliliters of water. When the marble is carefully lowered into the graduated bottle and is completely covered with water the volume of the water containing the marble has increased to 12.5 milliliters. Find the density of the marble. A) 0.3 g mL B) 3.0 g mL C) 7.5 g mL D) 12.5 g mL


Density (ρ) = mass (m) ÷ volume (v)

             = 7.5 grams ÷ (10 - 12.5) mL

             = 7.5 grams ÷ 2.5 ml

             = 3 g/mL
