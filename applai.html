5. What is importance of applaying the element and principles of arts in making artifacts?​


What is importance of applying the element and principles of arts in making artifacts?

First and foremost, one cannot produce art without incorporating at least a few of them. Second, understanding the aspects of art allows us to explain what an artist has created, analyze what is going on in a specific piece, and convey our views and conclusions in a common language.

Balance, contrast, emphasis, movement, pattern, rhythm, and unity/variety are the concepts of art and design. These principles can aid in determining whether or not a painting is successful, as well as whether or not it is complete. The artist chooses which artistic principles to use to a picture.

ELEMENTS OF ART

Elements of art are stylistic features added in a work of art to aid the artist in communicating. Line, shape, texture, form, space, color, and value are the seven most prevalent elements, with the additions of mark creation and materiality.
