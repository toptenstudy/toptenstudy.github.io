1. Mhel loves cheese burgers. He enters a 24 hour cheese burger eating contest. During the contest Mhel eats a cheese burger every 15 minutes. How many cheese burgers will he eat during the entire contest? 32 cheese burgers 36 cheese burgers 54 cheese burgers 96 cheese burgers


Convert 24 hour into minutes
24 hours(60mins/1hour) = 1,440 minutes

Mhel eaten cheese burger = 1,440/15
Mhel eaten cheese burger = 96 cheeseburgers

Mhel will eat 96 cheese burgers during the entire contest.

The Guidelines: Planning of Ala Carte and Buffet Foods tips

When food is served a la carte in a restaurant, it signifies that it is ordered individually. It is not a course of dishes or part of a larger meal. When you purchase a steak a la carte, you get simply a steak. It could come with garnishes or sauces. Some diners may like to order a side dish with their a la carte steak. But they'd also order the side dish a la carte.

That's because the distinguishing feature of an a la carte menu item is that it's served differently—separately—than the meal on the restaurant's full menu. This contrasts with set menus such as the table d'hote menu and the à la carte menu.
