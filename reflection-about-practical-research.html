PRACTICAL RESEARCH REFLECTION

This research provides an opportunity for a researcher to reflect on his work, think about solutions, look for alternative solutions, determine solutions, carry out solutions, assess for himself how changes, progress or improvements have been made.

Practical research refers to building useful knowledge to put it into practice. adhere to the clear basic principles of scientific inquiry research. questions, valid behavioral measures, systematic collection and analysis. data, and appropriate conclusions.

Understanding the benefits of practical research is related to both the researcher and the reader. The benefits of practical research are expected to contribute to the reader's understanding of a science.

The results of the research according to the benefits of practical research are expected to be useful in the development of science as well as the research can be useful as a reference for further research.

The benefit of practical research is that it can be used to solve practical problems or as an alternative solution to a problem.
