21. 2. Monday through Thursday, Teresa exercises for 30 minutes after coming home from work. On the weekends, she goes swimming for 1 hour on Saturday. Teresa does not exercise on Friday and Sunday. At the end of 2 weeks, how much time will she have spent exercising?​


Answer:

Teresa exercises for 30 minutes for 4 days, which is a total of 30 x 4 = 120 minutes or 2 hours per week.

She also goes swimming for 1 hour on Saturday, which means she exercises for a total of 2 + 1 = 3 hours per week.

Therefore, in 2 weeks, she will have exercised for a total of 3 x 2 = 6 hours.
