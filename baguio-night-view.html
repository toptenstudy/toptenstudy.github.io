A horse and rider are in Baguio City which is experiencing heavy fog, so they can see for only 10 yards in any direction a. Find the area of the rider’s field of view. b. Find the area of the horse’s field of view


AREA AND ANGLE

The area of the rider’s field of view is 157 square yards and the area of the horse’s field of view is 187.53 square yards.

FURTHER EXPLANATION

1. Different animals have different fields of view. Humans can generally see a 180° arc in front of them. Horses can see a 215° arc. A horse and rider in Baguio City are experiencing heavy fog, so they can see only 10 yards in any direction.

a. Find the area of the rider’s field of view.

He can see an area that is a semicircle at an angle of 180 degrees.

Given

Radius = r = 10 yardsAngle = c = 180°

Question

Area = A = ...

Solution

A = (c/360) · (π · r²)A = 180/360 · (3.14) · (10)²A = 1/2 · (3.14) · 100 A = 1/2 · 314A = 157

Therefore, the area of the rider’s field of view is 157 square yards.

b. Find the area of the horse’s field of view

Horses can see at an angle of 215 degrees.

Given

Radius = r = 10 yardsAngle = c = 215°

Question

Area = A = ...

Solution

A = (c/360) · (π · r²)A = (215/360) · (3.14) · (10)²A = (43/72) · (3.14) · 100 A = (43/72) · 314A = 187.53

Therefore, the area of the horse’s field of view is 187.53 square yards.
