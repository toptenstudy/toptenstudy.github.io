5. Which of the following signs or symptoms is not characteristic of bulimia? a. depressed mood b. noticeable weight loss or gain c. lack of concern about weight d. habit of visiting the bathroom immediately after meals


On the above, lack of concern about weight is NOT a sign or symptom of bulimia.

Bulimia nervosa or bulimia is an eating disorder or condition characterized by extreme eating then followed by purging.

Purging refers to attempt to get rid of the food that is eaten by vomiting or using laxatives.

Here are other signs and symptoms:
1. Lack of energy
2. Depression and Sleep disorders
3. Extreme consciousness of weight
4. Fix number of calories intake
5. Regular visiting of bathrooms after meals

12. in Food service business wjat are the considerations in planning meals or using a meal plan??


What is a meal planning?

Meal planning is the act of planning your meals for the week. It could be a plan for yourself or a  plan for the whole family. You can also plan your meals so you won't be spending money on eating at restaurants during the week.

Take note! It doesn't really matter what you plan. The goal of meal planning s not to start from zero for every single meal.

So, what are the considerations in planning meals in a food service business?

1. Mission and objective of the food business.

2. Food trends, habits, and preference  of customers.

3. Variety of food  choices.

4. The style of service .

5. Number of the customers that are going to be served .

6. The skills of the staff working .

7. The budget of the business.

Take note again! The menu plan of the restaurant must satisfy the customers with the food the restaurant served to them. The food should meet the standards of the customers every time.
