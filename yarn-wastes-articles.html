26. A piece of blue yarn is 5 2/7 yards long. A piece of pink yarn is 4 times as long as the blue yarn. How much longer is the pink yarn thab blue yarn.​


Answer:

148/7 or 21 1/7 or 21.14

Step-by-step explanation:

5 2/7 x 4; to multiply this, change the mixed fraction in to an improper fraction;

to change  5 2/7 into an improper fraction;

multiply the denominator to the whole number and add the numerator, that is:

37/7, now multiply it by 4:

37   x   4   (apply the cross multiplication)

7          1

= 148/7, is the answer, to change it to mixed fraction;

148 / 7= 21 1/7  (the quotient will be the whole number and the remainder                    will be the numerator, the divisor will become the denominator)

to make it into a decimal form: 148/7= 21.14 (estimated)
