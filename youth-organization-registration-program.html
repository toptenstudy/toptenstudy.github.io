5. What is the level of acceptance of Filipino youth on the program concept of ROTC?​


Students that participate in ROTC programs have several advantages and opportunities. In addition to receiving beneficial instruction, ROTC members also receive financial aid for their schooling. Strong character attributes like self-control, self-assurance, and leadership are encouraged in them.

One of the three parts of the National Service Training Program, which provides Filipino college students with civic education and defense preparation, is the Reserve Officers' Training Corps (ROTC). In order to educate students for national defense readiness, ROTC aspires to provide military education and training. Its specific goals include preparing college students to serve in the AFP in case of an emergency and preparing them to join the reserve forces and maybe become commissioned officers in the AFP.

All branches of the Philippine Armed Forces employ graduates of the ROTC advance program. The ROTC offers qualified student-cadets scholarship benefits through a merit-based incentive program in exchange for an obligation to complete military service in the reserve force, or active duty in the AFP if given the chance, after graduation. In 2008, ROTC graduates of the officer candidate schools of the various services made up roughly 75% of the AFP officer corps.

In addition to attending college like other students, ROTC student-cadets get officer and foundational military training from the service branch that oversees the school's ROTC organization. The students participate in extended training activities during the summer, such as the ROTC Summer Camp Training (RSCT) and the Advance ROTC Academic Phase Training, as well as regular ROTC instruction during the academic year (one academic year for Basic ROTC student-cadets and three academic years for Advance ROTC cadet-officers) (ARAPT).

College and university ROTC programs are managed by the Department of Military Science and Tactics (DMST), which is overseen jointly by the Department of National Defense and the institution's administration. These ROTC units are in turn managed by active duty officers of the AFP and the reservist organization representatives of the major services, the Philippine Army Reserve Command of the Philippine Army, the Philippine Navy Reserve Command of the Philippine Navy and the Philippine Air Force Reserve Command of the Philippine Air Force.

30. How important it is to know the general requirements as well as the procedures for registration of a business or organization?​


Answer:

Knowing the general requirements and procedures for registering a business or organization is incredibly important as it ensures that everything has been completed correctly and that your entity is legally registered. Proper registration enables you to obtain licenses, open bank accounts, hire employees, and become eligible for grants, investments, and other business opportunities that may be off limits to businesses that have not registered. Additionally, registering your business or organization is the first step to obtaining official business identification, which can be beneficial in many ways, from getting out of traffic tickets to obtaining discounted rates from vendors.
