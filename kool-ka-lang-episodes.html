10. kool-ad mixture o solution​


Answer:

ANSWER

A solution is a mixture in which one substance, called the solute is uniformly distributed in another substance called the solvent.  The most common solvent in our everyday life is water.   Some examples of solutions are Kool-Aid, cranberry juice, vodka and saline solution.  Solutions are typically clear (although they may be colored) and homogeneous (all the same).  My cherry Kool Aid is a clear red solution.  In this solution the solvent is water and the solutes are sugar, artificial flavor and artificial color.  Another interesting property of solutions is that different concentrations of solute can be made.  As all of you are aware, you can make very sweet Kool Aid and less sweet Kool Aid.

13. An 800g solution of Kool-Aid contains 780g of water. what is the masspercent of solute in this solution?


The mass percent of solute in the given problem is 2.5%.

A solute is any substance (example: sugar) dissolved in another substance called solvent (example: water). The combination of the solute and solvent is called a solution.

Here are the steps to calculate the mass percent of solute:

Determine the mass of the solute. Divide the mass of the solute by the mass of the solution. Multiply the resulting quotient by 100 to change it to percent.

Using the given problem, here is the solution:

Mass of solute = 800g of solution - 780g water of water = 20g of solute Mass of solute / mass of solution = 20g of solute / 800g of solution = 0.025 Quotient x 100 = 0.025 x 100 = 2.5%

20. 5. Aristotle stores his Kool-Aid in a cylindrical container with a diameter of 5.5 feet and a height of 8.25 feet. If the filled container springs a leak and the Kool-Aid is escaping at a rate of 8 cubic feet per hour, how long will it take before the container is empty?​


Answer:

-It will take 24.49 hours before the container is empty.

Step-by-step explanation:

Diameter = 5.5 feetRadius (r) = 5.5/2 = 2.75 ftHeight (h) = 8.25 feet

Volume of cylinder = πr²h

= 3.14×2.75×2.75×8.25

= 195.90656 ft³

Leak 8 cubic feet per hour,so time taken to empty

= 195.90656 / 8

= 24.48832

= 24.49 hours.
