Answer:

P109,824 - tuition fee of Raven last school year.

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

*Find the tuition fee increase:

P114,400 * 4% (0.04) = P4,576

*Subtract the current school year tuition fee and the tuition fee increase

P114,400 - P4,576 = P109,824

Final Answer: P109,824 was the tuition fee of Raven last school year.

17. Tuition fee is increased by 25% since five years. If the tuition fee then was ₱5 000, how much is the tuition fee now?


Answer: ₱6,250


Step-by-step explanation:

To get the answer,

First, you multiply it by 0.25.

It becomes:

5,000 × 0.25 = 1,250.

But, since the tuition is increased by 25%, we add 5000 to 1250.

5000 + 1250 = 6250.

Therefore, tuition today costs ₱6,250.

26. school tuition fees increase about 8% every year on the average if your tuition fee cost php45,000.00 per year how much would it cost next year​


Answer:

Next year's tuition fee would cost PHP 48,600.00.

Step-by-step explanation:

If school tuition fees increase by 8% every year, and your current tuition fee is PHP 45,000.00 per year, then the cost of tuition next year can be calculated as follows:

Next year's tuition fee = Current tuition fee + (Current tuition fee x Rate of increase)

Next year's tuition fee = 45,000 + (45,000 x 0.08)

Next year's tuition fee = 45,000 + 3,600

Next year's tuition fee = 48,600
