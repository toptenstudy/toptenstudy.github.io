Let x = number of students

½x = Math

⅓x = English

⅐x = Science

1 - (½ + ⅓ + ⅐) = Music

¹⁄₄₂x = Music

20 = Music

¹⁄₄₂(x) = 20

x = 42(20)

x = 840

b.) ⅓(840) = 280 students studying English

c.) ⅐(840) = 120 students studying Science

d.) 840 students

30. using venn diagramamong 120 freshmen at college 40 take mathematics and 50 take english and 15 both mathematics and english. Find the number of freshmen whoA. do not take mathematicsB. take matematics but not englishC.take exactly one of two subjects


Answer:

A. 80 freshmen do not take Mathematics.

B. 25 freshmen take Mathematics but not English.

C. 60 freshmen take exactly one of two subjects.

Step-by-step explanation:

Venn Diagram Word Problem

Venn diagram word problem is a method consisting primarily of entering the elements of a set into a circle or circles. It includes two or three classifications and a bunch of numbers. The given information are use to populate the diagram and figure out the remaining information.

Let us analyze the given problem above.

Given:

number of freshmen - 120

freshmen take Mathematics - 40

freshmen take English - 50

freshmen take both Mathematics and English - 15

There are two classifications in the problem above: freshmen who take Mathematics and freshmen who take English.

In venn diagram, two overlapping circles are labelled with the total in each.

15 freshmen are taking both classes so we need to put 15 in the overlap.

Since 40 freshmen take Mathematics, we need to account 15 from them, leaving 25 freshmen taking Mathematics but not English.

Then for English, we need to account 15 from 50, leaving 35 freshmen taking English but not Mathematics.

Let us now find the answers to the problem above.

A. do not take Mathematics

To know the number of freshmen who do not take Mathematics, we are just going to take away the number of freshmen who take Mathematics from the total number of freshmen.

120 - 40 = 80

80 freshmen do not take Mathematics.

B. take Mathematics but not English

Take away 15 from 40.

40 - 15 = 25

25 freshmen take Mathematics but not English.

C. take exactly one of two subjects

Add the number of freshmen who take Mathematics only and English only.

25 + 35 = 60

60 freshmen take exactly one of two subjects.
