B. Differentiate flash news from breaking news. Write FN for flash news and BN for breaking news.

FLASH NEWS 1. It is snippet of an important current event.

BREAKING NEWS 2. It is an important event in which full details must be reported.

BREAKING NEWS 3. This refers to events that are currently developing.

FLASH NEWS 4. This news is sudden, therefore, it's shorter.

HERE ARE THINGS YOU CAN EASILY REMEMBER!!

NEWS FLASH

News flash are freshly important news that interrupts sometimes the advertisements or movies you're currently watching just to inform all what's happening.

BREAKING NEWS

Breaking news shows after the movies, shows current events. It provides all the information asked because it's shown before or after all, it doesn't interrupt movies or series.

DIFFERENCES

Breaking news provides all informations about that event, it doesn't interrupt while the flash news shows only what is very important details that is needed to be known and interrupts what you're watching.
