11. A bolt has 40 meters of cloth. If it has enough fabric to upholster 5 taxi cabs, howmany meters are necessary for each cab? How many bolts must you buy for 18cabs?​


Answer:

1st Question: 8 meters

2nd Question: 4

Solution:1st Question:

Since 40 meters of cloth is enough for 5 taxi cabs, hence each taxi cab is upholstered by 40/5 meters of cloth

Therefore each cab is upholstered by 8 meters of fabric

2nd Question:

Since a bolt can upholster 5 taxi cabs, hence you need to buy 18/5 bolts

18/5 = 3.6

You cannot buy 3 bolts because 3 bolts are inadequate for 18 cabs.

Therefore you need to buy 4 so it will be sufficient for 18 cabs.
