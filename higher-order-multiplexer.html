The acronym VUCA stands for Volatile," "Uncertain," "Complexity," and "Ambiguity" or volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity which is used to describe situations that are difficult to understand, making it difficult to predict the outcome of any action taken.

The VUCA idea was immediately picked up by civil society who began to realize that VUCA elements also applied to volatile markets and the difficult conditions businesses often face.

The VUCA concept is starting to be applied to boardroom discussions that influence decision making and is used as a method of understanding where there is a lack of predictability thereby helping to improve business performance.
