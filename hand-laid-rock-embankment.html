3. are semendary rock usually laid down in horizontal layers? explain.​

Sedimentary Rock Rules

Sedimentary rocks are formed with the oldest layers on the bottom and the youngest on top. Sediments are deposited horizontally, so sedimentary rock layers are originally horizontal, as are some volcanic rocks, such as ash falls. Sedimentary rock layers that are not horizontal are deformed.
