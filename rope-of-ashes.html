29. 1. What is Ash Wednesday? 2. What is the meaning of the imposition of ashes on the forehead during Ash Wednesday?


[tex]\huge\bold{Answer}[/tex]

1. Ash Wednesday is a Christian holy day of prayer and fasting. It is preceded by Shrove Tuesday and falls on the first day of Lent. Ash Wednesday is traditionally observed by Western Christians.

Significance: It is the first day of Lent.

2. [tex]\large\boxed{Repentance}[/tex]

Ash Wednesday (officially known as the Day of Ashes) is a day of repentance, when Christians confess their sins and profess their devotion to God. During a Mass, a priest places the ashes on a worshiper's forehead in the shape of a cross.

The mother dropped tiny piles of twigs along the narrow path.

His mother advised him to look carefully and follow the path where she dropped the piles of twigs.

The farmer hid his mother under the kitchen floor of his small hut.
