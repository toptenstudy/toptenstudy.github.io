17. The family code of the philippines that we are currently abiding from was drafted from which of the following constitution?


The Family Code of the Philippines  

Based on Executive No. 209, July 6, 1987, with details:

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8533

AN ACT AMENDING TITLE I, CHAPTER 3, ARTICLE 39 OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 209, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE FAMILY CODE OF THE PHILIPPINES, NULLIFIES THE PRESCRIPTIVE PERIOD FOR ACTION OR DEFENSES GROUNDED ON PSYCHOLOGICAL INCAPACITY

Detailed Specifics in General

THE FAMILY

The State recognizes the Filipino family as the nation's bedrock in:

Section 1. Consequently, it will bolster its cohesion and actively encourage its overall development.

Section 2. Marriage, as an inviolable social institution, is the bedrock of the family and shall be safeguarded by the State.

Section 3: The State must:

(1) The right of spouses to establish a family in accordance with their religious beliefs and the responsibilities of responsible parenthood.

(2) The right of children to help, including sufficient care and nutrition, as well as specific protection against all sorts of neglect, abuse, cruelty, exploitation, and other conditions detrimental to their development.

(3) The right of the family to a salary and income sufficient for subsistence; and

(4) The right of families and family organizations to participate in the formulation and implementation of policies and programs affecting them.

(5)The family has the responsibility to care for its older members, but the State may also do so through social security programs.
