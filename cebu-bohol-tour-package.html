Answer: A total of Php 151,425

4. what is the difference between a package tour ang independent tour​


Answer:

A package tourist goes on a package tour, where they pay for all flights, transfers, accommodation, etc., together and in advance, usually through a travel agency. An independent traveler books different aspects of their trip separately (for example, they might book their flight on the internet, then get a taxi from the airport to their hotel, pay for their hotel when they arrive at their destination, etc.) and does not usually rely on an agency.

22. Compare Bohol and Cebu music. What are their differences?


Answer:

Bohol and Cebu are both provinces in the Philippines with rich cultural histories, including unique music traditions.

In terms of traditional music, both Bohol and Cebu have similar instruments, such as the kulintang, agung, and gong. However, there are some differences in the way they are used in the music.

One key difference is in the dance music of the two regions. In Bohol, the Kuradang is a popular dance that features music played on instruments like the banduria, guitar, and tamborine. The rhythm of Kuradang is faster compared to Cebu's Tinikling dance. Tinikling involves bamboo poles that are struck together in a rhythmic pattern while dancers weave in and out of the poles.

Another difference is in the style of singing. Boholano music often features a vocal style called balitaw, which is a slow and emotional form of singing. Cebuano music, on the other hand, has a more upbeat and lively style of singing, especially in their Visayan pop music.

Overall, while there are some similarities between Bohol and Cebu music, the two regions have distinct musical styles and traditions that set them apart from each other.
