3. Question:What is the APPROXIMATE time between November 21, 2019 to March 5, 2019?


Answer:

105 days

Step-by-step explanation:

in one month there is 30 or 31 or 29 days

November: 30 days - 21 days (given)

30 - 21 = 9

December: 31 days

January: 31 days

February: 29 days

March: 5 days (given)

9 + 31 + 31 +29 + 5 = 105 days

Answer:

Aladdin 2019 is the live-action remake of the 1992 classic animated movie. Directed by Guy Ritchie, the movie stars Will Smith, Mena Massoud, Naomi Scott, and Marwan Kenzari.

The movie follows Aladdin, a thief in the streets of Agrabah, who falls in love with the Sultan’s daughter, Princess Jasmine. After he discovers a genie inside a lamp, he uses its magic to win her hand in marriage.

In terms of visuals, the movie is stunning. Although it is a live-action remake, there is definitely a Disney magic in the film that makes you feel like you are watching an animated movie. The film also manages to recreate some of the classic songs from the animated film while creating some new ones as well.

When it comes to the performances, Will Smith absolutely steals the show as the Genie. His comedic timing is spot on and his rendition of "Friend Like Me" is both amusing and heartwarming. Mena Massoud and Naomi Scott also give good performances as Aladdin and Jasmine respectively.

Overall, Aladdin is a charming, fun movie with a lot of heart. It brings back memories of the original film while creating a few new touches of its own. Fans of the original film will not be disappointed.

30. Last 04 march, it was 46.4°F in korea and 36°C in the Philippines. What was the difference in the temperature of the two countries in °C? show solution


Answer:

28°C

Step-by-step explanation:

Okay, first we need to convert fahrenheit into celsius. To get the conversion of fahrenheit to celsius, you need to deduct 32, multiply by 5, then divide by 9. You get  8°C. Now, subtract 8°C from 36°C. 28°C.
