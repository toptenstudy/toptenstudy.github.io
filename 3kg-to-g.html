15. Tomas has two boxes to be shipped. one box weighs 3kg. the other weights 720 g. what is the combined weight of both boxes in grams?​


The unit of weight is one of the basic units in the field of exact science, also called mass. Based on the International Units (SI), the standard unit of weight is the kilogram (kg). In addition to the kilogram, other units used in everyday life are grams, ounces, pounds, lbs, quintals, and tons. We usually use this unit when shopping at the market or at a grocery store that sells basic needs, buying goods at a building store, and so on.

Calculating the unit of weight can use the following unit conversion ladder (see the picture below).

How to use the conversion ladder above is:

Every time you go down one ladder, the unit conversion is multiplied by 10.Every time you go up one ladder, the unit conversion is divided by 10.

Here are some equations for converting units of weight in different sizes for different units.

1 kg = 1000 grams1 kg = 10 ounces1 ounce = 100 grams1 hg = 1 ounce1 kg = 2 pounds1 pound = 5 ounces1 ton = 1000 kg1 ton = 10 quintals1 quintal = 100 kg

Based on the questions given above, the data obtained are:

Weight of the first box = 3 kg

Weight of second box = 720 grams

Solution

Because what we are looking for in the problem is the sum of the weights of the two boxes, before adding them up, we need to change the units to the same unit, namely grams. For the first box weighing 3 kg, we need to multiply it by 1000 because the change from kg to grams goes down three steps, with the value of one ladder multiplied by 10. So the weight becomes

First box = 3 x 1000 = 3000 grams

As for the second box, there is no need to change the unit because it is under what was asked in the question. Then, we can write to find the total weight of the two.

Total amount = 3000 grams + 720 grams = 3720 grams

So, the combined weight of the two boxes of 3 kg and 720 grams is 3720 grams.
