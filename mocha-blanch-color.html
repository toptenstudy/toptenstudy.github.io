6. A vanilla cupcake costs twice as much as mocha cupcake. A vanilla and a mocha cupcake cost Php 73.50. How much is 3 mocha and 5 vanilla cupcake​


Vanilla cupcake= 2x
Mocha cupcake = x
Vanilla & mocha cupcake = Php73.50

To get the price of each cupcake, this will be the equation;
2x+x= 73.50

Solution
2x+x= 73.50
3x= 73.50

You need to divide 73.50 by 3 to get the price of the mocha cupcake .
73.50/3
= php24.50

The price of the mocha cupcake is Php24.50 so the vanilla cupcake will be Php49 because the price of it is twice the price of the mocha.

The question is how much is 3 mocha and 5 vanilla, so this will be the equation;

(3*Php24.50)+(5*Php49)
(‘*’ represents multiply)

Solution
3*24.50= 73.50
5*49=245
Php73.50 + Php245
= Php318.50

Answer
Php 318.50

18. what is the meaning of blanch​


blanch
/blan(t)SH/

1.
make white or pale by extracting color.
"the cold light blanched her face"

2. flinch or grow pale from shock, fear, or a similar emotion.
"many people blanch at the suggestion"

Explanation:

BLANCH AT (SOMETHING):

To balk or hesitate when faced with something unpleasant. This phrase can also include or describe one's visible paleness, as to "blanch" something means to whiten it.

EXAMPLE:

All of my friends ran into the creepy haunted house, but I blanched at the sight of it.

Stella blanched at the plate of cooked ants that had been set before her.

BLANCH WITH (AN EMOTION)

To become visibly pale as a result of feeling a particular emotion.

EXAMPLE:

All of my friends ran into the creepy haunted house, but I blanched with fear when I saw it.

Stella blanched with disgust at the plate of cooked ants that had been set before her.
