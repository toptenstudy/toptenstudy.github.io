5. What are the materials used to make a bamboo bed? (Not Specific)


Answer:

the knife, handsaw,the chisel, hammer or mallet, measuring tools

Answer:

DIY Tutorial

In this DIY tutorial we'll show you how to make a bamboo bed with very basic hand tools and without the use of any metal nails or screws.

Inventory of Materials

bamboo-bed-materials.jpg

Tools Needed

tools-bed.jpg

Step 1: Drilling Double Holes in the Bamboo Bed Frame Rails

Cut two bamboo canes of 220 cm length (the tip of the cane should include a node). Continue by drilling two double holes in each bamboo bed frame rail (A). The first hole should be made at 8 cm from the tip of the bamboo cane and the second hole at 202 cm from the first. The holes should be 7 cm in diameter.

how-to-make-a-bamboo-bed_1.jpg

Step 2: Drilling Single Side-holes in the Bamboo Bed Frame Rails

Rotate the bamboo canes (A) 90° and drill 4 single holes (not penetrating the two sides of the cane). Drill the first hole at 24 cm from the tip of the cane (the one with the node), and the other ones 56 cm from the first. All holes must be 7 cm in diameter.

how-to-make-a-bamboo-bed_2.jpg

Step 3: Cutting a Fish Mouth Joint in the B1 Cane

The first cut is made with an angle of 45° starting at the end of the cane (B1). This cut must be made at both ends of the bamboo.

how-to-make-a-bamboo-fish-mouth-joint_1.jpg

Step 4: Cutting a Fish Mouth Joint in the B1 Cane

The second cut is also made at 45° using the previous cut as a reference point.
