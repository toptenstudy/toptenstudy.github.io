TV Commercials

Television advertising relays a specific sales pitch in a limited amount of time. Most television commercials are 30 or 60 seconds long, long enough to provide viewers with relevant information or to form an opinion about a product or service, but not long enough to lose the viewer's attention. Television commercials are typically placed during strategic breaks in the main programming as well as the few minutes before the start of the next hour.
