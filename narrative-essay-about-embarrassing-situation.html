7. How would you fell if Someone posted something embarrassing about you


Answer:

There are many ways on how to deal with embarrassments.  They are not all easy but not impossible.

If someone posted something embarrassing about you, you might:

1. Ignore it. You were not born to please everyone. And do not hate/hurt yourself.  Do not beat yourself up or the one who posted. 

2. Talked to the person who did you wrong. Tell them you're feelings and ask them to put it down.

3. If they refuse, tell your feelings and the side of your story to the people you trust (if you have a confidant, much better). You have to free yourself from the negatives.

4. If you were embarrassed because you were wrong, you must admit your mistakes and you may post your apologies and you'll promise to do your best not to make the same mistakes again.

5. If you were not wrong, you may post a counter-argument (it is your right unless it is not legal) but not in a way that you're giving threats to hurt someone/people, planning to start a gang war or some riot. You must stay decent and diplomatic.

6. Check if your rights are violated.  You may seek counsels/lawyers to help you.

7. Always report the abuser's post. The website might help you by warning the abuser and by deleting their post/account (via due process).

8. Do not be afraid to ask for help. You may seek your parents, trusted friend, leader of your religion sector, or a therapist. If the situation hurts you physically and mentally, you must seek help.
