24. if strand is replicated what complementary strand is produced from strand aatgccagtggt

If this strand is replicated, which of the following is the complementary strand that is produced?

Answer:

T T A C G G T C A C C A

Explanation:

In the DNA base pairing between the two strands of DNA,

   *A (adenine) is pair with T (Thymine) vice versa

   *G (Guanine) is pair with C (Cytosine) vice versa

  A    A     T    G    C    C    A   G   T   G    G    T

  ↓     ↓     ↓    ↓    ↓     ↓    ↓    ↓   ↓   ↓     ↓     ↓

  T     T     A    C   G     G   T    C   A   C    C    A

Here are the list of nitrogenous bases of DNA

   1.Adenine

   2.Thymine

  3. Guanine

   4.Cytosine

These sequence of nitrogenous bases is very important in making or synthesizing a protein needed for the body. Segment of DNA which codes for protein or genetic materials is known gene. The gene sequence is necessary in the body in maintaining its genetic instruction. Problem with miss pairing of the nitrogenous bases would result to some gene mutation which is accompanied by medical or genetic disorders.
