29. Nicholas is sticking rectangular shapes of 15 cm x 80 cm on a square board. If he has to cover the square board completely by the rectangular shapes, what is the smallest possible length of the side of the square board?​


The smallest possible length of the side of the square board would be 80 cm. This is because the width of the rectangular shapes (15 cm) is less than the length (80 cm), so the width would not be a limiting factor in covering the square board. The length of the rectangular shapes (80 cm) would be equal to the length of one side of the square board.
