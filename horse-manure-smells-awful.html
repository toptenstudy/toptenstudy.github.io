5. 0118. Why does the hoatzin manure have stinky smell as a cow manure?A. because the hoatzin ferments its food like a cowB. because the hoatzin eats the same plants the cow eats.C. because the hoatzin does not drink enough water for digestion.D. because the hoatzin uses its stinky smell to push away predators.​


A. because the hoatzin ferments its food like a cow.

Hoatzins smell like a herd of cows because they eat like a herd of cows. Foregut fermentation allows animals to get valuable calories from what would otherwise be mostly fiber, and yet the hoatzin is the only bird to have such an adaptation.
