6. january is 15°C and -5°C what is the temparature change?​


The temperature change will be 20°C.

SOLUTION:

15°C - (-5°C)

= 15 - (-5)

Let's do the keep, change, change rule.

Keep 15.

Change the minus sign ( - ) to addition. ( + )

Change ( - 5 ) to 5.

= 15 + 5

= 20 + °C

= 20°C
