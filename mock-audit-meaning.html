30. What does a continuous long whistle blow during mock drill means? ​


In sports, we first do mock exercises, this exercise is useful for understanding the situation in the field. In practice, there is a long-pitched whistle. The long whistle has two meanings, namely :

The game will take place.The game is temporarily suspended due to injury or other obstacles.

Detail Explanation

Sports are all forms of physical activity that are carried out either in a competitive or relaxed form. Sports generally aim to maintain or improve physical abilities and skills while providing a relaxing effect to people who do sports and entertainment to people watching.

There are whistle-tone symbols in sports that have different meanings:

Two short whistles and long followed as a signal :

So that the players are ready to go to the field.The game in the first half is over.The game in the second half is over.

The sound of one long whistle as a signal :

The game can be started.

The game is temporarily suspended because:

There is a foul.The second ball.There is a goal.There is a disturbance (rain, spectators).
