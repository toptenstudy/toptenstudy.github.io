Answer:

As a student in grade 11, I have had a lot of experiences with general mathematics. From solving equations and graphing functions, to learning about trigonometry and probability, I have gained a lot of knowledge and understanding in this subject. We learn how to approach and solve problems using different methods and strategies, which is a valuable skill in both our academic and personal lives.

Furthermore, general mathematics also helps us understand the world around us. We learn how to apply mathematical concepts to real-world situations, such as finance and statistics, which can be useful in our future careers. Additionally, general mathematics also helps us develop our problem-solving and analytical skills which can be beneficial to our professional lives.

However, there are also some disadvantages of studying general mathematics. It can be challenging and frustrating at times, especially when we are struggling to understand a concept or solve a problem.
