14. 18 is a factor of 54 because itA. “is greater than”C. “divides evenlyB. "is less than”D. multiples​


FACTORS

18 is a factor of 54 because it divides evenly.

What Is a Factor?

A factor is a number that completely divides another number. To put it another way, if adding two whole numbers results in a product, then the numbers we are adding are factors of the product because the product is divisible by them.

How to Find Factors of a Number?

Factors can be found using either division or multiplication. Rules of divisibility can also be applied.

Here are a few crucial characteristics of factors:

Any number's exact divisor is its factor.Every number has a factor of 1, thus.The factor must always be less than or equal to the original number.The greatest aspect of a number is generally thought to be the number itself.

Factors of 54.

The numbers that can divide 54 entirely or evenly are considered to be its factors. A pair of factors are considered to be pair factors when they multiply together to equal 54. The number is totally divided by the factors. As a result, these factors are not a fraction.

Factors of 54: 1,2,3,6,9,18,27 and 54

The whole numbers that are not fractional or decimal are the 54's factor pair numbers. We shall employ the factorization method to determine the factors of the number 54.
