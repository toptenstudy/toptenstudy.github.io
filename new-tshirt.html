2. nina boughts a bix of handkerchiefs and a tshirt for 163 pesos.if the tshirt cost 15 more than the handkerchiefs,how much did the tshirt cost?


Answer:

A t-shirt costs P89.


Step-by-step Explanation:


Let x and x + 15 be the prices.

x + (x + 15) = 163

2x + 15 = 163

2x = 163 - 15

2x = 148

2x/2 = 148/2

x = 74

A box of handkerchieves costs P74.

x + 15

74 + 15

= 89

A t-shirt costs P89.


Checking:

x + (x + 15) = 163

74 + (74 + 15) = 163

74 + 89 = 163

163 = 163
