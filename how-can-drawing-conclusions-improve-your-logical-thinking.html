23. This is the process of thinking about something in a logical way in order to form a conclusion or judgment.


The process of thinking about something in a logical way in order to form a conclusion or judgment is called reasoning.

Explanation details:

In collecting information, humans will use the five senses they have. The information that has been obtained will be filtered, important information will be used as data when making decisions. In the thought process to draw conclusions or judgments, people will try to process the data they know logically.

Examples of reasoning are:

September is the rainy season. During the rainy season, it often rains suddenly.

To anticipate the rain, I always have to bring an umbrella.

Conclusion: It's September and I always have to carry an umbrella to avoid the rain.

Conclusions or judgments drawn from a person's reasoning process are not always correct. Therefore, to get conclusions or correct judgments we must train our reasoning power and gather a lot of information that can be used.

Problem solving refers to the process of finding solutions to difficult or complex issues. Every person have their own problems either it is a professional problem or a personal problem, by understanding problem solving it can help a person to find the right and best solution to fix their problems.

Problem solving styles, in general there are four problem solving styles :

Social sensitive thinking : It refers to the problem solving style to find the best solution for all the people who are involved. The solver put their self in the perspective of others in place to identify with the person. The problem solving is based on the value system that respects other people in a situation. Indicators that are used in social sensitive thinking style; always try to treat others fairly, the problem is guided by everyone's personal issues, to help assess the impact of the problem on other people, and to make everyone be satisfied in the situation.Logical thinking problem solving style : This involves the exploration of the problem and the effects of the environment. The problem solver identify the problem that has occurred and explores the alternatives in solving the problem, and also develop a plan to solve the problem based on the information. Indicators that are used in the logical thinking style; the problem solver have to be analytical, the problem solver look for possible solutions to problems, the problem solver is reasonable, and the problem solver develop solutions and then choose the best options.Intuitive thinking problem solving style : This refers to where the problem solver rely on gut-level reactions or feelings. The solver does not spend a lot of time collecting facts and information about the situation but the solver rely on their feelings to give the best possible solution for everyone involved. Indicators that is used in the intuitive thinking style; the solver consider the future, the solver communicate creatively, the solver develops a imaginative solution to solve the problem, and the solver is able to see new possibilities.Practical thinking problem solving style : This refers to where the solver take in information that is clear and real especially the practical realities and facts, the solver rely on the trust of the pervious experience in dealing with the similar problems. Indicators that are used in the practical thinking style; the solver focuses on what is really happening, the solver trust their experience from previous problem situations, the solve trusts facts rather than other people, and the solver understand ideas through practical applications.
