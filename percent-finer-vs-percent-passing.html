20. Easter philosophy vs. wester philosophywhich phylosophy would you pass down to your children?​


Answer:

What I would pass down to my children is the habit of thinking - not systems or sects. Philosophy is the pursuit of the truth and truth does not belong to any sect and cannot be acquired while one remains grounded within a sect. ‘East’ And ‘West’ are just geographical tags. 23. the finer subdivinsions of the bronchi that connect to the elveoli are called​


Answer:

The segmental bronchi divide into many smaller bronchioles that divide into terminal bronchioles, and then into respiratorybronchioles, which divide into 2 to 11 alveolar ducts. Each alveolarduct has 5 or 6 associatedalveolar sacs. The alveolus is the basic anatomic unit of gas exchange.
