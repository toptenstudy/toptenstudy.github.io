24. Topic Laws of exponentI have learned. I have difficultyI want to know. I realizeTHANK YOU​


Answer:

The topic laws of exponent are:

1. The product of two exponents is always an exponents.

2. The mountain Rule: If the exponent of a number is raised to a power beyond 1, the result is a number that is the sum of the original number and the exponent.

3. The Base-10 Rule: When one number is multiplied by 10, the result is a number that is written in decimal form. 10 x a number is equal to the original number multiplied by 10 raised to the original number's exponent.

4. The Power Rule: The power of a number is the result of multiplying the number by itself raised to a certain power. The result is written in the same number of digits as the original number.

5. The Square Root Rule: The square root of a number is the result of taking the square of the number and dividing it by 2.
