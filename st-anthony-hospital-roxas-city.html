10. How would you connect the life of St. Anthony and St. Agnes in your life?​


Though they lived centuries apart, the lives of Sts. Anthony and Agnes have much in common. Both were young people who devoted their lives to God. Both lived simple lives of prayer and contemplation. And both were willing to suffer for their faith. Their example challenges and inspires us to live our own lives more fully for Christ.

In the life of St. Anthony, we see a man who was devoted to God from a young age. He hermitage in the Egyptian desert, living a life of prayer and contemplation. In his later years, he became a teacher and preacher, sharing the Gospel message with all who would listen. St. Agnes was a young girl who was also devoted to God. She too lived a life of prayer and contemplation. When she was just twelve years old, she was martyred for her faith.

Despite their differences, these two saints have helped people in many ways throughout people life. When people struggle with their faith, people think of St. Anthony and his unwavering dedication to God despite the trials he faced. (karapatang mabigyan ng pagkakataong masuri ang kanyang talaan pangkalusugan at mabigyan ng angkop na pagpapaliwanag)

3. The right to obtain from his physician complete current information concerning his diagnos,treatment and prognosis in terms the patient can reasonably be expected to understand. He has the right to know by name or in person , the medical team responsible in coordinating his care.

(karapatang mabigyan ng kaukulang impormasyon sa pamamaraang madaling maunawaan bago pumiorma sa isang kasunduan , na nagbibigay pahintulot upang ipagpatuloy ang napagplanuhang lunas, na kung saan napaloob ditto ang maaring kaakibat na panganib sa kalusugan ng pasyente )

8. The right to be given information as to continuing health care requirements following discharge or transfer to te other health facilities.

(karapatang mabigyan ng impormasyon tungkol sa gamutan o pangangalagang kailangan , matapos lumabas sa ospital)

The Monastery of Saint Anthony is a Coptic Orthodox monastery standing in an oasis in the Eastern Desert of Egypt, in the southern part of the Suez Governorate. Hidden deep in the Red Sea Mountains, it is located 334 km (208 mi) southeast of Cairo. The Monastery of Saint Anthony was established by the followers of Saint Anthony, who is the first Christian monk. The Monastery of St. Anthony is one of the most prominent monasteries in Egypt and has strongly influenced the formation of several Coptic institutions, and has promoted monasticism in general.
