19. Do you think that the Philippines is harmed as other countries transfer their activities to is through outsourcing


Answer:

Does the Philippines gets harmed when other countries transfer activities through outsourcing?

No. In fact, the Philippines benefits from it.

When a certain company in other countries involves itself in an outsourcing (https://brainly.ph/question/880550), normally it brings harm locally than any other countries outside. And given that the Philippines has outsourcing companies (https://brainly.ph/question/858812), this means that the act of companies involving in outsourcing opens up works for the Filipinos. Hence, the Philippines benefits to it.

What is Outsourcing?

Outsourcing is a practice in the business world where a certain company hires a party outside the company to do or perform services and make or create goods which normally should be done within the company.

Why do Outsourcing?

Companies do outsourcing to cut down labor costs particularly the salaries of its personnel. More so, outsourcing is done in order for the company to focus on the core aspects of the core business and delegate less critical outside the organization.

Downside of Outsourcing

The downside of outsourcing includes security threat (https://brainly.ph/question/1601563) and the difficulty in communication between the company and the party. There is a security threat if the party has access of the confidential information of the company and the party suffers a data breach. In terms of communication, there might be delays in the communication between the company and party which eventually delays services and production.
