5. whyhello can i ask questions? why compatibility of pc parts is very important in building a pc? ​


The compatibility of PC parts is crucial when building a PC because it ensures that all the components work together effectively and efficiently. PC parts come in different shapes, sizes, and specifications, and not all of them are compatible with each other.

For example, a motherboard has a specific socket type for its CPU, and not all CPUs will fit that socket. If you buy a CPU that's not compatible with your motherboard's socket, you won't be able to use it at all.

Similarly, the power supply unit (PSU) has a specific wattage capacity and connector types. If the PSU doesn't provide enough power or doesn't have the necessary connectors for the components you want to use, then you may run into issues such as unstable performance, system crashes, or even hardware damage.

Incompatibilities between PC parts can also lead to poor performance, such as bottlenecks, where one component is holding back the performance of the other components, or thermal issues, where the heat generated by some components can't be dissipated effectively because of incompatible cooling solutions.

Therefore, it's important to research and carefully choose compatible PC parts to ensure that they work seamlessly together, and to avoid wasting time and money on parts that won't work or perform optimally.

NERVOUS SYSTEM – Motherboard

All computer parts are connected to each other on the motherboard. All electrical signals are

conducted through it. Without a motherboard, all other components are lifeless.

HARD DRIVE = BRAIN – Subconscious

All knowledge of programs, files and data is stored on the Hard Drive. Just like the brain, it is

sectioned off for various purposes. The hard drive stores and retrieves all of our precious

information and programs for use by use whenever we wish. Just like our long term memories.

CPU CHIP = SPINAL CORD – (Central Processing Unit)

A chip that is on the Motherboard that is responsible for processing instructions (commands)

received from hard drive (brain). It controls every activities of the computer. Just like the spinal

cord, it receives instructions from the brain (hard drive).

RAM - Random Access Memory = BRAIN – conscious thought

RAM is a holding area of files and instructions that are to be used and then forgotten about. It

allows the computer to multi-task just as your brain’s conscious thought allows you to multitask. The more RAM, the better your computer can multi-task, and the faster it will perform.

SPEAKER / PRINTER = MOUTH

Both are output devices. They produce what the computer needs to give out.

MOUSE / KEYBOARD = HANDS

Both are input devices. They are used to send in instructions and information into and out of

the computer.

WEB CAM = EYES

Web cam detect light and send signals through the optic lens to the visual and the computer to

the CPU. Just exactly like how the eyes works sending visual signals to the brain through optic

nerves.

MICROPHONE = EARS

Microphone receives sound waves and sends it into the computer for processing

MONITOR = FACE

This is an output device. It has a screen which it uses to display what the computer is doing or

how it feels. Just like the human face shows different expressions.

SYSTEM CASE = SKIN

This gives the computer a definite shape. It defines a computer telling us more about it, how it

responds to the conditions around. Same implies the human skin, it defines the human body.

27. What is the essence of following the correct procedure in connecting the PC parts?


Answer:

The Essence of following the correct procedures in connecting the PC parts

Connecting PC parts requires skills and safety procedures. Without proper skills on how each part works, you may end up destroying the computer and much worse, is hurting yourself. We wouldn't want that to happen. Before we touch any computer parts, we must be familiar on what part should be touched first and what would be the next. We must also know how each part works and their functions to ensure that our unit or computer is working smoothly, thus producing the right output.

Knowing how your computer works and their parts is not enough, the much important part is following the correct procedures in connecting PC parts. The reason behind it is for safety measures. Like the first reasons, we must protect ourselves from damages and problems that might arise if we just connect each part without following right procedures. If we fail to put on safety gears and observe proper behavior in handling PC parts, we might end up grounded, hurt and we could destroy our computer.
