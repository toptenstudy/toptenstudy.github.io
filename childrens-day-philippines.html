4. give your reaction answer in to the provided space 1. An apple a day keeps the doctor away. Reaction: 2. Vaccine is the solution to this pandemic. Reaction: 3. The Philippines is the happiest country in the world. Reaction: 4. Children must respect parents and elderly all the times. Reaction​


Answer:

Explanation:

1."An apple a day keeps the doctor away." This is a common saying that Emphasizes the importance of eating a healthy diet and taking care of one's health. While eating an apple every day may not completely prevent someone from needing to see a doctor, consuming a balanced diet that includes fruits and vegetables is beneficial for overall health.

2."Vaccine is the solution to this pandemic." This statement is supported by scientific evidence and consensus among public health experts. Vaccines have been proven to be effective in preventing the spread of infectious diseases, including COVID-19. Getting vaccinated is a crucial step in ending the pandemic and returning to normalcy.

3."The Philippines is the happiest country in the world." This statement is subjective and depends on various factors such as the methodology used in the survey, the sample size, and the demographics of the respondents. While the Philippines may have scored high in a particular survey, it does not necessarily mean that it is the happiest country in the world. Happiness is a complex and multifaceted concept that is difficult to measure objectively.

4."Children must respect parents and elderly all the times." This statement emphasizes the importance of respecting one's elders and is a common cultural value in many societies. However, respect is a two-way street, and it is important for parents and elderly individuals to also show respect to children and younger generations. Additionally, respect should not be automatic or unconditional, and it is important for individuals to earn respect through their actions and behavior.
