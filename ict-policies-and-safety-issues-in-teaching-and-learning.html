Eight policy themes are commonly identified in educational technology policies around the world. These relate to

(1) vision and planning;

(2) ICT infrastructure;

(3) teachers;

(4) skills and competencies;

(5) learning resources;

(6) EMIS;

(7) monitoring and evaluation; and

(8) equity, inclusion, and safety. Four stages of policy development can be identified related to each of these themes.
