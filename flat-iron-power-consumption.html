1. On weekend our household keeper ironed clothes using flat iron (900Watts) she used the electric fan (100Watts) and watched tv (200Watts) for 4hrs.Compute the total electric power consumption.


Answer:

Total electric power consumption is 4.8 kW-hr.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Write the given

900 Watts of using flat iron100 Watts of using electric fan200 Watts of watching TVTotal usage of 4 hrs.

Step 2: Determine the formula that will be used.

The unit of measurement of electric power consumption is in kW-hrIn order to solve for the total electric power consumption, add the power usage of each appliance. Assuming that all of the appliances are working or open during the 4 hrs given. Multiply the hours of usage to the power usage to solve for the electric power consumption.

Step 3: Solve for the total electric power consumption

Power usage of all the appliances= 900 Watts + 100 Watts + 200 WattsPower usage of all the appliances is 1200 W or 1.2 kWElectric power consumption is 4.8 kW-hr

Therefore, the total electric power consumption is 4.8 kW-hr.

24. a radio has a power consumption of 160 watts if we turn on the radio for 24hours what is the electric consumption?


Answer:

The electric consumption of the radio is 3.84 kW-hr.

Step-by-step explanation:

Electric consumption is the amount of electricity used by a certain device or machine in a specific period of time. It is usually written in kilowatt-hour instead of just watt-hour.

Since we have the given Power P = 160 W and and time t = 24 hours, we can directly solve the electric consumption of the radio.

The solution goes like this

[tex]Electric Consumption = Pt\\Electric Consumption  = (160W*\frac{1 kW}{1000W})(24hours)\\ Electric Consumption  = 3.84kWh[/tex]

29. 1.In your opinion, which type of flat iron is more convenient and can help you save the consumption of your electricity at home? ​


Answer:

1. Basic clothes iron

The basic clothes iron is the most commonly used iron. They have a dial to control the temperature according to the type of cloth. It has a metal sole plate. These types of irons are inexpensive and are easily available in the market. Sometimes they are inadequate for your needs. You may often need to use a spray bottle with the simple dry iron. These irons are often heavy and, they don’t have additional features of auto shut-off.

2. Steam irons

The majority of irons used by dressmakers in the market are steam irons. These irons include a small section of the water container. This section is filled with water, allowing the iron to produce steam and hence it is known as a steam iron. A neater finish and a smoother press job is created with the help of steam. Especially garments like cotton or linen are very well finished with it. The stubborn wrinkles are removed by the steam. The other common features that are found in this product are auto shut-off and retractable cord.

3. Vertical steam

This is the latest innovation in the field of clothes iron. The vertical clothes iron is horizontal as well. This is a feature that needs to spend more on. But it will definitely be an excellent time saver. This iron produces steam just like a normal steam iron but it is very helpful in removing wrinkles and proves to be a better alternative to the traditional method of ironing.

4. Travel irons

These are smaller and lighter irons than the standard one. These are very helpful for the travelers as they are compact for packing and some of them even hold the folding feature. They may or may not offer the feature of steam capabilities. Some are chargeable and others are cordless. If you are a traveler, then this type of iron will be the most compatible one. However, many hotels have the facility of laundry and ironing for their customers. This may be less essential than you think but the travel-sized iron can also be proved helpful in the case of emergency. Choose a travel iron that is small and easy to use and that works well for your personal travel needs.

5. Deluxe clothes irons

The best quality irons are deluxe clothes iron and it includes excellent steam features and easy temperature controls. The features like auto shut-off and a retractable cord are also available. They consist of high quality non-stick sole plates. Multiple heat holes are present and the water tank is very easy to fill. These irons are expensive but if you want a heavy-duty iron then it will be worth the money.
