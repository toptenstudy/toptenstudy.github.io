21. sumulat nang tatlong uri nang halamang ugat​


Explanation:

Carrot , singkamas , beet

Answer:

Ang halamang-ugat ay mga halamang gaya ng kamote, patatas, at katulad na itinatanim dahil sa malakí at nakakaing ugat.

Itinuturing itong geophyte, ibig sabihin, nagtataglay ng organong nag-iimbak ng enerhiya sa ilalim ng lupa. Sa pamamagitan nito, natatagalan ng halamang-ugat ang mga mapinsalang kondisyong tulad ng matagal na tagtuyot at taglamig.

Ang mga halamang-ugat ay nagkakaiba sa komposisyon ng sugar, starch, at carbohydrates. Mayroong dalawang uri ng halamang-ugat: ang true roots na kinabibilangan ng tuberous roots at taproots; at ang non-roots tulad ng tubers, risoma, korm, at bulbo.

24. 6. Alin ang halamang may malaking ugat o taproots? ​


Alin ang halamang may malaking ugat o taproots?

​

A taproot is a large, dominating root that sprouts additional roots laterally from it. A taproot is often straight and thick, with a tapering form that develops directly downward. The taproot of some plants, such as carrots, is a well-developed storage organ that has been cultivated as a vegetable. Taproot include jicama, parsnips, salsify, and turnips, among other root vegetables. Several weeds, such as plantain and kudzu, rely on taproot to live. Balloon flower, Bugbane, Butterfly weed, Comfrey Cushion spurge, Dill False blue indigo, Horseradish, Lupine, Oriental poppy, Parsley Sea holly are some common garden flowers and herbs with taproots (along with their cultivars).
