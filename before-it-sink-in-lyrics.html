Problem:

You drop your cellphone while in the bathroom in front of the mirror while the water is running is in the sink below. If you are 0.45meters above the sink,how long do you have before your cell phone is a gonner.

Given Data:

Vi (initial velocity)  =  0 m/s (at top)

d (distance) = 0.45 m

t (time) =  unknown

a (acceleration) = 9.8 m/s²

Solution:

We can use the formula  t = √d/1/2a  since Vi = 0 m/s

 t = √d/1/2a

   = √ 0.45 m / 1/2(9.8 m/s²)

)

   = √ 0.45 m / 4.9 m/s²

   = √0.0918 s²

   = 0.303 s

Answer:

You have  0.303 seconds or 0.30 seconds or 0.3 seconds

15. C. Directions. Write float or sink before the number 11. metal spoon 12. detergent soap 13. plastic cup 14. coconut husk 15. ship​


Answer:

SINK 11. metal spoon

Explanation: The metal spoon is denser than water, so it would sink.

SINK 12. detergent soap

Explanation: The surfactants such as dish soap or detergent powder break up water's surface tension. As a result, objects floating in water will sink or change shape as the surface tension changes.

FLOAT 13. plastic cup

Explanation: It completely submerged, the buoyant force on the cup is very similar to its own weight, so it appears to neither sink nor float underwater. Plastics made up of "Polystyrene" are about 1g/cm³. these will do. heavier duty plastic cups will definitely sink faster, although not as fast as a coin.

FLOAT 14. coconut husk

Explanation: The Coconut is a large and heavy fruit. It is adapted for dispersal by water in the following way. It has spongy fibre, which helps it to float in water.

FLOAT 15. ship​

Explanation: The air that is inside a ship is much less dense than water. That's what keeps it floating! ... If the average density of the ship is ever greater than the density of water, then the ship will sink beneath the surface of the water.
