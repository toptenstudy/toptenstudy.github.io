GINSAHR

The answer is SHARING.

This is the act or value of giving or lending something as a gift for being kind and concerned for someone who is in need. True definition of sharing is giving without asking something in return. In tagalog, "Ito ay pagtutulong at pamamahagi ng walang hinihinging kapalit." Those who share may not get something in return from other people, but God will provide blessings.

Examples:

Sharing your food to a beggar in the street.

Sharing a book to a classmate who left his.

Sharing offering to the Lord.
