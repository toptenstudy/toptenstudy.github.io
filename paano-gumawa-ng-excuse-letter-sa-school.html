5. example of excuse letter for school


EXCUSE LETTER SAMPLE FOR SCHOOL

(Date)

Dear Teacher/s,

Please excuse me for being absent from your class today because I’m having a headache due to cough and colds. I promise to come back to school when I am already well. Your prayer for my recovery is highly appreciated.

Thank you for your kind consideration regarding this matter.

Respectfully yours,  

signature

(NAME OF PUPIL)

HOW TO WRITE AN EXCUSE LETTER Write the date as the very beginning of your letter. Write the name of whom you are going to address your letter. Begin the body by stating the purpose of the letter direct to its point. Be sure to sound your letter formal and polite. Give your reason of being absent. You can hope or thank the consideration or the positive response about your situation. Write your closing. Write your name below the closing in uppercase letters and bold. Write your signature above your name.  Have somebody to send it to school or to your teacher while you are absent so you will be excused.

PARTS OF A LETTER Heading – this is used for companies and other formal institutions Date – written to inform what date the letter is made or take effect Address – address of where the letter is being sent to Salutation -  giving respect or addressing the certain person whom the letter is intended for '; also called as greetings Body – this is where you state your purpose of giving a letter Complimentary Close – the closing part before the name of the sender that expresses the latter’s regard to the receiver Signature – name and signature of the sender/owner of the letter

19. Excuse Letter for Being absent in school due to Fever


Answer:

EXCUSE LETTER

Excuse letter is a formal written notification  asking for apology,  it is a note of explanation of an absence. Excuse letter is expected from a students who has unexpectedly missed a day in school.  Excuse letter should include time and the reason of absence. The letter is usually short and simple that contains basic facts.

Parts of an excuse letter

1. Heading - It includes the return address and date

2.Greetings

3. Body

4. Closing

Example of an excuse letter

May 7, 2019

123 Kamias Street, Paranaque

Metro Manila, Philippines

Dear Maam:

Good day!

Please excuse me for being absent in your class today, July 5, 2019 because I have a fever. I was not able to go to school because I feel so dizzy.  I hope that you will understand me regarding this matter and allow me to have a remedial activities on the lesson I had missed.

Thank you for your kind consideration.

Sincerely yours,

ANGEL VEM

Grade 3 - Ilang-Ilang
