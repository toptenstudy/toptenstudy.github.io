1. Abilities and Skills

3. Knowledge

-facts and information

4. Energy or capacity to do work

1. TIME

- includes long and short periods

2. MONEY

- purchasing power to help acquire needed goods and services

3. Goods and Property

- materials may be durable , non perishable or perishable

4. Community Facilities

- include police station, park, roads, public schools, library , health centre etc.

5. ENVIRONMENTAL RESOURCES

- include, rain, sunlight, air and soil which help human provide basic needs such as foods, shelter and clothing.

Resources vary from family to family. Those family with more resources can easily accomplish more activities at home. Those with fewer resources need to budget and use their resources wisely
