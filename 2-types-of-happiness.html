ANALOGY

Answer:

1. Type of  Analogy: Antonym

inflation : _______ :: strong : _______

The appropriate words to fill in the blanks are "deflation" and "weak". Therefore, the analogy will be read as: inflation is to deflation as strong is to weak.

2. Type of Analogy: Part to Wbole

petal : _______ :: keyboard : _______

The appropriate words to fill in the blanks are "flower" and "computer". Therefore, the analogy will be read as: petal is to flower as keyboard is to computer.

3. Type of Analogy: Synonym

fortunate : _______ :: thankful : _______

The appropriate words to fill in the blanks are "lucky" and "grateful". Therefore, the analogy can be read as: fortunate is to lucky as thankful is to grateful.

4. Type of Analogy: Location

Mt. Parker : ______ :: Mt. Matutum : _________

The appropriate words to fill in the blanks are "Tboli" and "Tupi". Therefore, the analogy will be read as:

Mt. Parker is to Tboli as Mt. Matutum is to Tupi.

5. Type of Analogy: Cause and Effect

______ : yawning :: ______ : scratching

The appropriate words to fill in the blanks are "sleepy" and "itchy". Therefore, the analogy can be read as: sleepy is to yawning as itchy is to scratching.
