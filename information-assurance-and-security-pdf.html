6. how can you assure that your data is secured​


Answer:

There are several steps that you can take to help secure your data:

Use strong passwords: Use long, complex passwords that are difficult to guess or crack. Avoid using the same password for multiple accounts and consider using a password manager to help you generate and store strong passwords.

Enable two-factor authentication: Two-factor authentication (2FA) requires you to provide an additional piece of information (such as a code sent to your phone) in addition to your password when logging into an account. This can help prevent unauthorized access to your accounts.

Keep your devices and software up to date: Make sure to keep your devices and software (including your operating system, web browser, and antivirus software) up to date with the latest security patches and updates. These updates often include important security fixes and can help protect against new threats.

Use encryption: Encrypting your data makes it unreadable to anyone who does not have the decryption key. This can help protect your data if it is intercepted or accessed by an unauthorized party.

Be cautious when sharing personal information: Be careful about sharing personal information, such as your name, address, or financial information, online or over the phone. Only share this information with trusted sources and make sure that the website or service you are using is secure.

By following these steps, you can help protect your data and keep it secure.
