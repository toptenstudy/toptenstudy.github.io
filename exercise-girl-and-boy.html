1. the ratio of the boys to girls in the gym is 11:7. When 12 more girls enter the gym to exercise, the ratio of boy ti girls becomes even. Altogother how many boys ti girls are in the gym ?


Let x = ratio

Math Equation

11x = 7x + 12

Solve for x

11x = 7x + 12

Transpause

4x = 12

x = 3


Solve for boys and girls

Substitute x

11(3) = 7(3) + 12

33 = 21 + 12

33 = 33


Answer: There are 33 boys and 33 girls in the gym.

Original ratio of boys and girls ⇒  11 : 7

Common factor: x

When 12 more girls enter the gym, the ratio becomes 1:1.

New number of boys and girls:

Boys: 11x

Girls: 7x + 12

Equate (Proportion):

11x : (7x+12) = 1:1

11x = 7x + 12

11x - 7x = 12

4x/4 = 12/4

x = 3

Original number of boys and girsl in the gym:

Boys: 11(3) = 33

Girls: 7(3) = 21

Present number with 12 more girls and ratio of 1:1:

Boys: 33

Girs: 21+12 = 33

ANSWER: There are 33 boys and 33 girls.

(Note the present tense "are" in the question}.

16. EXERCISE 3 Read the situation below, then determine if it shows bias or prejudice. Leonardo chose the white-skinned girl over the black-skinned boy to be his friend. pls need ko po


Answer:

The situation shows prejudice based on skin color.

Explanation:

The situation shows prejudice because Leonardo chose the white-skinned girl over the black-skinned boy based solely on their skin color, without considering their individual personalities, abilities, or qualities. Prejudice is a preconceived opinion or attitude about someone based on their perceived group membership, such as race, gender, religion, or nationality, without taking into account their individuality. Bias, on the other hand, is a tendency or preference towards a particular perspective or point of view, which can be based on personal experience, culture, or social norms. While bias and prejudice are related, prejudice specifically involves discrimination or unfair treatment based on someone's perceived group membership.
