18. what did romeo challenges faced in story romeo and juliet​


Answer:

The main obstacle that Romeo and Juliet face is the feud that divides their two families. On the broadest level, the feud makes it impossible for them to go to their families and request to marry each other, as they know it would never be allowed.

26. what role did Romeo play in the play, Romeo and Juliet?


Answer:

Romeo is the male lead in the play and acts as the play's hero. He is central to the plot through his relationship with Juliet, the heroine, and many of Romeo's choices govern the action in the play. For example, his choice to go to the Capulet feast against his better judgement leads to him meeting Juliet.
