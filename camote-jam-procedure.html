6. Why is it important to follow the proper procedure in cooking jellies, jams, marmalades and preserves?​


Jams, marmalades, preserves and conserves are fruit products preserved by sugar. These products differ in gel consistency, ingredients and how the fruit is prepared. They are easy to make at home.

Jams are made from crushed or ground fruit and usually have a thick consistency due to high pectin content.

EXPLANATION:

Marmalade is a jelly with pieces of fruit suspended in it. 22. Procedures in making mango jam​


Answer:

1. Wash mangoes thoroughly to remove dirt.

2. Slice into tidbits

3. Scoop the flesh using a spoon.

4. Scrape the pulp from the seeds using a knife. Mash with a fork. Measure.

5. Add an equal amount of sugar. Mix well.

6. Cook. Stir constantly.

7. Add 0.3% citric acid (1 and 1/2 tsp. for over 4 and 1/4 cups of mango pulp) when almost thick.

8. Continue cooking until temperature reaches 105oC.

9. Pour hot jam in sterilized jars and leave 1/4 inch head space.

10. Cover tightly. Cool.

29. Angel had 1 3/4 kilos of camote. After his father gave him some kilos of camote, he has10 kilos of camote now. How much kilos of camote did his father give?​


Answer:

8 1/4 kilos of camote

Step-by-step explanation:

Subtract the total of kilos Angel had before her father gave him camote from 10 kilos of camote.

10 - 1 3/4 = 8 1/4

Checking:

8 1/4 + 1 3/4 = 10

FINDING THE DIFFERENCETo find out how many were given, subtract the following.

[tex]sum = addend1 + addend2 \\ 10 = 1 \frac{3}{4} + n \\ \\ sum - addend1 = addend2 \\ 10 - 1 \frac{3}{4} = n[/tex]

Convert the Mixed Number and the Whole Number into an Improper Fraction.

[tex]10 - 1 \frac{3}{4} = n \\ \frac{10}{1} - \frac{7}{4} = n[/tex]

To Subtract, the denominators should be in the same value. To find that, find the LCM of the denominators.

[tex] \frac{10 \times 4}{1 \times 4} - \frac{7 \times 1}{4 \times 1} = \frac{40}{4} - \frac{7}{4} \\ [/tex]

Subtract the following.

[tex] \frac{40}{4} - \frac{7}{4} = \frac{33}{4} \\ [/tex]

Simplify if the numerator is greater than the denominator.

[tex] \frac{33}{4} = 8 \frac{1}{4} \\ [/tex]

Thus, the difference is 8¼
