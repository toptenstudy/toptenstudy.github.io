14. Nico and Ella purchased a radio for 128 pesos. Nico paid 36 pesos more than Ella. How much did each pay?​


memememmemememememmememem

Answer:

Cost of Radio = 128 pesos

E = Ella's share

N = Nico's share = E + 36

So Cost of Radio = E + N

128 = E + N

but N = E + 36

so,

128 = E + (E + 36)

128 = 2E + 36

2E = 128 - 36

2E = 92

[tex]\huge\boxed{E = 46}[/tex]

Now solve for N

N = E + 36

N = 46 + 36

[tex]\huge\boxed{N = 82}[/tex]

So Nico paid 82 pesos while Ella paid 46 pesos.
