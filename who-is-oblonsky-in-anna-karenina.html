1. Reflective essay of Anna karenina​


answer:

Anna Karenina can be considered as a vivid depiction of Russian life for a socially advantaged family in the nineteenth century. Having to deal with various social influences that lead to vices such as adultery, the relations of the family and more so of the siblings are put to the test. The Oblonsky family is described at great lengths and a vivid picture is created on the goings on of the society at the time. The novel begins with the introduction of Stephen Oblonsky arguing with his wife. The cause of the fight is the affair that Stiva had with one of the female governess. Dolly, (Stiva’s wife) is threatening to leave him but Anna Karenina; Stiva’s sister comes and quells the quarrel. Anna Karenina is married to a high-ranking government.

28. Anna and her brother are both 62 inches tall. anna is in the 85th percentile for height for her age and her little brother is in the 90th percentile. who is taller for their age?


According to percentile data, the Anna brother is taller for his age.

Explanation :

Percentile is the term in statistics for dividing a data group into 100 equal parts. So, there are 99 percentile values that divide the data into 100 parts. The same terms as quartiles divide the total data into 4 parts.

If Anna and her brother have the same height metrically but have different percentile levels.

Anna is 62 inches tall with the 85th percentile for her age which means her maximum height at Anna's age is 72.9 inches.

Meanwhile, the Anna brothers have the same height as the 90th percentile for their age, which means that their maximum height at Anna's Brothers age is 68.8 inches.

This means that based on age, Anna brothers is taller than Anna by 90 percentile of 62 inches.
