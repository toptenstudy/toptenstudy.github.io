In Bohol, tarsiers are used as a tourism symbol, although there are little regulations in place. At roadside attractions, many tarsiers are kept in appalling conditions. Because tarsiers are difficult to maintain alive in captivity, mortality is high; when they die, replacements are gathered from the wild. Wild tarsiers are also threatened by deforestation and more typhoons as a result of climate change.
