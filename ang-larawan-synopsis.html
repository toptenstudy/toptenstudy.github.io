1. ang mahiwagang biyulin synopsis​


Answer:

"Ang Mahiwagang Biyulin" is a popular Filipino folklore about a magical violin that possesses the power to enchant and control people through its music. The story revolves around a poor but talented musician named Pedro, who dreams of owning a biyulin so he can use its magic to help his community.

Explanation:

One day, Pedro meets a mysterious old man who offers him a biyulin in exchange for a promise that he will use it only for good deeds. Pedro agrees and soon discovers the incredible power of the instrument. He uses the biyulin to cure the sick, calm raging storms, and even stop a war.

However, Pedro's success catches the attention of the corrupt governor, who wants to use the biyulin for his own gain. He kidnaps Pedro and demands that he use the instrument to control his enemies and increase his power. Pedro refuses, and the governor threatens to harm his family and destroy his village.

Desperate to save his loved ones, Pedro agrees to play the biyulin for the governor, but instead of using its power for evil, he plays a beautiful melody that touches the hearts of all who hear it. The governor is moved by the music and sees the error of his ways, releasing Pedro and returning the biyulin to him.

Pedro returns home a hero, using the biyulin's power to bring peace and prosperity to his village. He teaches others to play the instrument and continues to spread its magic for the good of all.

21. what is the invironmental influences. synopsis of biag ni lam ang


Answer:

Biag ni Lam-ang (lit. 'The Life of Lam-ang') is an epic story of the Ilocano people from the Ilocos region of the Philippines. It is notable for being the first Philippine folk epic to be recorded in written form, and was one of only two folk epics documented during the Philippines' Spanish Colonial period, along with the Bicolano epic of Handiong.[1](p6) It is also noted for being a folk epic from a "Christianized" lowland people group (the Ilocano people),[2] with elements incorporated into the storytelling.
