13. is 213 divisible by 3?​


Answer:

Yes

Step-by-step explanation:

The divisibility rule for 3 states that an integer is divisible by 3 if the sum of the integer's digits is divisible by 3.

As the rule states, we should find the sum of the digits of the number first, then, check if the sum is divisible by 3.

In this case, the sum of the digits of 213: 2 + 1 + 3 = 6 is divisible by 3, therefore, 213 is divisible by 3.
