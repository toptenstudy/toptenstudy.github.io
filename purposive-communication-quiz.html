28. Assignment: (English) Do it on a whole sheet of paper . look for the definition of the following words 1. communication. 2. non-verbal communication. 3. verbal communication 4. Speech 5. gestures Note: Prepare for a short quiz on monday about multimedia elements​


ANSWER:

Communication refers to the process of exchanging information, ideas, or messages between individuals or groups through various mediums such as speech, writing, or body language.

Non-verbal communication refers to the transmission of information or messages through facial expressions, body language, tone of voice, or other physical cues rather than words.

Verbal communication refers to the use of words, either spoken or written, to convey information, thoughts, or ideas.

Speech refers to the act of speaking or delivering a message to an audience or listener. It can also refer to the formal presentation of ideas or opinions to an audience.

Gestures refer to physical movements, usually of the hands or arms, that are used to convey a message or communicate a meaning. They can be intentional or unintentional and can vary in meaning depending on cultural context.
