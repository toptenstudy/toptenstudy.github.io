30. Ifrs prohibits which cost flow assumption


Answer:

FIFO and average-cost are the only two acceptable cost flow assumptions permitted under IFRS. IFRS requires companies to use the same cost flow assumption for all goods of a similar nature. GAAP has no specific requirement in this area.
