2. To qualify for a police academy, candidates must score in the top 10% on a general abilities test. Assume the test scores are normally distributed and the test has a mean of 200 and a standard deviation of 20. Find the lowest possible score to qualify.


Answer : 225.64

[tex] \frac{score - 200}{20} = 10\% = 0.10[/tex]

The standard normal distribution shows that

x = 1. 282

[tex] \frac{score - 200}{20} > 1.282[/tex]

Score > 1.282 × 20 + 200 = 225.64

The lowest possible score to qualify is 225.64
