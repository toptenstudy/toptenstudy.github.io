6. who is Antonio Pigatifa​


Antonio Pigafetta was a Venetian scholar and explorer. He joined the expedition to the Spice Islands led by explorer Ferdinand Magellan under the flag of King Charles I of Spain and after Magellan's death in the Philippines, the subsequent voyage around the world

Antonio Pigafetta (Italian: [anˈtɔːnjo piɡaˈfetta]; c. 1491 – c. 1531) was a Venetian scholar and explorer. He joined the expedition to the Spice Islands led by explorer Ferdinand Magellan under the flag of King Charles I of Spain and after Magellan's death in the Philippines, the subsequent voyage around the world. During the expedition, he served as Magellan's assistant and kept an accurate journal, which later assisted him in translating the Cebuano language. It is the first recorded document concerning the language.

Pigafetta was one of the 18 men who made the complete trip, returning to Spain in 1522, under the command of Juan Sebastián Elcano, out of the approximately 240 who set out three years earlier. These men completed the first circumnavigation of the world. Others mutinied and returned in the first year. Pigafetta's surviving journal is the source for much of what is known about Magellan and Elcano's voyage.

At least one warship of the Italian Navy, a destroyer of the Navigatori class, was named after him in 1931.

Partnership Business

Ms. Carreon's contribution is worth a total of ₱1,483,780. Mr. Carreon and Mr. Abad are in a partnership-style business. Since Ms. Carreon shared 46 3/5% shares, the other percentage will be shouldered by Ms. Abad which is Php 1,700,000.

Here is the step-by-step solution:

Since the share should be cut to two, Ms. Abad's share is equal to:

[tex]100% - 46\frac{3}{5} %[/tex]% - [tex]46\frac{3}{5}[/tex]% = [tex]53 \frac{2}{5} %[/tex]% which is also equal to Php 1,700,000.

In order for us to solve the problem easier, we should convert the percentage form into decimal form. That is:

[tex]\frac{53\frac{2}{5} }{100}[/tex] or dividing the fraction by 100%. We shall get 53.4% so we need to move the decimal twice to the left twice so we will get 0.534.

Set the equation to find the value of x.

0.534x = ₱1,700,000
x = ₱1,700,000 / 0.534
x = ₱3,183,520.60

So the total capital is ₱3,183,520.60 but what we are looking for is Ms. Carreon's contribution.

To know Ms. Carreon's share. we need to multiply her share by the total capital. We will obtain:

0.466 x ₱3,183,520.60 = ₱1,483,780.

20. The total mass of Eric and Antonio is 79.5 kg. If Eric loses 3.5 kg and Antonio's mass remains unchanged, they will have the same mass. What is Antonio's mass?​


Answer:

Antonio's mass is 76 kg.

Step-by-step explanation:

Eric's mass + Antonio's mass = 79.5 kg

Let's denote Antonio's mass by "A".

According to the problem statement, the total mass of Eric and Antonio is 79.5 kg, so we can write:

Eric's mass + Antonio's mass = 79.5 kg

We also know that if Eric loses 3.5 kg and Antonio's mass remains unchanged, they will have the same mass. Let's call this new mass "M". So we can write:

(Eric's mass - 3.5 kg) + Antonio's mass = M

Antonio's mass remains unchanged, so we can substitute "A" for Antonio's mass in both equations:

Eric's mass + A = 79.5 kg

(Eric's mass - 3.5 kg) + A = M

We want to find Antonio's mass "A", so we need to eliminate Eric's mass from these equations. One way to do this is to subtract the second equation from the first:

Eric's mass + A - (Eric's mass - 3.5 kg) - A = 79.5 kg - M

Simplifying this equation, we get:

3.5 kg = 79.5 kg - M

Adding M to both sides, we get:

M + 3.5 kg = 79.5 kg

Subtracting 3.5 kg from both sides, we get:

M = 76 kg
