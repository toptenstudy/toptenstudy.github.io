Flocking technique lines the interior of drawers and boxers with a soft velvety finish.

Inlaying combines several techniques that involve inserting decorative pieces in to a base object to incorporate new designs on the original product

24. Give Me 5 Benefits Of Woodcrafting


Benefits of Wood crafting or Woodworking
*The Enjoyment in creating the craft.
*The satisfaction of building something on your own.
*enhancement of your practical skills.
*Helps Health for it can be an exercise.
*may became a job or business in the near future.Well, aside from the unhelpful things that are caused by wood-crafting like cutting off trees, it can also give us benefits or merits.
1. Having more furniture that are not fragile
2. By cutting down trees that are already sick, we can plant new trees and still use the sick and old one for new things that can be made using woodcraft. (in other words, recycling)
3. People that love art may find their futures or passion by wood-crafting.
4. There are some of us that have trouble finding a job, so if you have a lot of trees then you can use them for wood-crafting and make money (just make sure to replace the trees you used).
5. You can have fun using all sorts of art on wood-crafting, it can even be a hobby.
