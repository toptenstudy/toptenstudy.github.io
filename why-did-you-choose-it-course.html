In particular, in systems analysis and design, we were able to solve real world problems by discovering design patterns - general solutions to commonly occuring patterns. Thus through information systems you will learn how to identify peoples problems, and oversee a solution (not always software in some cases) from start to finish, to ultimately solve the users problem, which usually leads to solving a problem to a bigger group too, like society. Information systems helped me work better with people, as almost all the projects were group projects, and helped me handle conflict better. 29. why did you choose cookery course


Reasons to take a cookery course

In everyday life, food is one of the basic human needs. That's why there are so many outlets selling various foods. And to produce good food, there are many schools or institutes that open cookery courses for people who want to learn to cook.

1. To master cooking skills

Of course, this is one of the main reasons for taking the cookery course. By following the cookery course, it is hoped that we will be able to master all the basic skills of cooking. Starting from how to choose the ingredients, process them and then mix and match all the ingredients into one delicious dish. This cooking skill can later be used for a career or for cooking at home.

2. Encourage a healthy lifestyle

When learning to cook, besides getting to know the various types of ingredients, we will also learn about the nutrients in each ingredient. We also learn how to process or cook food in a healthy way. If we apply it in daily life, then slowly we will start to encourage a healthy lifestyle.

3. To open a restaurant or food outlet.

Maybe not everyone who takes the cookery course wants to open a restaurant or food outlet, but one of the benefits that we get after taking the cookery course is having sufficient ability to be able to open a restaurant or food outlet, and participants who want to do business can consider opening their own restaurant.

4. To become a chef

This is another reason to take the cookery course. By taking this course, course participants will gain a lot of knowledge about the types of dishes and how to make them. When the course is finished, interested participants can start a new career in the culinary field, one of which is becoming a chef.
