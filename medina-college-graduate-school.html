19. Studies have concluded that a college degree is a very good investment. Suppose that a college graduate earns about 75% more money per hour than a high-school graduate. If the lifetime earnings of a high-school graduate average Php 12.5 million, what is the expected value of earning a college degree?


Answer:

EXPECTED VALUE OF EARNING OF A COLLEGE GRADUATE:

Php 21,875,000 (Twenty-one million, eight hundred seventy five thousand pesos)

Explanation:

The problem states that a college graduate earns 75% more than a high school graduate. A high school graduate on the other hand earns an average on Php 12.5 million.This problem involves percentages. To solve this we simply multiply Php 12.5 million by 175%. Why 175%? It's because 75% more means 100% + 75%Next, 175% in decimals would be 1.75Next, Php 12.5 million x 1.75 = Php 21, 875,000.

Another way is:

Multiply Php 12.5 million by 75%, so we get Php 9,375,000Since college graduates earn 75% MORE, we will add Php 9,375,000 to the principal amount of Php 12.5 millionTherefore, our final value is Php 21, 875,000.
