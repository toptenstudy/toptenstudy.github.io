Answer: A total of Php 151,425

Step-by-step explanation:

75%(2500)=Php 1875 each VTP

75%(2800)=Php 2100 each BTP

27 VTP × Php 1875=Php 50625

45 BTP × Php 2100=Php 100800

17. define tour package #asap​


Answer:

A package tour, package vacation, or package holiday comprises transport and accommodation advertised and sold together by a vendor known as a tour operator. Other services may be provided such a rental car, activities or outings during the holiday. Transport can be via automobile, buses, charter airline, and may also include travel between areas as part of the holiday. Package holidays are a form of product bundling.

Package holidays are organised by a tour operator and sold to a consumer by a travel agent. Some travel agents are employees of tour operators, others are independent.

21. what is the difference between a package tour ang independent tour​


Answer:

A package tourist goes on a package tour, where they pay for all flights, transfers, accommodation, etc., together and in advance, usually through a travel agency. An independent traveler books different aspects of their trip separately (for example, they might book their flight on the internet, then get a taxi from the airport to their hotel, pay for their hotel when they arrive at their destination, etc.) and does not usually rely on an agency.
