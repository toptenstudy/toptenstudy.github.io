25. Shane walks 10 mi at 4 mph. Damien walks 12 mi at 5 mph. Which of them will take the longest?​


To determine who will take the longest, we need to calculate the time it takes for each person to complete their respective distances. We can use the formula:

time = distance / speed

For Shane, time = 10 miles / 4 mph = 2.5 hours.

For Damien, time = 12 miles / 5 mph = 2.4 hours.

Therefore, Shane will take the longest time to complete his distance, with a time of 2.5 hours, compared to Damien's time of 2.4 hours.
