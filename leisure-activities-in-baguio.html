1. what activities are considered as leisure activities


People participate in leisure activities when they have free time in the evenings and on weekends. They are never work oriented and do not involve tasks such as housework or sleeping. Examples of leisure activities are bicycling, bowling, curling, horseback riding, golfing, hiking/walking, skating, skiing and swimming.

8. What are examples of recreational/leisure activities?


Answer:

Recreational and leisure activities are those that individuals engage in for enjoyment and relaxation, rather than for work or other obligations. Some examples of recreational and leisure activities include:

Sports: such as basketball, soccer, tennis, golf, swimming, etc.

Outdoor activities: such as hiking, camping, fishing, rock climbing, skiing, snowboarding, etc.

Arts and crafts: such as painting, drawing, pottery, knitting, scrapbooking, etc.

Music: such as playing an instrument, singing, listening to music, attending concerts, etc.

Reading: such as books, magazines, newspapers, etc.

Watching movies or TV shows: either at home or in a theater.

Playing board games or card games with family or friends.

Traveling: visiting new places and experiencing different cultures.

Cooking or baking: trying out new recipes or preparing meals with family or friends.

Gardening: planting and tending to a garden, growing vegetables or flowers.

These are just a few examples of the many different recreational and leisure activities that people enjoy
