25. 10 Bad Study Habits Successful Students Avoid ​


Answer:

1. Procrastination: Successful students avoid procrastination and prioritize their tasks to complete them on time.

2. Cramming: Successful students don't wait until the last minute to study for an exam or complete an assignment. They study consistently and avoid cramming sessions.

3. Distractions: Successful students avoid distractions, such as social media, games, and television, while studying. They create a quiet and distraction-free study environment.

4. Lack of planning: Successful students plan their study schedule in advance and set realistic goals to achieve them.

5. Multitasking: Successful students avoid multitasking as it can lead to distraction and lower productivity.

6. Lack of focus: Successful students maintain their focus and concentration while studying. They avoid multitasking and take regular breaks to refresh their minds.

7. Lack of sleep: Successful students prioritize their sleep and avoid staying up late to study. They understand the importance of getting enough rest to retain information and perform well.

8. Prohibited substances: Successful students avoid using prohibited substances such as drugs and alcohol while studying.

9. Ineffective study methods: Successful students use effective study methods such as active recall, spaced repetition, and summarization to improve their retention of information.

10. Poor time management: Successful students manage their time effectively and avoid wasting time on non-productive activities. They allocate their time wisely, balancing their academic work with their personal and social life.
