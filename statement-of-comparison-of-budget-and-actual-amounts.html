19. The citys budget is $8,000,000. The city actually spends $12,000,000. What is the cities deficit?


Answer:

The city's budget deficit is $4,000,000.

Step-by-step explanation:

Budget deficit is when the expenditures exceeds the allotted budget.

Savings/Deficit = Allotted budget - Expenditures

Savings/Deficit = $8,000,000 - $12,000,000

Savings/Deficit = - $4,000,000

Since the result is negative, there's a budget deficit. The deficit is $4,000,000.

Deficit is defined as an excess of expenditure or liabilities over income or assets in a given period.

In this case, all we have to do is deduct the expense from the city's budget
$8,000,000 - $12,000,000 = - 4,000,000

Which means that the deficit of the city is $4,000,0000.
