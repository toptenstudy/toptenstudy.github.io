29. how to start an essay about myself


First list down your best traits. Then list down your worst traits. Then group the traits to form a story that is interesting to read. But do not make your list too long or your essay will become boring! If you do already have a long list, ask your bestest friend to filter what you have written so only the best traits of you appear on the essay.
