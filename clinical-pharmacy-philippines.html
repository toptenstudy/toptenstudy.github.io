23. In your own words, describe the pharmacy education in the Philippines.​


Answer:

For those who desire to work in one of the many areas of the pharmaceutical sector, a four-year undergraduate degree called the Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy is available. The program's main goals in the Philippines are to find, isolate, synthesize, and formulate physiologically active molecules for application in medicine. Additionally, students are able to gain a deeper grasp of the various elements influencing drug behavior in the body.
