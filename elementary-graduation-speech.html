10. Reflection on the graduation speech by Sungwon Hong​


Answer:

Sungwon Hong's graduation speech was a powerful reflection on the journey of self-discovery and personal growth that one goes through during their time in college. Hong's speech was both inspirational and thought-provoking, as he urged his fellow graduates to embrace the challenges and struggles that they will inevitably face in the future.

One of the key themes of Hong's speech was the importance of taking risks and pursuing one's passions, even when the path ahead may seem uncertain or daunting. He emphasized the need to be bold and fearless in the pursuit of one's dreams, and to approach life with a sense of curiosity and wonder.

Another notable aspect of Hong's speech was his acknowledgement of the role that failure can play in our lives. He encouraged his fellow graduates to embrace failure as a necessary part of the learning process, and to view setbacks as opportunities for growth and development.

Overall, Sungwon Hong's graduation speech was a moving and inspiring call to action, urging his audience to pursue their passions with courage and conviction, and to embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

21. graduation grade6 speech​


Answer:

Dear Teachers, Parents, and Fellow Graduates,

Today marks a significant milestone in our lives as we graduate from grade 6. We have come a long way since our first day of school, and we should be proud of all that we have accomplished.

Firstly, I would like to thank our teachers for their unwavering commitment to our education. They have been our mentors, our guides, and our friends throughout this journey. They have challenged us to think critically, encouraged us to explore our interests, and helped us develop the skills we need to succeed in life. We are grateful for their patience, dedication, and passion for teaching.

I would also like to express our gratitude to our parents, who have been our constant support system. They have cheered us on through every success and failure, helped us with our homework, and provided us with the love and encouragement we needed to keep going. We could not have made it this far without their unwavering support.

As we move on to the next chapter of our lives, we will face new challenges and opportunities. We will encounter new people, new ideas, and new experiences. But we should remember the lessons we have learned in grade 6. We should continue to work hard, stay curious, and never give up on our dreams.

Graduation is not the end, but rather the beginning of a new journey. We are ready to take on the world and make a positive impact in our communities. We are the future, and we have the power to shape it.

Congratulations, fellow graduates. Let us celebrate this momentous occasion and look forward to the bright future that lies ahead.

Thank you.
