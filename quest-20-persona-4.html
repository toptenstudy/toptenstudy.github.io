When mapping a customer journey, it's essential to have a clear understanding of the persona or group of personas you want to focus on. A persona is a fictional representation of your ideal customer that helps you understand their needs, goals, behaviors, and pain points.

To define the persona, you need to gather data from various sources, such as customer surveys, interviews, website analytics, and social media insights. This data will help you create a detailed profile of your ideal customer that includes demographic information, job title, income, hobbies, interests, and more.
