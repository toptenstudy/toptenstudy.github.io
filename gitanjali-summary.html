1. What is the theme and summary of Gitanjali poem 8?​


Answer:

Theme:

A theme refers to the underlying message or meaning of a work that the writer conveys directly or indirectly. A work can have both major as well as minor themes. It can also have several themes.

Explanation:

Throughout the Gitanjali collection, Tagore expresses a joyful, personalized spirituality with emphasis on devotion, faith, and an individual’s relationship with the divine in contrast with the official rules and practices of orthodox religion.


2. The Story of Gitanjali


Explanation:

Gitanjali embodies most of these aspects of Indian philosophy. The major theme in Gitanjali is devotion to God. This paper focuses on the Indian philosophical aspects and the theme of devotion in Rabindranath Tagore’s Gitanjali.

Gitanjali focuses on the all-pervading presence of God everywhere. Gitanjali brings its readers into direct contact with the Infinite. Gitanjali proclaims that God is neither an abstraction, nor an incarnation, but an ever-present force and an all-pervasive influence. He is to be seen in the various forms of nature including humans.
