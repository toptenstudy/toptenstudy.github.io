If collector current increases due to change in temperature or change in β, emitter current IE also increases and voltage drop across RE increases thus reducing the voltage difference between base and emitter. 4. ab= 3x - 5 and DC= x+7, find the value of x, Ab and DC​


Step-by-step explanation:

We are given that:

AB = 3x - 5

DC = x + 7

We can use these equations to find the value of x, AB, and DC.

To find x, we can set the two expressions equal to each other and solve for x:

AB = DC

3x - 5 = x + 7

2x = 12

x = 6

Therefore, x = 6.

To find AB, we can substitute x = 6 into the expression for AB:

AB = 3x - 5 = 3(6) - 5 = 13

Therefore, AB = 13.

To find DC, we can substitute x = 6 into the expression for DC:

DC = x + 7 = 6 + 7 = 13

Therefore, DC = 13.

So the value of x is 6, AB is 13, and DC is 13.

DC motor

A DC motor is any of a class of rotary electrical motors that converts direct current electrical energy into mechanical energy. The most common types rely on the forces produced by magnetic fields.

DC generator

A direct-current (DC) generator is a rotating machine that supplies an electrical output with unidirectional voltage and current. ... The field is produced by direct current in field coils or by permanent magnets on the stator. The output, or armature, windings are placed in slots in the cylindrical iron rotor.
