1. Find the ratio of A-cash of 60,700 pesos. To B-cash of 30,000 pesos.


Answer:

60,700 : 30,000 = 607 : 300

Solution:

We can simplify the ratio 60700 : 30000 by dividing both terms by the greatest common factor (GCF).

The GCF of 60,700 and 30,000 is 100.

Divide both terms by 100.

60,700 ÷ 100 = 607

30,000 ÷ 100 = 300

Therefore:

60,700 : 30,000 = 607 : 300
