5. How do the principles of effective oral communication differ from those of effective written communication? Do they have similarities at all?


Answer:

Both principles have similarities; the purpose of both principles is to give tips and tricks in order to have/give an effective output. However, they differ in terms to where these principles are applicable.

Principles of Effective Oral Communication is only applicable to any communication done verbally. On the other hand, Principles of Effective Written Communication is applicable to any communication done through writing.
