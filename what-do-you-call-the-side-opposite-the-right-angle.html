8. in a right triangle,what do you call the opposite side of the right angle?​


Answer:

Hypotenuse

Step-by-step explanation:

There are 3 sides in a triangle and there is one right angle in a right angle triangle.

A right angle in a triangle is denoted by a small rectangle.

The name of the three sides in a triangle are base, perpendicular and hypotenuse.

Base and perpendicular of a right triangle are the sides that intersect at right angle in a triangle.

Here, AB and BC are the sides that intersect at right angles, and hence their names are perpendicular and base.

Whereas the hypotenuse is the longest side in a right triangle, which is AC in the figure of triangle ABC

Therefore, the side opposite to the right angle in a triangle is called hypotenuse of the triangle.
