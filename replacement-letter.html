In the Philipines, we are colonized by Spain from 1521 to 1898. Since we are colonized for so long we have learned some of the language and letters of Spain. The result is that we replaced and add letters like "ñ" in the Filipino alphabet in order to speak and write our native language with regard to the Spanish alphabet.

The modern Filipino alphabet (Filipino: makabagong alpabetong Filipino), otherwise known as the Filipino alphabet (Filipino: alpabetong Filipino), is the alphabet of the Filipino language, the official national language and one of the two official languages of the Philippines. The modern Filipino alphabet is made up of 28 letters, which includes the entire 26-letter set of the ISO basic Latin alphabet, the Spanish Ñ and the Ng digraph of Tagalog. It replaced the Pilipino alphabet of the Fourth Republic. 15. A sample is small when n≤30. Select one: a. The statement is FALSE. Replace the letter n with letter x. b. The statement is FALSE. Replace the ≤ with ≥ c. The statement is FALSE. Replace the word small with large. d. The statement is TRUE.


In a multiple choice test, exam or quiz, You must read the statement carefully and understand the choices. Sometimes the choices  are close to each other but you are going to choose the best.

A sample is small when n≤30.

The best answer must be letter D. The statement is true.

As I recall during my high school life, my teacher said that when you do research, it is better to have a sample size greater than 30. But there is no problem if you have a small sample size which is less than 30.

The central limit theorem states that “If you have a population with mean μ and standard deviation σ and take sufficiently large random samples from the population with replacement, then the distribution of the sample means will be approximately normally distributed”. This will hold true regardless of whether the source population is normal or skewed, provided the sample size is sufficiently large (usually n > 30). If the population is normal, then the theorem holds true even for samples smaller than 30.
