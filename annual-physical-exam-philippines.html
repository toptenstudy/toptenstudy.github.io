Percentage of 18 students:

18 ÷ 0.24 = 75%

Percentage of students that aren't cleared:

24 - 18 = 6 students

6 ÷ 0.24 = 25%

Davao - This city in Mindanao has an annual average temperature of around 27°C (81°F). The hottest months are April and May, with temperatures reaching up to 33°C (91°F). The coolest months are December and January, with temperatures dropping to around 24°C (75°F).

Zamboanga - Located in western Mindanao, Zamboanga has an annual average temperature of around 28°C (82°F). The hottest months are April and May, with temperatures reaching up to 33°C (91°F). The coolest months are December and January, with temperatures dropping to around 24°C (75°F).
