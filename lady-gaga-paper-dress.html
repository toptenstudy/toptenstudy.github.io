7. which line from the poem the ladys dressing room would best help the reader infer celias social class


Answer:In reading the poem, The Dressing Room by Jonathan Swift, we can appreciate it by analyzing and undwrstanding it. We can move on by analyzing the lines of the poem that can help the reader infer the character's (Celia's) social class.

To be able to understand a poem, one must apply various strategies and techniques to analyze it. Consider this link to be able to understand analyzing poem. brainly.ph/question/251953

Also, one must consider his or her points of analysis. There are several techniques and / or methods that can be used to focus your analysis in different points of view, and one of which is through knowing the socio-political issues, social classes, and social mobilization of characters, and this is through Marxist analysis. You can refer to this link to know more about social classes. brainly.ph/question/99140. To know about social mobilization, click this link brainly.ph/question/275299.

Consider the poem The Lady's Dressing Room by Jonathan Swift. There are several lines that could give us a hint or could describe the social status, social class, and further, the social mobilization.

Consider these lines:

1. Five hours, (and who can do it less in?)

By haughty Celia spent in dressing;

2. The goddess from her chamber issues,

Arrayed in lace, brocades and tissues.

3. Now listen while he next produces

The various combs for various uses,

These three lines could prime us that somehow, Celia belongs to a class that can afford several cosmetic items to suffice her beauty regimen needs.

The fact also that there is a room dedicated to house her cosmetic materials is an indication that she lives in a spacious abode.

The language of the poem that was used, e.g. the specificity of the cosmetic material, i.e. various combs, lace, brocades, tissues, etc. signifies the meticulousness of a lady in terms of affording those kinds of wants.

All in all, words and lines in the poem that would signify one's social class can be interpreted and arranged to be able to decode and to infer characterization and a character's social class.

Answer:

₱745.00 paid by each sister

Step-by-step explanation:

₱385.00 × 4 = ₱1,540.00

₱1,540.00 - ₱50.00 = ₱1,490.00

₱1,490.00 ÷ 2 = ₱745.00
