12. An new electric fan used by James uses less energy than his older fan. If the older fan uses 75W and the new fan uses only 60W, how much would james save when he uses the new fan with electricity cost at ₱19.5/kW.H, for 3 hours in 14 days?


Answer:

James can save ₱98.28 in electricity cost for 14 days by using the new fan instead of the old fan.

Explanation:

To compute the energy consumption and cost of electricity for both fans, we need to know the total energy used for 3 hours by each fan.

The energy consumption of the old fan in 3 hours is:

Energy = Power x Time

Energy = 75W x 3 hours

Energy = 225 Wh or 0.225 kWh

The energy consumption of the new fan in 3 hours is:

Energy = Power x Time

Energy = 60W x 3 hours

Energy = 180 Wh or 0.18 kWh

Therefore, the new fan consumes 0.045 kWh less energy than the old fan in 3 hours.

To compute the total energy used in 14 days, we need to multiply the energy consumption for 3 hours by the number of times James uses the fan in 14 days, which is:

Number of times used in 14 days = 14 days x (24 hours/ day) / 3 hours per use

Number of times used in 14 days = 112

The total energy consumed by the old fan in 14 days is:

Total Energy = Energy per use x Number of times used

Total Energy = 0.225 kWh x 112

Total Energy = 25.2 kWh

The total energy consumed by the new fan in 14 days is:

Total Energy = Energy per use x Number of times used

Total Energy = 0.18 kWh x 112

Total Energy = 20.16 kWh

Therefore, the new fan saves 5.04 kWh of energy compared to the old fan in 14 days.

To compute the cost of electricity for 14 days, we need to multiply the total energy consumed by the electricity cost per kWh, which is:

Cost = Total Energy x Cost per kWh

Cost = 25.2 kWh x ₱19.5/kWh

Cost = ₱491.40

The cost of electricity for 14 days using the old fan is ₱491.40.

The cost of electricity for 14 days using the new fan is:

Cost = Total Energy x Cost per kWh

Cost = 20.16 kWh x ₱19.5/kWh

Cost = ₱393.12

Therefore, James can save ₱98.28 in electricity cost for 14 days by using the new fan instead of the old fan.
