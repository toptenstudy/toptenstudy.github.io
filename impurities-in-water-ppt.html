6. What is the % mass of 4.35 g of nacl dissolved in 105 g of water ppt?


Answer:

3.98%

Solution:

%mass = (mass of the dissolved NaCl/total mass of the solution) times 100%

total mass of the solution = 4.35 g + 105 g  

total mass of the solution = 109.35 g

%mass = 4.35/109.35 times 100%

%mass = 3.98%
