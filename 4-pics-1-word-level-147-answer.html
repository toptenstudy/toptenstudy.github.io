27. how many multiples of 8 are between 4 and 147​


Answer:

There are 18 multiples of 8 in between 4 and 147.

Step-by-step explanation:

The problem is an application of arithmetic sequence where it was being asked the the value of n.

In mathematics, arithmetic progression also known as an arithmetic sequence is define as a sequence of numbers such that the two consecutive terms has a common difference.

Common difference means the second minus the first one.

Arithmetic Sequence FormulaFinding nth term: an= a1 + (n-1)dFinding sum: sn= 1/2n(a1+an)Where

an is the nth term

sn is the sum of all terms

a1 is the first term

n is the number of terms

d is the common difference

ProblemSolve for n

Given

a1= 8

an= 144

d= 8

Solution

an= a1 + (n-1)d

144= 8 + (n-1)8

144= 8 + 8n - 8

144= 8n

n= 18

Solving in other waysSince the number between 4 and 147, so the first multiple of 8 is 8, the first term of multiple.

So, you can give the number of multiples by simple dividing it by 8.

147 ÷ 8 = 18.375

147 ÷ 8 = 18

Thus, there are 18 multiples of 8 in between 4 and 147.
