3. I need your help, can you answer now. thank u po Questions (Business Law) 4. What is prestation? 5. What is consideration?


✏Answer✏

4.In legal terms, prestation refers to the act of performing or fulfilling an obligation or duty. It is an essential element of a contract, wherein one party agrees to provide goods, services, or perform an act, while the other party is obligated to pay for or compensate for such performance.

5.Consideration, in the context of business law, refers to something of value that is given or promised by one party in exchange for the promise or performance of another party. It is a crucial element of a contract, and it can take the form of money, goods, services, or a promise to do or refrain from doing something. Consideration is necessary to create a legally binding contract, as it signifies that there has been a bargained-for exchange between the parties involved. Without consideration, a contract may be unenforceable.

4. Ma’am Jenny is obliged to transport the goods of Ma’am Irish from Manila to Cebu, and Ma’am Irish is obliged to pay Ma’am Jenny 10,000 Pesos as transport cost, under contract of carriage. i. Who is the active subject? (2.5 Points) ii. Who is the passive subject? (2.5 Points) iii. Which is the prestation? (2.5 Points) iv. What is the efficient cause? (2.5 Points)​


Answer:

I. The active subject is Ma'am Jenny as she is obliged to transport the goods.

II. The passive subject is Ma'am Irish as she is obliged to pay the transport cost.

III. The presentation is the contract of carriage between Ma'am Jenny and Ma'am Irish.

IV. The efficient cause is the agreement between Ma'am Jenny and Ma'am Irish regarding the transportation of goods in exchange for payment of 10,000 Pesos.

Answer:

i. The active subject in this scenario is Ma'am Jenny, as she is the one who is obligated to transport the goods from Manila to Cebu.

ii. The passive subject in this scenario is Ma'am Irish, as she is the one for whom the goods are being transported by Ma'am Jenny.

iii. The prestation in this scenario is the transportation of goods from Manila to Cebu, which is the service being provided by Ma'am Jenny to Ma'am Irish.

iv. The efficient cause in this scenario is the contract of carriage between Ma'am Jenny and Ma'am Irish, which establishes the obligations and rights of both parties with regards to the transportation of goods and payment of transport cost.
