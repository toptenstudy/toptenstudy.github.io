11. pagkakaiba at pagkakapareho ng (pcm)perfectly competitive market at (icm)imperfectly competitive market ​


Answer:

Pagkakaiba:

1. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market ay may maraming mga kalakal at mga serbisyo na magagamit sa pamilihan, habang ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay may mas maliit na bilang ng mga kalakal at mga serbisyo.

2. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market ay may malinaw na presyo na nakikipag-ugnayan sa kalakal at serbisyo, habang ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay may mga presyo na hindi malinaw at maaaring lumikha ng oligopoli.

3. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market ay may mga maliliit na tagagawa at konsyumer na nag-aangkin ng pag-aari, habang ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay may mga malalaking tagagawa at konsyumer na nag-aangkin ng pag-aari.

Pagkakapareho:

1. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market at ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay parehong hindi makontrol ang presyo ng mga kalakal at serbisyo.

2. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market at ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay parehong naglalayong makamit ang pinakamataas na kita para sa mga tagagawa at konsyumer.

28. What is crime?What is the Golden Rule in Criminal Investigation?3.Differentiate PCO and PNCO ?4.Tools in Criminal Investigation?5.Differentiate Tactical Investigative Response and Strategic Investigative Response?​


Crime- an action or omission that constitutes an offense that may be prosecuted by the state and is punishable by law.

Golden Rule- The Golden Rule in Criminal Investigation. Do not touch, alter, move, or transfer any object at the crime scene unless it is properly marked, measured, sketched and/ or photographed."

Tools- Tools To establish facts and develop evidence, a criminal investigator must use these tools-information, interview, interrogation, and instrumentation.

TIR & SIR- These two different types of investigative responses are defined by the nature and status of the event that the investigator is facing.
