4. a big greeting card with a message promoting respect for differences


Greeting Card

A greeting card has limited features since it is something that is only used for short messages and for greeting someone of certain celebratory occasions.

An example of a greeting is:

Dear Someone,

You might this surprising but we are congratulating you on your first promotion.

You certainly have done well.

Love,

Your Department.

You see, a greeting card isn’t supposed to contain long messages or sappy love messages. It is solely for greeting someone for something. Though that may be the case, it is still up to the person writing on the card to decide whether to only include a greeting or write long meaningful sentences.
