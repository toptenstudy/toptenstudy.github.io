One planet living is simply adjusting resource usage

In sum, one planet living is a vision where humans will have to use natural resources that are available in mother earth. As one may know, with each passing day and with each usage of natural renewable or non renewable resource, some day there will be resource scarcity or even complete disappearance of it.

Thus, the goal of one planet living is to make use of the resources available in this planet with great attention and realization that it will disappear some day. d. Lastly, resources usage whose end goal is to benefit the human themselves (markets and social issues) is not what is meant with 'one planet living', as this statement is more focused on consuming and benefiting for the humans, not resources-driven.
