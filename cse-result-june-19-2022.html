6. roles of school and parents/guardians in cse (comprehensive sexuality education)program.​


Answer:

The Comprehensive Sexuality Education (CSE) program aims to provide young people with accurate, age-appropriate, and culturally sensitive information about sexuality and relationships. The program is a joint responsibility of schools and parents/guardians, and both have important roles to play.

The roles of schools in CSE include:

1. Providing a safe and inclusive learning environment where students can ask questions, express their views, and receive accurate and non-judgmental information about sexuality and relationships.

2. Developing and implementing a comprehensive CSE curriculum that is age-appropriate, culturally sensitive, and evidence-based.

3. Ensuring that teachers and other school staff receive adequate training on CSE, including how to teach the subject matter effectively, address sensitive topics, and respond to students' questions and concerns.

4. Encouraging the involvement of parents/guardians in the CSE program, such as by inviting them to parent-teacher meetings, providing them with information about the curriculum, and seeking their feedback and input.

The roles of parents/guardians in CSE include:

1. Being involved in their children's education and supporting their learning about sexuality and relationships.

2. Providing accurate and age-appropriate information about sexuality and relationships based on their cultural and religious values and beliefs.

3. Encouraging open communication with their children about sexuality and relationships, including answering their questions, listening to their concerns, and respecting their opinions.

4. Monitoring their children's access to media and technology and helping them navigate potential risks and challenges related to sexuality and relationships.

Overall, the collaboration between schools and parents/guardians is crucial to the success of CSE programs. By working together, they can ensure that young people receive accurate, comprehensive, and inclusive information about sexuality and relationships, and are equipped with the knowledge and skills they need to make informed decisions and lead healthy and fulfilling lives.


7. what is comprehensive sexuality education (cse)​


Comprehensive sexuality education (CSE) is a holistic approach to teaching about sex and relationships. It is an age-appropriate, culturally relevant approach that provides scientifically accurate information on topics such as human development, sexual health, gender roles, relationships, sexual orientation, responsibility, and respect. It is used by teachers, parents, and other stakeholders to help young people develop the knowledge, attitudes, skills, and values to make healthy, responsible decisions about sexuality.

16. express your thoughts and feelings about the 2022 election result


The elections is one of the fundamental features of a Democratic country. During elections, the Filipinos are empowered by their right of choice to elect the people that they want to lead the country.

The recently held elections shows one such result of the citizens exercising the citizens’ power. The Filipinos have chosen the leaders that they want from a choice of qualified candidates both in the national and local levels. What is most notable about the recently held elections is the number of votes recorded which exceeded the votes recorded during the previous elections. In particular, outgoing president Duterte (who is already considered very popular during his campaign) garnered a total of over 16 million votes. On the other hand, the incoming president-elect Marcos Jr. garnered a total of over 31 million votes – almost twice as much as Duterte’s votes. The same can be said about the results for the senatorial elections.

It is important to note that the leaders elected by the citizens will be responsible for leading the country for the years to come. These leaders will be responsible for representing the Philippines in foreign relations and crafting policies to assist the Filipinos as we go through our day to day live. We can be hopeful that the leaders will do everything within their power to ensure that their actions are done with the best interest of the Philippines in mind. However, this is not to say that we must solely rely on the elected leaders to better our lives. Each of us must still work hard to contribute to the development of our lives and the lives of those around us. The leaders are elected only to lead us to a common goal. It is still up to us as individuals to forge our path to success or failure.
