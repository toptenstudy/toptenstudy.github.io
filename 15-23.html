3. What is the mean of the data set [12, 15, 25, 23, 15, 12, 22, 23, 15].


What is the mean of the data set [12, 15, 25, 23, 15, 12, 22, 23, 15].

ANSWER: 18

HOW TO GET MEAN

∑x / n

∑x = sum of all values

n = number of values

Basically, add all the values, then divide the sum by the number of values.SUBSTITUTION

12 + 15 + 25 + 23 + 15 + 12 + 22 + 23 + 15 = 162

162 / 9 = 18

Question:

What is the mean of the data set [12, 15, 25, 23, 15, 12, 22, 23, 15] ?

Answer:18Step-by-step explanation:

Mean is the average. Let us add all of the values of the data set, then divide it with the number of values that are in the data set.

12 + 15 + 25 + 23 + 15 + 12 + 22 + 23 + 15 = 162

162 ÷ 9 = 18

The mean of the data set is 18.
