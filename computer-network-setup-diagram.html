10. -if you will be given a chance to setup a company's networking, what would you prefer? traditional networking or cloud networking and why?? ( paki sagot po plss! )​


Answer:

I would choose cloud networking, as the cloud provides a range of advantages over traditional networking. Cloud networking allows for on demand scalability, increased flexibility, improved access to remote and global locations, enhanced security, and streamlined cost savings, all without the need for a massive on-site computing platform. Additionally, cloud networking also has near-instant deployment and disaster recovery capabilities, which can be critical for businesses operating in highly competitive markets.

(Answered by Knowledge original and Valid Opinions to help and guide the questioners)

As an IT professional, if I were given the chance to set up a company's networking, I would carefully consider the specific needs of the company before deciding between traditional networking and cloud networking.

If the company requires a highly secure and customizable network, traditional networking may be the best option. This is because traditional networking involves the use of physical hardware that can be more easily secured and customized to meet the company's specific needs. However, traditional networking can be expensive and time-consuming to set up and maintain, so the company would need to have a dedicated IT team to manage the network infrastructure.

On the other hand, if the company needs a scalable, flexible, and cost-effective network, cloud networking may be the better choice. Cloud networking involves the use of virtualized network infrastructure that is hosted and managed by a third-party provider. This type of networking is scalable, flexible, and cost-effective, making it a good option for companies that need to quickly adapt to changing business needs. Cloud networking also provides better security and disaster recovery options, which can be important for companies that need to protect sensitive data.

The choice between traditional networking and cloud networking depends on the specific needs of the company. As an IT professional, I would carefully evaluate the company's needs before making a recommendation on which type of networking to use.

20. if you plan to design computer shop/laboratory network setup. what are the things that you need to consider and why is it important in the physical layout of the network ​


Answer:

When designing a new computer network, whether for five people or 500, it's important to weigh the needs and desires of those who will be using the network with the budget of those who will be paying for it. Some important things to consider that may not be on anyone's wish list include balancing security issues with the desire for easy access to information; building redundancy into the network in anticipation of breakdowns; and standardizing hardware and software to keep maintenance costs in check. In case something terrible happens, a disaster recovery plan should be in place. In case things go well for the organization, room for future growth should be built into the network design.
