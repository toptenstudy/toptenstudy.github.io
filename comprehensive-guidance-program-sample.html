3. who are supposed to be the personnel of a guidance program?​


Answer:

Personal / social, educational, and career growth is facilitated through guidance and therapy services and programs. Guidance means supplying others with some support or assistance. Guidance needs someone's personal support; it is intended to help the person determine where he wants to go, what he wants to do, and how best to achieve his goal.

Counseling is the support given to a person who is having a problem and needs clinical assistance to solve it. Consequently, therapy assistance is needed for people with developmental disabilities because of the disability they experience in any emotional region, either because of genetic factors or environmental conditions.

Guidance and counseling personnel

Guidance counseling staff means those delivering guidance and counseling, a professional faculty, principal, school health staff, administrative staff , teachers, consultants, counselors, adults, peers, etc.

Staff's position in guidance and therapy

Guidance and counseling workers work together to deliver quality instruction, mitigate or address educational , occupational and socio-personal challenges such as physical health issues that could lead to emotional disturbance and stress. Guidance and therapy are vital components of the education system in order to provide people with an ability to see a range of choices available and then help the client make a wise decision. Both instruction and therapy are aimed at supporting all students ' personal / social , educational, and career growth.

7. Explain why continuous evaluation is essential for the school’s guidance program ​


Answer:

School counselors conduct evaluations for the purpose of measuring the value of a program, its activities, and the staff who implement it, in order to decide if the program is viable and worth continuing. These evaluations are also used to determine if changes should be made to a program the next time it is run.

22. What is the legal basis in the implementation of career guidance program?​


Answer:

Career guidance field provides direction, provision, and ability to students to have career readiness, able to plan, making career decisions and achieving career maturity.
