RO = BE

3x + 10 = 5x -16

combine like terms

16 + 10 = 5x -3x

26 = 2x

isolate X

26/2 = 2x/2

13 = X

(if x is 12)

substitute 13 on x

RO = 3x + 10

RO = 3(13) + 10

RO = 39 + 10

RO = 49

BE = 5x -16

BE = 5(13) -16
