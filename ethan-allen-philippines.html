3. paanu mu pahahalagahan ang Philippine Organic Act of 1902 na ipinaglaban ni Allen Cooper​


Congressman Henry A. Cooper authored the Philippine Bill of 1902

Philippine Organic acc (1902)

The Philippine Organic Act (c. 1369, 32 Stat. 691) was a basic law for the Insular Government that was enacted by the United States Congress on July 1, 1902. It is also known as the Philippine Bill of 1902 and the Cooper Act, after its author Henry A. Cooper. The approval of the act coincided with the official end of the Philippine–American War.

16. Allen was a pig at dinner.​


Answer:

Metaphor

Step-by-Step Explanation:

The sentence compares Allen to a pig, without literally meaning it.

If the sentence compared two things using the words 'as' or 'like', the sentence would be a simile.

Because the sentence compares two things without 'as' or 'like', the sentence is a metaphor.

Ethyne is more commonly known under the trivial name acetylene. It is the simplest of the alkynes, consisting of two carbon atoms connected by a triple bond, leaving each carbon able to bond to one hydrogen atom. Since both carbon atoms are linear in shape, all four atoms lie in a straight line.
