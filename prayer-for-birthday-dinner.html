17. 1. We are making preparations for her birthday (last week, next week) 2. Mother prepared our favorite dish for dinner (today, tonight)​


Answer:

1. We are making preparations for her birthday (last week, next week).

The correct sentence is "we are making preparations for her birthday next week" because they will still prepare it for the upcoming birthday. Last week means it's done.

2. Mother prepared our favorite dish for dinner (today, tonight)

The correct sentence is "mother prepared our favorite dish for dinner tonight" because it is referring to the word "dinner" which is it only happens at night.
