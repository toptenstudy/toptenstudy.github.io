10. name of the chemical formulaHCI=____. SO²=____. CaF²=____. etong ganto ² nasa baba dapat yan hehepakisagot po ng maayos auto report po mga biro lang gawa​


Answer:

HCI-

hydrochloric acid

hydrogen chloride (HCl), a compound of the elements hydrogen and chlorine, a gas at room temperature and pressure. A solution of the gas in water is called hydrochloric acid.

SO²-

Sulfur dioxide

Chemical compound

Sulfur dioxide or sulphur dioxide is the chemical compound with the formula SO ₂. It is a toxic gas responsible for the smell of burnt matches. It is released naturally by volcanic activity and is produced as a by-product of copper extraction and the burning of sulfur-bearing fossil fuels.

Formula: SO₂

IUPAC ID: Sulfur dioxide

Molar mass: 64.066 g/mol

CaF²-

CALCIUM FLUORIDE

Calcium fluoride is used as component of electrolyte, fluxing agent for aluminum metallurgy. ... Calcium fluoride is a mineral composed of CaF2. It occurs as cubic, isometric crystals and cleavable masses. When pure, it is colorless and transparent, or translucent with a glassy luster.

Calcium fluoride is the inorganic compound of the elements calcium and fluorine with the formula CaF₂. It is a white insoluble solid. It occurs as the mineral fluorite, which is often deeply coloured owing to impurities.

Formula: CaF2

IUPAC ID: Calcium fluoride

Molar mass: 78.07 g/mol

Melting point: 1,418 °C

Boiling point: 2,533 °C

Solubility: insoluble in acetone; slightly soluble in acid

Density: 3.18 g/cm3
