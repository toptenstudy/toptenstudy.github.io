4. what is the organization of the philippines congress ?



The Philippine Congress is the organization of the national legislature of the Philippines.

It is the Bicameral body consisting of the Senate (upper chamber), and the House of Representatives (lower chamber), although commonly in the Philippines, the term congress refers to the latter (or the House of Representatives).

30. cite some accomplishments of Philippine Congress ​


1. The passage of the Philippine Reproductive Health Law in 2012, which provides universal access to contraception and reproductive health information.

2. The passage of the Magna Carta of Women in 2009, which protects the rights of Filipino women and provides them access to resources and protection from discrimination.

3. The passage of the Home Development Mutual Fund Law in 2009, which provides Filipino workers with access to safe housing loans.

4. The passage of the Expanded Senior Citizen’s Act in 2010, which provides elderly Filipinos with access to benefits such as a 20% discount on medical services and 20% discount on select goods and services.

5. The passage of the Anti-Child Labor Law in 2003, which provides protection to children from exploitation.

6. The passage of the Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act in 2010, which provides a comprehensive approach to disaster management and risk reduction.

7. The passage of the Data Privacy Act in 2012, which provides protection to personal data from unauthorized processing.

8. The passage of the Philippine Competition Act in 2015, which promotes fair and healthy competition in the market.
