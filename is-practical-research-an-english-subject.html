Answer:

The problem is to develop a method of learning English speaking skills based on the language functions used in the food and beverage service. This method should enable the learner to acquire the necessary language skills to communicate effectively in a professional food and beverage setting. The method should also provide guidance on how to use the language functions in a range of contexts and how to develop an understanding of the cultural aspects related to food and beverage service.
