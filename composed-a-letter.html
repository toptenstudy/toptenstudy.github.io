21. Consonant are composed of letters or sounds combined together


Answer:

When there are two consonants together and each makes its own sound, it's called a consonant blend. Consonant blends are more common than you think — you probably read and say hundreds of them a day!

Bl-blends: black, blow, blue

Pl-blends: plan, play, plug

Gl-blends: glad, glow, glue

Fl-blends: flat, flip, flute
