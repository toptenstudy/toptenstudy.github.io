5. What province is known for beautiful woven mats? a. Mindoro b. Romblon c. Marinduque d. Zambales​


KNOWN FOR BEAUTIFUL WOVEN MATS

Answer:

B. Romblon is known for its fine marble items as well as for the delightfully woven mats and sacks out of Romblon plants.

Individuals of Romblon are for the most part Visayans whose language is like Hiligaynon. Romblon and the Neighboring islands of Mindoro, Panay and Masbate have a long history of contact. Part of the Visayan populace in southern Mindoro and in southwestern Masbate can follow their starting point to the islands. Romblon is renowned for its customary weaving and basketry. Handiworks are a significant home industry where the ladies are locked in. The area of Romblon is noted, particularly, for its fine and fragile knit bands and quilts.

29. What province is known for marble craftmaking?a Marinduqueb. Mindoroc. Palawand. Romblon​


Answer:

D. Romblon

Explanation:

Romblon is an archipelagic province of the Philippines located in the Mimaropa region. Its main islands include Tablas, the largest, which covers nine municipalities, Sibuyan with its three towns, as well as the smaller island municipalities of Corcuera, Banton, Concepcion, San Jose, and Romblon, the provincial capital. The province lies south of Marinduque and Quezon, east of Oriental Mindoro, north of Aklan and Capiz, and west of Masbate. According to the 2015 census, it has a total population of 292,781.
