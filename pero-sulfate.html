23. The fertilizer ammonium sulfate, (NH4)2SO4, reacts with sodium hydroxide to produce sodium sulfate, water and ammonia. Determine the mass of sodium hydroxide needed to react with 15.0 g of ammonium sulfate.​


Solution:

Step 1: Write and balance the chemical equation.

(NH₄)₂SO₄ + 2NaOH → Na₂SO₄ + 2H₂O + 2NH₃

Step 2: Calculate the molar mass of substances needed.

For (NH₄)₂SO₄

molar mass = (14.01 g/mol × 2) + (1.008 g/mol × 8) + (32.07 g/mol × 1) + (16.00 g/mol × 4)

molar mass = 132.15 g/mol

For NaOH

molar mass = (22.99 g/mol × 1) + (16.00 g/mol × 1) + (1.008 g/mol × 1)

molar mass = 40.00 g/mol

Step 3: Determine the mole ratio needed.

mole ratio = 1 mol (NH₄)₂SO₄ : 2 mol NaOH

Step 4: Calculate the mass of NaOH needed by using the mole ratio.

[tex]\text{mass of NaOH = 15.0 g (NH₄)₂SO₄} × \frac{\text{1 mol (NH₄)₂SO₄}}{\text{132.15 g (NH₄)₂SO₄}} × \frac{\text{2 mol NaOH}}{\text{1 mol (NH₄)₂SO₄}} × \frac{\text{40.00 g NaOH}}{\text{1 mol NaOH}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{mass of NaOH = 9.08 g}}[/tex]

24. A 125g of sulfate are found in 1kg of water. what is the concentration of sulfate in ppm? with solution pls help


SOLVING FOR THE CONCENTRATION OF SULFATE IN PPM

If we solve the problem the concentration of in water is 125 ppm. The problem that  would like to solve is to look for  the concentration of sulfate in parts per million (ppm) in a solution of water.  

Solution

These are the given to  solve the problem using the following conversion:

(Convert grams to milligrams) 1 g = 1000 mg (to convert from grams to milligrams)

(Covert the kilograms to liters ) 1 kg = 1000 L

Here is a steps to calculate the concentration in mg/L as follows:

125 g sulfate / 1000 g water =  0.125 g sulfate/g water

0.125 g sulfate/g water x 1000 mg/g =  125 mg sulfate/kg water

125 mg sulfate/kg water / 1000 L/kg =  0.125 mg sulfate/L

To sum up, the concentration of sulfate in mg/L is 0.125.

In converting  to parts per million (ppm), we need to multiply by 1,000:

0.125 mg sulfate/L x 1000 = 125 ppm

So the concentration of sulfate in water is 125 ppm.
