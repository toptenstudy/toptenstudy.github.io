1. If the cost of all ingredients for 3 cakes is P 360. 00, how much does the ingredients per cake cost?

   3 cakes = P 360

   cost per cake = [tex]\frac{P 360}{3}[/tex]

                           = P 120

The cost of ingrediets per cake is P120.

2. If a cake was sold for P 220, how much is the profit for the three cakes?

   profit per cake = P220 - P 120

                             = P 100

   Profit for the 3 cakes = P 100 x 3

                                       = P 300

The profit for the three cakes will be P 300.

1. Identify the given quantity and the desired unit.

         Given quantity is 36,000

         Desired quantity : ___ g

2. Determine the conversion factor to be used.

   Ex. 1000 mg = 1 g

3. Express the conversion factor into fraction. The numerator should be the quantity of the desired unit and the denominator is the quantity of the given unit.

   Ex.  1000 mg = 1 g

           [tex]\frac{1 g}{1000 mg}[/tex]

4. Multiply the given quantity by the conversion factor to get the result.

A.

1. In a cake, 12,000 mg of cake flour is needed, that is equivalent to 12 grams. Therefore, the cake flour needed for three cakes is 36 grams, that is twelve grams per cake multiplied by 3 cakes.

2. Two cups of milk is equivalent to 500 ml of milk, since a cup is equal to 250 ml. Therefore, for three cakes the amount of milk should be 1500 ml.

That is 500 ml of milk per cake times 3 cakes, giving 1500 ml of milk for three cakes.

two cups per cake multiplied by three cakes is equal to 6 cups.

B.
