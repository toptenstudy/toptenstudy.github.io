23. This organism breaks a part of itself to reproduce


The organism that breaks a part of itself to reproduce is called a starfish or a sea star. This process is called "regeneration." When a starfish loses an arm or a portion of its central disk, it has the ability to regenerate the lost body part. In some species of starfish, the lost limb can even grow into a new, fully functional organism. This allows starfish to reproduce asexually, without the need for a mate. Regeneration is a unique ability that is not found in many other animals, and it has fascinated scientists and researchers for many years.
