10. Find the area of the tablet if it is 4x - 5 units long and 3x – 2 units wide.


Answer:

The area of a rectangle is given by the formula:

Area = length x width

Substituting the given values, we get:

Area = (4x - 5) x (3x - 2)

Expanding the product, we get:

Area = 12x^2 - 8x - 15x + 10

Simplifying, we get:

Area = 12x^2 - 23x + 10

Therefore, the area of the tablet is 12x^2 - 23x + 10 square units
