11. Get a 5 circular objects available around you.Use a ruler to find the diameter (in cm) of a circle.Use a string to finds it circumference.Pa help poh​


Answer:How do you find the diameter of a circle with a ruler?

Measure just the radius of the circle if it is very large. The radius is the distance from the center to any point on the circle. Multiply the radius by two to produce a measurement for the diameter.

How do I find the diameter from the circumference?

Divide the circumference by π, or 3.14 for an estimation.

And that's it; you have the circle's diameter.
