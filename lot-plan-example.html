United States of Mexico or Mexico (Spanish: Estados Unidos Mexicanos or México) is a country located in North America and is bordered by the United States, Guatemala and Belize to the southeast, the Pacific Ocean to the west and the Gulf of Mexico and the Caribbean Sea to the east.

Mexico's topography consists of mountain ranges, including the Sierra Madre Occidental to the west, the Sierra Madre Oriental to the east, the Cordillera Neovolcánica to the south, and the Sierra Madre del Sur to the south, lowlands mostly along the coast, and on the Yucatan Peninsula. Mexico's climate varies greatly in north-south extension, and varies at high altitudes. Most countries have two seasons: rainy (June-September) and hot (October-April). Generally low rainfall inland, and north. Rainfall is plentiful on the east coast, in the south, and on the Yucatan Peninsula.
