22. what is the effects of social media usage to the academic performance of grade 12 humss students?​


Answer:

Studies have shown that excessive use of social media can have negative effects on the academic performance of students. It can lead to distractions, procrastination, reduced study time, and decreased motivation. Additionally, social media can also negatively impact sleep, mental health, and overall well-being, all of which can indirectly affect academic performance. However, it is important to note that the impact of social media usage can vary among individuals and is also dependent on the type and frequency of usage.
