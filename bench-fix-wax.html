11. circular benches were constructed around each of the 4 trees in the school ground. the diameter of each bench is 1.5 m. what area of the school ground is occupied by the benches​


Answer:

7.065 m

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

d = 1.5 m

4 trees

Solutions:

r = d ÷ 2

r = 1.5 ÷ 2

r = 0.75 m

A = πr²

π = 3.14

A = (3.14) (0.75)²

A = 1.76625 m²

Total area = 1.76625 x 4

Total area = 7.065 m
