Each of the following questions is independent of the others. Allen and Daniel agree that some of the inventory is obsolete. The inventory account is decreased before David is admitted. David invests P40,000 for a 1/5 interest. What is the amount of inventory written down? a. P4,000 b. P10,000 c. P15,000 d. P20,000


(Accounting for Partnership & Corporation)

Total Equity, Beg = 180,000

*140,000+40,000

Total Equity, End = 40,000 x 5

= 200,000

*During

=200,000- 40,000 (Total equity,end less David investment)

= 160,000 (The partnerships Capital before David investment)

= 180,000 - 160,000

= 20,000
