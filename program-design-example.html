3 Examples of Information and its Data

1. Weather Data - Information about the weather conditions such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation can be collected and stored as data. This data can be analyzed to make predictions about future weather patterns, inform decision-making, and inform the public about current and expected weather conditions.

2. Customer Data - Information about customers, such as their names, addresses, purchase history, and preferences, can be collected and stored as data. This data can be used to personalize marketing campaigns, improve customer service, and better understand customer behavior and preferences.

3. Sales Data - Information about sales transactions, such as the products sold, the date of sale, and the amount of the sale, can be collected and stored as data. This data can be used to track sales trends, identify best-selling products, and make informed decisions about inventory management and pricing strategies.
