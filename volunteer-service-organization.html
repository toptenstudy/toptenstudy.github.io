7. Why is the National Voluntary Blood Services Program or NVSB of the Department of Health targeting the youth as volunteers in it's Blood Donation Program?​


Answer:

The National Voluntary Blood Services Program of the Department of Health is targeting the youth as volunteers in its Blood Donation Program for several reasons:

The youth are generally healthy and fit, making them suitable donors for blood.

The youth are often more open-minded and willing to take part in voluntary programs, such as blood donation.

Engaging the youth in blood donation can help raise awareness about the importance of regular blood donation and encourage them to continue donating blood in the future.

The youth are the future of the country, and engaging them in voluntary programs can help develop a culture of volunteerism and social responsibility among the younger generation.

The NVSB program aims to increase the number of blood donors in the country, and targeting the youth can help achieve this goal by providing a new pool of potential donors.

19. where can we volunteer to share our service and time for different communities? ​


Answer:

There are many places where you can volunteer your time and service to help communities in need. Here are a few options to consider:

1. Local non-profit organizations - Many non-profits, such as schools, hospitals, and charities, rely on volunteers to help with a variety of tasks, including fundraising, event planning, and direct service to those in need.

2. Community centers - Community centers often have programs and activities that rely on volunteers to assist with planning and execution.

3. Religious organizations - Many religious organizations, such as churches, synagogues, and mosques, have volunteer programs that allow members to get involved in their communities.

4. Government agencies - Federal, state, and local government agencies often have volunteer programs that allow individuals to get involved in their communities and contribute to important causes.

5. International organizations - If you are interested in volunteering abroad, there are many international organizations that offer opportunities to work with communities in other countries.

By volunteering your time and service, you can make a difference in the lives of others and contribute to the well-being of your community.
