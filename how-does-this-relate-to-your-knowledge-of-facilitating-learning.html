The child's development.

The child's learning capability mainly depends on the child's development. As studies say, the IQ of a child highly depends on its childhood development. Observing a healthy lifestyle for the kid may improve the child's ability to learn.

Some of the things that may improve the child's development in relation to a child's learning ability are the following:

Play -Free play promotes better learning, memory, and growth of the cerebral cortex. It also enhances the development of language, spatial intelligence, counterfactual reasoning, and mathematical skills.Gestures - There's also good evidence that gesturing with your hands improves your ability to remember and learn.Adequate sleep and efficient nutrition

Here are five areas in child development:Cognitive development. This includes the ability to think, learn, understand, solve problems, and remember.Language development. How does your little one learn to recognize sounds, words and sentences, using sign language and body to communicate with others. This also includes how they learn to understand the communications of others.Physical development. How children learn to move and use their muscles. These can be separated into large motor skills (such as using muscles to stand, walk, run) and small motor skills (such as using muscles to eat, draw, and write).Social and emotional development. Social development is how children learn to develop relationships and work together with family members, friends, and teachers. Meanwhile, emotional development includes how the Little One learns to express things, understand, and handle their emotions.
