1. In a short paragraph, define mercenaries and Private Military Companies (PMC). Summarize today's use of mercenaries and Private Military Companies.


Answer:

Mercenaries are individuals hired to fight in a foreign conflict, often for financial gain. Private Military Companies (PMCs) are businesses that provide military services to governments, corporations, and other organizations. Today, mercenaries and PMCs are used more frequently as a more cost-effective alternative to traditional military forces in conflicts around the world. PMCs are often used for security, training, and intelligence services.
