✎ Questions:

1. It can be done when a force produces a motion.

A. WorkB. EnergyC. PowerD. Potential Energy

2. It refers to the capacity of doing work.

A. WorkB. EnergyC. PowerD. Kinetic Energy

3. It is defined as the rate of doing work and a rate at which energy is consumed or utilized

A. WorkB. EnergyC. PowerD. Kinetic and Potential Energy

4. It is the capacity of doing work developed in a body due to its position or configuration.

A. Kinetic EnergyB. Potential EnergyC. PowerD. Energy

5. It refers to the energy of a body due to it's motion.

A. Kinetic EnergyB. Potential EnergyC. PowerD. Energy✎ Answers and explanation:

1. The correct answer is letter A.

✔︎ Work is done when a force acting upon an object moves a distance in the direction where the force is directed to.

2. The correct answer is letter B.

✔︎ Energy is the capacity to do work. There are many forms of energy, but all are expressed in joules.

3. The correct answer is letter C.

✔︎ Power is the amount of work done or energy transferred in a certain period of time.

4. The correct answer is letter B.

✔︎ Potential Energy is stored energy. This is the energy of an object if it has been moved to a position from which it can do work when released.

5. The correct answer is letter A.

✔︎ Kinetic Energy is energy in motion. This is the energy possessed by a moving object by virtue of its mass and velocity.
