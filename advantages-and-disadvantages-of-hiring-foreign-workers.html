15. Write an opinion if you are in favor of lowering the minimum capital requirements of foreign investors. Why" What are the advantages or disadvantages of this move?


Answer:

I am in favor of this move because I believe that it will bring more jobs and investment into our country.

Lowering the minimum capital requirements will make it easier for foreign investors to set up shop in the Philippines. This will create more jobs, as businesses will need people to work in their factories and offices. It will also lead to more investment, as foreigners will be more likely to put money into a country when it is easy for them to do so. This could help us grow our economy and reduce poverty.

However, there are some potential disadvantages of lowering the minimum capital requirements. First, if we make it too easy for foreigners to invest here, they may take away jobs from Filipinos. Second, we may not get as much investment as we hope if the process is too simple and streamlined; investors may prefer countries with less red tape involved in investing there. Finally, without proper regulations governing how much foreign ownership is allowed in certain industries, we could end up giving away too many control over our economy's key sectors

22. An engineer hired 150 workers to pave a road in 25 days. How many workers would be hired to do the same work in 30 days?


Inverse Proportion

Answer

125 workers should be hired to do the same work in 30 days.

Step-by-step explanation:

Inversely Proportional Problems

Proportion shows the relationship between two ratios or fractions. In real life, there are situations that involve inverse proportion. It means that as one quantity increases, the other decreases. One example is the number of workers required to finish a task. As the number of days required to finish the task increases, the number of workers needed decreases. In this example, the two quantities, the number of days (a) and the number of workers required (b), are related in such a way that when one quantity increases, the other decreases. Below are the steps to solve:

1. Write down the proportional relationship

2. Write the equation using the proportional constant

3. Find the value of the constant using the given values

4. Substitute the value of the constant in the equation.

Ex.

Number of workers in 25 days:

150 workers x 25 = 3750

Number of workers in 30 days:

150 x 25 = 30n

3750 = 30n

[tex]\frac{3750}{30}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{30n}{30}[/tex]

125 = n
