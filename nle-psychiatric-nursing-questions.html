QUESTION

A nurse is communicating with a client on an in-patient psychiatric unit. The client moves closer and invades the nurse's personal space, making the nurse uncomfortable. which is an appropriate nursing intervention

ANSWER

Your question is incomplete, and you should've included the given choices that refer to "which is an appropriate nursing intervention."

However, I'll answer your question based on what I've learned and what I think is the best nursing intervention.

As a nurse, if the patient makes me feel uncomfortable and invades my personal space, I will immediately tell my patient calmly and therapeutically that their actions make me uncomfortable. Simply moving away from the patient won't help since they may perceive that you're trying to avoid them.

Remember that the nurse's safety is also a priority. It is valid for them to express their feeling of discomfort and to set boundaries with their patient. With that, the patient will be aware of their limitations and limit their inappropriate behaviors.

21. Practical Research 1 po itoPragmatism Rules: The Intervention and Prevention Strategies used by Psychiatric NursesWorking With Non-suicidal Self-harming IndividualsPredictor Variable: __________Criterion Variable: __________​


Answer:

Predictor Variable: The intervention and prevention strategies used by psychiatric nurses when working with non-suicidal self-harming individuals can be considered the predictor variable. This variable includes the various techniques and approaches that the psychiatric nurses use to prevent and intervene in self-harm behaviors, such as counseling, psychotherapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy, and dialectical behavior therapy.

Criterion Variable: The criterion variable in this case would be the reduction in non-suicidal self-harm behaviors among the individuals being worked with by the psychiatric nurses. This variable measures the effectiveness of the intervention and prevention strategies used by psychiatric nurses in reducing non-suicidal self-harm behaviors among their clients.
