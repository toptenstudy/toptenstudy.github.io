10. What is the main purpose of train law, advantages and disadvantages of this law in philippines?


The pros it to lower the tax from workers while on the other hand it affect the price of basic needs of the filipinos.  Higher price of food products. The cost for everyday life is burdened Filipino's more.  

The main purpose of TRAIN law is to increase taxes on the commodities while lessening the taxes being paid by the workers. It makes the tax system easier and more efficient.  

The advantages of this are: (1) workers bring home more money than the before because the tax is based on the salary you are getting and (2) there are enough funds for the government’s projects.

The disadvantages of this are: (1) prices of the commodities may continue to rise, thus, the inflation rate too and (2) the price hikes of commodities may lead to the further rising levels and percentages of poverty and unemployment.
