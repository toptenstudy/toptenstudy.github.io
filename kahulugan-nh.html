6. Kahulugan nh globalisasyon?


Answer:

Globalization is a phenomenon

- Globalization is an event happening everyday whenever and wherever

Globalization is a process

- Globalization is not something happen in a zap it has a process to follow

Globalization has drivers

- Drivers are the reasons why globalization takes place it can be people or events etc.

Globalization involves the collapsing of the borders

- Not necessarily borderlines between countries but breaking down figurative walls of ideologies that divide individuals among nations and government.

Globalization involves the shrinking of the world

- Not the planet get smaller but the communication/interaction of individuals, nations or governments have ease in globalize world.

Globalization is geographic

- Some places on earth doesn't experience globalization and some of it do.

Globalization is inevitable

- Globalization spreads from one place to another, it cannot be avoided.

17. ano ang kahulugan nh woodwing at brasswind???


Explanation:

Wood wind instruments

any group of wind instrumentsCharacterized by a cylindrical or conical cube of wood or metal usually ending in a slightly flared bell, that produce tones by the vibration of one or two reeds in the mouthpiece or by the passing of air over a mouth hole.Examples of wood wind instrumentsclarinet, flute, oboe or saxophone.

Brass wind instrument

is a musical instrument that produces sound by sympathetic vibration of air in a tubular resonator in sympathy with the vibration of the player's lips.It is also called labrosones , literally means lip-vibrated instruments.

Four main brass wind instruments

Tuba - is the lowest pitched musical instrument in the brass family.Trumpet - was created in around 1500 B.C , highest pitched musical instrument in the brass family.Used in classical and jazz ensembles.Trombone - Originally knows as Sackbut in the 1700s ,second place in order of pitch.French Horn- is a brass instrument made of tubing wrapped into a coil with a flarred bell.
