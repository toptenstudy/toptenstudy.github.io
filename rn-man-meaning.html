- a word used to connect clauses or sentences or to coordinate words in the same clause

Find next number in the sequence 90,80,90,50,85,95,?

Solution:

The given series is 90,80,90,50,85,95,?

Step by step solution of the sequence is

The given sequence 90,80,90,50,85,95 is a combination of 2 series

I. 90,90,85

II. 80,50,95

Next possible number in the given sequence I. 90,90,85 is 75

Next possible number in the given sequence II. 80,50,95 is 137.5

∴ The next number for given series 90,80,90,50,85,95 is 75
