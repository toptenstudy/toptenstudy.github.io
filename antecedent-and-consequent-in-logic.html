2. antecedent and consequent phrase​


Answer:

In a period, the phrase ending with the less conclusive cadence is called the “ antecedent ” and the phrase ending with the more conclusive cadence is called the “ consequent .” 


3. antecedent and consequent phrase twinkle​


Answer:

There is an antecedent and a consequent to each musical phrase. In Twinkle, Twinkle the first antecedent is “Twinkle, twinkle little star”
