8. "Ang Gatas na maraming PLUS! Tulong para may LABAN NG KATAWAN + Wyeth Nutrition Bonakid Pre-School 3+ Powdered Milk Drink For kids over 3 years old H 350 g Per 129 SP IMMUNITY GROWTH ENERGY SUPPORT NUTRIENTS" what types of propaganda techniques is this tagline?​


This tagline uses the following propaganda techniques:

Plain Folks: The use of the phrase "Tulong para may LABAN NG KATAWAN" suggests that the product is designed to help ordinary people fight against physical challenges and promote a healthy lifestyle.Bandwagon: The mention of "Wyeth Nutrition Bonakid Pre-School 3+" implies that many other parents are already using the product, and it is popular among children over 3 years old.Glittering Generalities: The tagline highlights several positive benefits of the product, including "IMMUNITY GROWTH ENERGY SUPPORT NUTRIENTS." These terms are vague but have a positive connotation, which could persuade people to try the product.

Overall, this tagline uses a combination of different propaganda techniques to appeal to the audience's emotions and persuade them to purchase the product.
