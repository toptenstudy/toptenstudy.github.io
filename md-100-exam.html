6. WHAT IS 10 DIVIDED BY =J4/D4*100 ITS FOR MY EXAM!


It's not possible to determine the value of 10 divided by =J4/D4*100 without knowing the values of cells J4 and D4.

The formula =J4/D4*100 is a mathematical formula that divides the value in cell J4 by the value in cell D4 and then multiplies the result by 100. The result of this formula is a percentage that represents the ratio of the value in cell J4 to the value in cell D4.

Assuming cell J4 contains the value 20 and cell D4 contains the value 5, the formula =J4/D4*100 would calculate the result as follows:

=20/5*100

=4*100

=400

Therefore, if J4 equals 20 and D4 equals 5, then 10 divided by =J4/D4*100 would equal 10 divided by 400, which is equal to 0.025.
