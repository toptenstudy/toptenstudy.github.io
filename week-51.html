Answer:

51 ÷ 3 = 17

-51 ÷ 3 = -17

51 ÷ -3 = -17

-51 ÷ -3 = 17

0 ÷ 51 = 0

Step-by-step explanation:

Every numbers that have different signs and divided together, the answer is always in negative. And if the numbers have the same signs and divided to each other, the answer will be always positive.

And any numbers divided to zero, the answer will always be zero.
