3. what are the basic standard requirements for restaurant​


Answer:

What are the basic standard requirements for restaurant​

A restaurant is a place where food are prepared and served to customers. It is a cozy and ambient place where people can enjoy their time being together while enjoying a table of mouthwatering foods and delicacies. Food service is one of the most promising business investments today because food is one of the necessities in life. Proper gathering of information and planning will help investors to gain profit over a period of time and may be able to make a name for its business.

What are the pre-requisites of setting up a restaurantFunds - it is the determining factor when setting up a business. Your fund will tell you how far can you go with it and how well you can work with it. Franchising is one of the current trends in food service for it offers a lot of convenience and assurance of return of investment. Most franchisee offers their brand, which is currently patronized and enjoyed by many, so that the risk of making a new name for you own restaurant is disregarded. Also, together with it are the construction plans and designs needed for the franchise so it will be more convenient for the franchiser because the company will shoulder the construction expenses. Still, if you want to venture and make a name for yourself and your business, you may do so but be sure to secure enough fund to cover the construction expenses and others.Location - since you are about to set up a business, a handful of information might be necessary in determining what kind of restaurant that may set up. Some of the considerations are, the people living/working in the place, their buying/eating habits, their schedules, their financial capacities, even the existing businesses around will help you identify what kind of restaurant is feasible in the place. Service style - this focuses on how you will deliver your food to your customers. Fast food restaurants are one of the emerging choices in food industry because of its availability and efficiency. Customers make orders and after a few minutes, they can grab their order and proceed to their destinations, though they can still have their orders and eat inside the restaurants. Mid scale restaurants offer a bit different process of serving their food. A crew will take their orders and deliver it to their table while waiting. The most sophisticated is the fine-dining restaurants that are usually seen in hotels and the like. They focus on the ambiance of the place and the food service and quality is top class. Food concept - it is the choice of food that you are about to offer and the one that you need to consider is your target customers. Remember that some may enjoy the food while others do not because of their preferences. With this in mind, you may still push for your choice of food concept. A list of currently patronized food concepts are: fast foods, family-style restaurants, casual dining, coffee/tea houses, pizzeria, bakeries and pastries, ethnic-style, steakhouses, and more. Your choice of food concept is very crucial because the patronage of your target customers will make or break your business's lifespan but with proper information gathering, your business will sure be able to grow.
