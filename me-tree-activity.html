29. 4. The class of Mrs. Velasquez is going to have a tree planting activity. Initially, there werex students who volunteered to join the activity. After a week, the number of thosejoining doubled. However, because another option for an activity opened, fifteenpeople backed out. The final count of those joining the activity was 43.a. Write an equation needed to find the number of students who initially signed upfor the tree planting activity.b. Use the equation in a) to solve for x or the number of students who initiallysigned up for the tree planting activity.( help me)​


Answer:

x = 29

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex](x \times 2) - 15 = 43[/tex]

or

[tex](29 \times 2) - 15 = 43[/tex]

The clue word is double so multiply the x by 2. Then subtract it by 15 people who backed out. The total was 43, therefore you need to add 43 by 15 and the answer is 58. Then divide 58 by 2 and the answer is 29 which is the x. We found the x so let's do the equation above.

Solving the equation:

[tex](29 \times 2) - 15 = 43[/tex]

[tex]58 - 15 = 43[/tex]

[tex] = 43[/tex]
