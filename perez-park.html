26. Who is Rodolfo Paras-Perez?


Rodolfo Paras Perez is a Filipino Asian Modern & Contemporary artist who was born in 1934. He is a painter, printmaker, and art critic and art historian.He graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from the University of the Philippines, MA from the University of Minnesota, and PhD in Art History from Harvard University. He held his first solo exhibit at the Luz Gallery in 1962 and international printmaking exhibitions in Geneva, Cracow, and Florence. Among his awards from the AAP Annual Competitions were: First Prizes for “The Kiss” in 1962 and “Ethereal Glow in 1963. He did a series of woodcut prints illustrating Francisco Baltazar’s “Florante at Laura.” He won the Gold Medal at the II Internazional della Grafica in 1969. Among the scholarly books and publications he has written are those on Dominator Castañeda, Galo B. Campo, Vicente Manansala, Sanso, Romeo Tabuena, Fernando Zobel, Guillemo Tolentino and Edades and the Thirteen Moderns. Rodolfo Paras-Perez's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from $253 USD to $3,230 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2013 the record price for this artist at auction is $3,230 USD for Florante at Laura, sold at León Gallery, Makati in 2015.

28. Art period of fish vendors by sey perez


Answer:

Title of the Contemporary Artwork: FISH VENDORS

Artist: SEY PEREZ

Region: Region III PAMPANGA, PHILIPPINES

1. What is artwork all about? Pictured are two fishermen and a farmer who show that they live on a daily basis and what we usually eat as Filipinos which is from agricultural like fish.

2. What does the artist want to convey to his/her audience? He wants to convey to us that the Philippines is a rich country with natural resources and at the present time it seems that our Government is no longer paying enough attention to the Agricultural sector where we prefer to just import to other countries. But we can keep up with the field of Agriculture, only support for the Gov’t and adequate funding is really a big help so that we can further enrich our own resources.

3. What evidence is present in the artwork for you to consider it as a contemporary Philippine art? Until now, farming and fishing (fish vendors) are one of the common livelihoods that we Filipinos have. It is part of our cultural identity as a Filipino living on a rich island. We can say that it is a contemporary art because it is an identification of a culture that still lives as part of everyday living.

30. Sino si Luis Perez Dasmariñas


Answer:

Noong Disyembre 3, 1593, si Luis Perez Dasmariñas ay naging gobernador ng pangkalahatang ad interim sa pamamagitan ng pag-atas ng kanyang amang si Gomez Perez Dasmariñas. Siya ay kabalyero ng Order of Alcantara.

Explanation:

Sa kanyang pagiging matatag, ang Maynila ay napabuti, ang mga bagong gusali ay naitayo, at ang Manila Confraternity ng La Misericordia, tahanan ng mga ulila at distitute, ay itinatag. Noong 1595, sa ilalim ng Luis Perez, Cagayan, Viscaya at Isabela ay nasakop. Dumating si Doctor Antonio de Morga sa taong ito noong Hunyo 11, na hinirang na gobernador ng tenyente ayon sa isang maharlikang cedula na namamahala sa mga espirituwal na gawain. Tulad ng kanyang ama, inutusan niya ang pagpapadala ng isang bagong ekspedisyon sa Mindanao sa ilalim ng utos ng Marquis Esteban Rodríguez de Figueroa (1595) upang maipangaral ang mga lupain na ito ng karamihan sa populasyon ng mga Muslim, isang layunin na nagpatunay na napakahirap sa poot ng ang mga lokal na sultans; ang sariling Figueroa ay pinatay sa kurso ng isang tugma. Personal din na pinangunahan ni Dasmarinas ang isang ekspedisyon upang isumite sa soberanya ng Espanya ang bulubunduking mga rehiyon ng Hilagang Luzon, at isakatuparan ang paniniwala ng Katoliko sa mga tao, ang misyon na ipinagkatiwala nina Diego de Soria at Tomás Castellar sa mga Dominikanong prayle.
