What is the APA format?

APA stands for American Psychological Association.It is used as a standard format for citations in almost every social science-related field research.This format is created to create standard scientific writing.This aids the authors of research to be free from plagiarism and avoid severe consequences if they ever accidentally plagiarize something from a book, article, or any other form of literature.If it concerns the name of the author and the date the literature was published, we have three ways in APA in writing it.Parenthetical citation, narrative citation, and reference list entry are the only APA formats for author-date-related citations.Parenthetical Citation: (Ebuetada, 2017) Narrative Citation: Ebuetada (2017) Reference List Entry: According to Ebuetada (2017)

What are other forms of citations?

We have several citation styles but here are three common styles: APA, MLA, and Chicago/Turabian style.MLA stands for Modern Language Association. This format is often used by people researching Humanities.Chicago/Turabian is commonly used by business-related people, historians, and artists.

26. Consider a Problem: In 2017, Venus’ birthday fell on Saturday, June 3. On what day of the week does Venus birthday fall in 2020? Note that the year 2020 is a leap year​


Wednesday

June 3, 2017 to June 3, 2018 = 365 days = 1 mod 7

June 3, 2018 to June 3, 2019 = 365 days = 1 mod 7

June 3, 2019 to June 3, 2020 = 366 days (due to extra day in february) = 2 mod 7

In effect, June 3, 2020 is 1+1+2=4 days of the week after saturday which is wednesday
