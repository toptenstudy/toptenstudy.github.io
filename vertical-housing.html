25. A 3.5 m. ladder is leaning against the wall of a house at a point just beneath one of its windows. If the ladder was placed 1.5 m. away from the base of the house, what is the vertical distance of the window from the ground?​


Answer:

The vertical distance of the window from the ground is approximately 3.16m

Step-by-step explanation:

Note: See attached image for illustration.

We can use the Pythagorean theorem to find the missing side.

Formula: [tex]a^{2} +b^{2} =c^{2}[/tex]

First, let’s assign values to a, b, and c.

Let a = be the missing side

     b = be the base

     c = be the size of the ladder (hypothenuse)

Therefore;

     a =?

     b = 1.5m

     c = 3.5m

Substitute:

  =   [tex]a^{2} +b^{2} =c^{2}[/tex] 

  =   [tex]a^{2} +(1.5)^{2} =(3.5)^{2}[/tex]

  =    [tex]a^{2} +2.25=12.25[/tex]

  =    [tex]a^{2} =12.25-2.25[/tex]

  =    [tex]a^{2} =10[/tex]  ---- Get the square root of both sides

We will have:

   a = 3.16m
