5. one of the acute angles of a right triangle measures 25Â°,the other acute angle measures?


A right triangle is a triangle with one right angle. The sum of the interior angles of a triangle is 180°.

To look for the missing angle, we will subtract the sum of the given angles from 180. Given that [tex]x[/tex] is the missing angle:

Equation:

[tex]90 + x + 25 =180[/tex]

[tex]x = 180 - 25 - 90[/tex]

[tex]x = 65[/tex]

Answer: 65°

Another easier way to solve this is to simply subtract the other given angles (not the 90°) by 90.

[tex]x = 90-25[/tex]

[tex]x = 65[/tex]
