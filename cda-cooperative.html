9. In parallelogram ABCD, the angle ABC is equal to 3x+50 and the angle BCD is equal to x+90. What is the angle CDA?​


here po ung sagot ko

Answer:

m∠CDA = 80°

Step-by-step explanation:

∠ABC and ∠BCD are consecutive angles and supplementary. The sum of their measures is 180°.

Given:

∠ABC = 3x + 50∠BCD = x + 90

Equation:

3x + 50 + x + 90 = 180

4x + 140 = 180

4x = 180 - 140

4x/4 = 40/4

x = 10

The measure of each consecutive angle:

m∠ABC = 3(10) + 50 = 80°m∠BCD = (10) + 90 = 100°

Find m∠CDA:

∠CDA = ∠ABC  ⇒  Opposite angles are congruent.

ANSWER: m∠CDA = 80°
