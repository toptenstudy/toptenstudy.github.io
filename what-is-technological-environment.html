6. what are the effects of nuclear technology to the environment?​


Answer:

The effects of nuclear technology on the environment can be both positive and negative. On the positive side, nuclear energy has the potential to provide a clean, safe and renewable source of electricity. It also has the potential to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, which are linked to global warming. On the negative side, nuclear technology can have serious environmental consequences, such as the release of radioactive materials into the environment and the potential for nuclear accidents. Additionally, the mining and processing of uranium, which is used to fuel nuclear reactors, can cause significant environmental damage.
