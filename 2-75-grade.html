30. The ratio of boys to girls in the sixth grade is 2 : 3. c) f there are 75 students, how many more girls are there than boys?


Answer:

15 more girls than boys

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio and Proportion

ratio of boys to girls = 2 : 3, total number of students is 75

2N + 3N = 75

5N = 75

N = 75 / 5

N = 15

boys = 2N = 2 ( 15 ) = 30 boy student

girls = 3N = 3 ( 15 ) = 45 girl student

45 - 30 = 15, 15 more girls than boys
