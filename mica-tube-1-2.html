23. Chin is 2 years older than Mica. In 9 years the sum of their ages will be 30.


˚✦₊FINDING AGE

==================================

• PROBLEM:

◆ Chin is 2 years older than Mica. In 9 years the sum of their ages will be 30.

ANSWER: Mica's age is 5 years old, while, Chin's age is 7 years old.

————————————————-—

• SOLUTION:

╰➢ Since Chin is 2 years older than Mica and the sum of their ages is 30 in the next 9 years. Let x will be the representer of Mica's age.

Hence;

Mica's age = x

Chin's age = x + 2

❖ The sum of their Ages in the next 9 years is 30.

Mica's age + Chin's age = 30

❖ Find the current age of Mica;

x + (x + 2) = 30

x + (x + 2) = 30

2x + 2 = 30

2x = 30 - 2

2x = 28

2x / 2 = 28 / 2

x = 14 — Mica's age in the next 9 years

❖ Now, to find Chin's age in the next 9 years, just add the 2 years to Mica's age in the next 9 years.

Hence;

14 + 2 = 16 — Chin's age in the next 9 years.

❖ Since we are just finding the Mica's and Chin's current age, we will subtract 9 to their age in the next 9 years.

14 - 9 = 5 — Mica's current age

16 - 9 = 7 — Chin's current age.

Checking:

Mica's age in the next 9 years + Chin's age in the next 9 years = 30

14 + 16 = 30

∴ Mica's age is 5 years old and Chin's age is 7 years old.

27. Chin is 2 years older than Mica. In 9 years the sum of their ages will be 30.


PROBLEM:

Chin is 2 years older than Mica. In 9 years the sum of their ages will be 30.

ANSWER:

Let's write the statement in mathematical expression and equation.

This year, let the age of Mica be [tex]\sf x[/tex]. Therefore, Chin is [tex]\sf x + 2[/tex] years old.

In 9 years, the sum of their ages will be 30. That means we need to add both nine to both of their ages then equate it to 30.

Mica will be [tex]\sf x + 9[/tex] years old in 9 years while Chin will be [tex]\sf x + 2 + 9 [/tex]years old. The equation will be [tex]\sf( x + 9 ) + ( x + 2 + 9 ) = 30[/tex].

Let's solve for the value of x.

[tex] \sf ( x + 9 ) + ( x + 2 + 9 ) = 30[/tex]

[tex] \sf 2x + 9 + 2 + 9 = 30[/tex]

[tex] \sf 2x + 20 = 30[/tex]

[tex] \sf 2x = 30 - 20[/tex]

[tex] \sf 2x = 10[/tex]

[tex] \sf x = 5[/tex]

If x is equal to 5, then their ages are:

THIS YEAR:

Mica is 5 years old.Chin is 5 + 2 = 7 years old.

IN NINE YEARS:

Mica will be 5 + 9 = 14 years old.Chin is 5 + 2 + 9 = 16 years old.To check, 14 + 16 is 30.
