6. What is the process of delivery and storage in online business?


Delivery in online business typically involves the transportation of goods from the business's warehouse or storage location to the customer's desired address. The process of delivery can involve various shipping carriers, such as FedEx, UPS, or the postal service, and may involve different shipping options, such as ground, expedited, or overnight shipping.

Storage in online business refers to the holding of goods in a warehouse or storage facility until they are ready to be sold or shipped to customers. To ensure efficient storage, many businesses use a warehouse management system (WMS) to track inventory levels and manage the flow of goods in and out of their storage facilities. Good storage practices, such as proper labeling and organization, can also help businesses maintain accurate inventory records and minimize the risk of loss or damage to goods.
