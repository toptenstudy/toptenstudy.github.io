14. — “Paano nagkaroon ng pope?”— “at bakit naging tirahan ng pope ang Vatican City?”​


Answer:

Ang Pope ay isang pinuno ng Simbahang Katoliko at kinikilala bilang successor ni San Pedro, na itinuturing ng mga Katoliko bilang unang Pope at pinakamalapit na alagad ni Jesus. Sa pamamagitan ng panahon, ang tungkulin at kapangyarihan ng Pope ay tumutubo at naging isang malaking bahagi ng pananampalataya at kultura ng mga Katoliko sa buong mundo.

Ang Vatican City ay isang independent na siyudad-estado sa gitna ng Rome, Italya. Ito ay naging tirahan ng Pope at ng Simbahang Katoliko mula noong 1870. Bago ito, ang Pope ay nanirahan sa iba't ibang lugar sa Rome, kabilang ang Castel Gandolfo, na isang palasyo sa labas ng lungsod. Ngunit dahil sa mga pangangailangan para sa isang mas malaking at mas ligtas na tirahan, ang Vatican City ay itinatag bilang isang independent na siyudad-estado upang masiguro ang seguridad at kalayaan ng Pope at ng Simbahang Katoliko. Ngayon, ito ay tumatanggap ng mga bisita mula sa buong mundo at kinikilala bilang isang importanteng sentro ng pananampalataya at kultura sa mundo.

22. write a descriptive paragraph about pope john paul II and pope francis.


Answer:

Pope John Paul II was the head of the Catholic Church from 1978 until his death in 2005. He was born Karol Józef Wojtyła in Poland and was the first non-Italian pope in more than 450 years. Pope John Paul II was known for his extensive travels, having visited over 120 countries throughout his papacy. He was a strong advocate for human rights, including the rights of workers, women, and young people, and he was instrumental in the fall of communism in Eastern Europe. He was also known for his writings, including his encyclicals, which dealt with social and spiritual issues.

Pope Francis, born Jorge Mario Bergoglio, became the head of the Catholic Church in 2013, succeeding Pope Benedict XVI. He is the first pope from the Americas and is known for his focus on social justice issues, particularly poverty and inequality. Pope Francis is known for his simplicity and humility, often choosing to live a modest lifestyle and advocating for the poor and marginalized. He has also been an advocate for environmental protection, and has called for action to address climate change. He has taken a more progressive stance on issues such as homosexuality, divorce, and contraception, and has encouraged the Catholic Church to focus more on mercy and compassion.
