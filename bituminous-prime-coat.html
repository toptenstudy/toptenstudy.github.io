7. A store has a 25% off sale on coats. With this discount, the price of one coat is $34.50. What is the original price of the coat?


Original Price: x
New Price after deducting the discount of 25%:  34.50
25% = 0.25

34.50 = x - 0.25x

0.75x = 34.50

x = 34.50/0.75

x = 46.00

ANSWER: The original price is 46.00.

Check:
New Price = Original Price - Rate (original Price)
34.50 = 46.00 - 0.25 (46.00)
34.50 = 46.00 - 11.50
34.50 = 34.50

Amount of Discount = Rate × Original Price
Amount of discount = 0.25 × 46.00
Amount of discount = 11.50

New Price = Original Price - Amount of discount
34.50 = 46.00 - 11.50
34.50 = 34.50

11. Which of the following refers to a name of a dull black coal with 70% carbon?a. Bituminousb. Anthracitec. Lignited. Peat​


Answer: C. Lignite

Explanation: Lignite is the lowest rank of coal. It is a peat that has been transformed into a rock, and that rock is a brown-black coal. It has a carbon content of between 60 and 70% on a dry ash-free basis.
