Steve Jobs: The most important thing is to stay focused and believe in yourself. Don't be afraid to take risks and don't be afraid to fail. Success comes from learning from your mistakes.

Host: Thanks for that great advice, Steve.

Finally, we have celebrity chef Emeril Lagasse. Emeril, what's the best way to become a successful chef?

Emeril Lagasse: The best way to become a successful chef is to practice, practice, practice. You have to be passionate about food and cooking and never stop learning. Stay up to date on the latest trends and techniques, and always be willing to experiment with new flavors and ingredients.

30. Give an example of positive scripting


Answer:

Instead of…

“I’m afraid that isn’t included in your policy…”

Say…“Your policy doesn’t cover that, but I can check if we can offer that to you in a different package.”

Do say:

Hi, my name is X. May I ask your name?

Great to meet you, Y. How can I assist you today?

How can I help you?

What can I help you with today?

Don’t say:

What is wrong?

Are you unsatisfied?

Are you confused?

What’s your problem?
