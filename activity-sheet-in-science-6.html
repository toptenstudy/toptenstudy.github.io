What is the relationship between latitude of an area and the amount of energy the area received?6. Give atleast four reasons why reasons change.​


Answer:

1.)How do you relate the tilt of the Earth and the length of daytime?

*THE TILT OF THE EARTH`S AXIS ALSO DEFINES THE LENGTH OF DAYLIGHT.

*DAYLIGHT HOURS ARE THE SHORTEST IN EACH HEMISPHERE`S WINTER BETWEEN SUMMER AND WINTER SOLSTICE,THE NUMBER OF DAYLIGHT HOURS DECREASES,AND ALSO THE RATE OF DECREASE IS LARGER THE HIGHER THE LATITUDE.THE FEWER SUNLIGHT HOURS THE COLDER THE NIGHTS.

2.)How is the length of the daytime related to the amount of energy received?

*THE LONGER OF THE LENGTH OF THE DAYTIME THE HIGHER THE AMOUNT OF ENERGY RECEIVED,THE SHORTER OF THE LENGTH OF THE DAYTIME THE SHORTER WILL BE THE EXPOSURE OF THE EARTH ON THE SUN CAUSING LESSER THE AMOUNT OF SOLAR ENERGY.

3.)How do you relate the position of the Earth in its orbit and the height of the Sun from the sky?

*AS THE EARTH ORBITS AROUND THE SUN,THE POSITION OF THE AXIS RELATED TO THE SUN CHANGES.THIS RESULT OF THE CHANGE IN THE OBSERVED HEIGHT OF OUR SUN ABOVE THE HORIZON.

4.)How do you relate the height of the Sun in the Sky to the amount of energy received?

*WHEN THE SUN IS OVERHEATED,ITS RAYS ARE MOST DIRECTAND INTENSE.AS IT BECOMES LOWER FROM THE SKY,THE SAME AREA OF LIGHT FROM THE SUN COVERS A LARGER AREA OF THE EARTH.

5.)What is the relationship between latitude of an area and the amount of energy the area received?

*THE TILT OF THE EARTH CAUSES DIFFERENT AREAS TO RECEIVE DIFFERENT AMOUNTS OF SOLAR ENERGY. THE DIFFERENCE OF IN SOLAR ENERGY RECEIVED AT DIFFERENT LATITUDES DRIVES ATMOSPHERIC CIRCULATION.

6.)Give atleast four reasons why reasons change.

*AREAS OF THE EARTH RECEIVE LESS LIGHT OR MORE LIGHT DEPENDING ON ITS POSITION RELATIVE TO THE SUN AND DEGREE OF AXIS TILT.

*HOWEVER,OTHER AREAS RECEIVE EQUAL AMOUNTS OF LIGHT AND DARKNESS,CAUSING FOUR DISTINCT SEASONS.

*SOME REGIONS RECEIVE MORE SUNLIGHT YEAR ROUND,PRIMARILY THE EQUATORIAL REGIONS.

*SEASON CHANGES BECAUSE OF THE TILT OF THE EARTH AND THE PLANET`S MOVEMENT AROUND THE SUN.

Cooking lechon manok. _______________ 2. Ice melts because the air around is warm. _______________ 3. Drying newly washed clothes in the sun. _______________ 4. Heating the frying pan. _______________ 5. Sauna bath (tuob). _______________ 6. The cover of the cooking pot becomes hot when cooking.


Answer:

1. Radiation

2. Convection

3. Conduction

4.Conduction

5. Convection

6. Conduction

Explanation:

Radiation - this is a type of heat transfer that happens when there's a movement of electromagnetic waves between the source of heat and the food.

Convection - it is the transfer of heat through the movement of fluids - air or liquid.

Conduction - there's a transfer of heat between two objects by physical contact.

Conduction - there's a transfer of heat between two objects by physical contact (the cover and the pot itself.) The movement of air molecules inside the pot causes the heat to come in contact with the cover, hence, it can also be convection. To say that it is insulation may be wrong because the method being asked is about how the heat is transferred whereas insulation is a process of stopping or impeding the transfer of heat.
