1. what is the relationship of the VMGO to your course automotive?​


what is the relationship of the VMGO to your course automotive?

VMGO stands for Vision, Mission, Goals, and Objectives

Vision: The vision for an automotive program can be to produce highly skilled and knowledgeable graduates who can contribute to the development of the automotive industry.

Mission: The mission of the program can be to provide quality education and training to students in the automotive field, while instilling in them the values of professionalism, innovation, and ethical behavior.

Goals: The goals of the program can include developing students' technical skills, critical thinking abilities, and communication skills, as well as fostering their creativity and problem-solving skills.

Objectives: The objectives of the program can be to ensure that students have a solid understanding of automotive systems and technologies, are able to perform diagnostic and repair procedures, can work effectively in a team, and are aware of the latest trends and developments in the industry.

20. what is the automotive services​


If you love working on cars, this may be a great career option for you to consider!

An automotive service technician (or auto mechanic) inspects, maintains, and repairs electric, gas, hybrid and alternative fuel vehicles. In smaller businesses, their duties may include a full range of repair and maintenance services. In larger businesses, they may specialize in certain areas, such as brakes, transmissions or air conditioning systems.

Automotive service technicians need to discuss automotive problems—along with options to fix them—with their customers. Automotive businesses depend on repeat clients, therefore a technician must always be courteous, a good listener, and ready to answer a customer's questions
