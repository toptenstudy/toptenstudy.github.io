1. top 10 social issues in the philippines


Top 10 Social Issues in the Philippines

Social issues are problems, or situations that are not normal, faced by people within a society. In the Philippines, there are many social issues that have been present for years and there are also new ones being encountered by society. Some of those that can be included in the Top 10 Social Issues in the Philippines include poverty, corruption of government officials, drug war, pollution, and environmental destruction. Aside from these, human rights, gender issues, agricultural concerns, lack of education, and the rising numbers of unemployment are also likely to join the list.

Further List of Top 10 Social Issues in the Philippines

Since social issues vary among sectors and groups, the list may be interchangeable. Likewise, the issues below can be part of the record:

BullyingViolenceReligious conflictPolitical conflictWater shortageMinimum wageMental health and suicideIllegal loggingSame-sex marriageHealthcare issues
