29. Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Snack mix for each bag:

13 cups of oat cereal 23 cups corn cereal

Part A

How many cups of cereal do they use for each snack bag?

Operation: Addition

Solution:

13 cups (oats) + 23 cups (corn) = 36 cups

Total cups of cereal for each snack bag = 36 cupsANSWER: 36 cups

Part B

Debbie and her friends make 18 snack bags.

How many cups of cereal did they use?

Operation: Multiplication

Solution:

Total cups of cereals for total snack bags = 18 bags  ×  36 cups/bag

Total cups for 18 bags = 648 cupsANSWER: 648 cups
