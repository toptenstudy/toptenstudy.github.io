15. A regular octagon is inscribed in a circle of radius 5√2 units . Find the area of the circle that is outside the octagon​


[tex] \large \mathcal{ANSWER:} [/tex]

[tex] \boxed{15.66\textsf{ sq. units}} [/tex]

[tex] \large \mathcal{SOLUTION:} [/tex]

[tex] \begin{array}{l} A_{\textsf{Segment}} = \dfrac{1}{2}r^2 (\theta - \sin \theta), \textsf{where }\theta\textsf{ is in radians.} \\ \\ \textsf{Area of the circle that is outside the octagon:} \\ \\ A = 8\times A_{\textsf{Segment}} \\ \\ \textsf{Given that }r=5\sqrt{2}\textsf{ units and}\: \theta = \dfrac{2\pi}{8} = \dfrac{\pi}{4}, \\ \\ A = 8\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)(5\sqrt{2})^2\!\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4} - \sin\left(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\right)\!\right) \\ \\ A \approx \boxed{15.66\textsf{ sq. units}} \end{array} [/tex]
