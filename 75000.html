24. Tolu earn #75000 monthly;he spends 10% of his salary on feeding 15% of the remainder on clothing and 25% of the balance on rent and transportation.if he save of the how much does he save money


To solve the problem, we can follow these steps:

1. Calculate how much Tolu spends on feeding:

   10% of 75000 = 0.10 x 75000 = 7500

2. Calculate the remainder after spending on feeding:

   75000 - 7500 = 67500

3. Calculate how much Tolu spends on clothing:

   15% of 67500 = 0.15 x 67500 = 10125

4. Calculate the balance after spending on feeding and clothing:

   67500 - 10125 = 57375

5. Calculate how much Tolu spends on rent and transportation:

   25% of 57375 = 0.25 x 57375 = 14343.75

6. Calculate how much Tolu saves:

   Saving = Balance - Spending

   Saving = 57375 - 14343.75 = 43031.25

Therefore, Tolu saves #43,031.25 per month.
