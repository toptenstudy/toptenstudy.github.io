26. Learning Task 3: Answer the following question. 1.Why it is more comfortable for us to wear white or light-colored clothes during summer or warm weather? 2.Why do you think that we see stars twinkling during night time ❤​


Answer:

1.We prefer white clothes in summer because white clothes reflect most of the sun's heat and absorb very little of the sun's heat and keeps our body cool. We prefer to wear black or dark coloured clothes in winter as dark clothes absorb most of the sun's heat and keep our body warm.

2.The stars twinkle in the night sky because of the effects of our atmosphere. When starlight enters our atmosphere it is affected by winds in the atmosphere and by areas with different temperatures and densities. This causes the light from the star to twinkle when seen from the ground.
