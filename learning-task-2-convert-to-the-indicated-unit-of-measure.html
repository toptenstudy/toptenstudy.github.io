Conversion

Conversion expresses the same property as a different unit of measurement. For example, to convert inches to feet, the appropriate conversion value is 12 inches equal 1 foot. To convert minutes to hours, the appropriate conversion value is 60 minutes equal 1 hour. Unit conversion is a multi-step process. Involves multiplication or division by a numerical factor, selection of the correct number of significant digits, and rounding.

Answer:

Convert to the indicated unit of measure

Explanation:

1. 1.708 mm = 0.001708 m

To convert from Millimetre(mm) to Meter(m)

We have to divide the Millimetre value by 1000,

Therefore, 1.708 / 1000 = 0.001708.

2. 15.6 km = 1560 dam.

To convert from Kilometer(km) to Decameter(dam),

We have to multiply the kilometer by 100,

Therefore, 15.6 * 100 = 1560 dam.

3. 200° F = 93.33° C

To convert from Fahrenheit to Celsius,

The formula is (200°F − 32) × 5/9 = 93.333°C.

4. 3,569 secs = 0.04130787 days

To convert seconds to days,

We have to divide the time value by 86400,

Therefore, 3,569 / 86400 = 0.04130787 days.

5. 4 Score = 959.999 mo.

4 score = 80 years.

To convert years to months, we have to multiply the year value by 12,

Therefore, 80 * 12 = 959.999 months(960).

6. 16.5 L = 4.358839 gal

To convert the liter value to gallons,

We have to divide the volume value by 3.785.

Therefore, 16.5 * 3.785 = 4.358839 gal.

7. 10,866 mg = 0.010866 kg

To convert Milligram(mg) to Kilogram(kg).

1 mg = (1/1000000) kg = 10-6 kg.

Therefore, 10,866 mg = 0.010866 kg.

8. 108cc = 108 mL

To convert Centimeter to Milliliter,

We have to multiply the volume value by 1.

Therefore, 108 * 1 = 108 mL.

9. 299k = 25.85°C

To convert Kelvin to Celcius,

The formula is 299K − 273.15 = 25.85°C.

10. 8tbsp = 118.294 mL

To convert tablespoon to Millilitre,

We have to multiply the volume value by 14.787.

Therefore, 8 * 14.787 = 118.294 mL.
