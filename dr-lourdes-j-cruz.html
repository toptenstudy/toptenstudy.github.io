WILLIAM HARVEY- was the first to recognize the full circulation of the blood in the human body and to provide experiments and arguments to support this idea.

FABIAN M. DAYRIT- has led the Integrated Chemists of the Philippines. Under his leadership, the Chemistry Profession Act was enacted, regulating professional practice in the country. He has received many awards and honors for his contribution to science.
