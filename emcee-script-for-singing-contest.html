1. emcee script for teacher


Answer:

Here's a sample emcee script for a teacher's recognition event:

Good evening, everyone! Welcome to this special event where we honor and recognize the hard work and dedication of our amazing teachers. My name is [Your Name], and I am honored to be your emcee for this evening.

Tonight, we gather to pay tribute to our teachers who have made a significant impact in the lives of their students. They have not only imparted knowledge and skills but also inspired and motivated their students to become better individuals.

We have with us tonight a group of outstanding teachers who have gone above and beyond their duties to provide quality education to their students. They have spent countless hours preparing lesson plans, grading papers, and providing guidance to their students. Let's give them a round of applause for their hard work and dedication!

To start off our program, I would like to invite [Name of School Principal] to say a few words.

[School Principal speaks]

Thank you, [Name of School Principal], for your inspiring words.

Now, it's time to introduce our teachers who will be recognized tonight. As I call your name, please come forward and receive your certificate of recognition.

[Call out the names of the teachers and have them come forward to receive their certificates.]

Congratulations, teachers! Your dedication and hard work have not gone unnoticed. You have touched the lives of many students and have inspired them to reach their full potential.

Now, let's hear from some of your students. We have prepared a video presentation of messages from your students, expressing their gratitude and appreciation for all that you do.

[Play the video presentation.]

Thank you, students, for sharing your heartfelt messages.

Finally, we would like to invite all the teachers to come up on stage for a group photo.

[Take a group photo.]

Once again, congratulations to our outstanding teachers! You are truly an inspiration to us all. Thank you for your unwavering commitment to education and for making a positive impact in the lives of your students.

3. example of Christmas Party Script for emcee's? ​


Sample Christmas Party Script for host

Good evening, everyone! Thank you for joining us here tonight for our annual Christmas party. My name is [Name], and I will be your emcee for the evening.

First, I would like to extend a warm welcome to all of our guests, and thank you for taking the time to celebrate the holiday season with us. We are so grateful to have such a wonderful community of friends, family and colleagues who make this time of year so special.

Now, let's get the party started! Our first order of business is to get into the holiday spirit with some Christmas carols. I would like to invite our choir to come up and lead us in some festive songs. Please join in and sing along!

After we have finished singing, it's time for some fun and games. We have a variety of activities planned for the evening, including a holiday-themed scavenger hunt, a gift exchange, and a trivia contest. So get ready to let your competitive spirit shine and win some great prizes!

Of course, no Christmas party is complete without a delicious feast. We have a wonderful spread of food and drinks waiting for you in the banquet hall. Please help yourselves and enjoy the culinary delights of the season.

As the night comes to a close, we would like to express our heart felt gratitude to everyone who has made this event possible. From the organizers and volunteers, to our sponsors and donors, we could not have done this without your support.

We hope you have had a wonderful time tonight and that you leave feeling happy, joyful and full of the holiday spirit. Merry Christmas, everyone!

21. criteria for judging singing contest


This is an example of criteria for judging for a singing contest:

•Stage Presence/Audience Impact- 10%

•Diction- 20%

•Vocal Quality- 35%

•Rhythmic Interpretation- 10%

•Dynamics- 10%

•Song Choice- 15%

Why is criteria for judging important?

•It is important for judges to know how to score and important for contestants to know what they should improve and focus on their performance.
