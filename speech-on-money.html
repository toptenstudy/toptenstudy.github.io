The figure of Speech – Simile

Meaning: Money is going to run out one day. Money does not generate itself; you must work for it.

2. Sentence –Christmas money from Santa Claus.

The figure of Speech – Allusion

Meaning: A gift or a present/ given voluntarily.

3. Sentence – Rain on your parade.

The figure of Speech – Analogy

Meaning: To prevent or spoil your plan.

4. Sentence – Sometimes fate is like a small sandstorm that keeps changing direction.

The figure of Speech – Simile

Meaning: Fate follows us no matter what choices we make.

27. What figure of speech in the sentence"Time is money" and what is the explanation to.​


Answer:

Metaphor

Explanation:

The statement compares time and money, and it does not literally mean that the amount of time you have equals the money that you have. Instead, it means that time is a valuable resource, and it should be used effectively to earn money.
