21. Quiz #3 I Convert The following measurement 6g=______oz 80g=______Ib 56g=_______kg 8kg=_______g 18oz=_______oz​


Answer:

1 oz = approximately 28.35 g

1 lb = approximately 453.59 g

1 kg = approximately 1000 g

6g = 0.21 oz

80g = 0.176 lb

56g = 0.056 kg

8kg = 8000g

18oz= 18 oz

Note: The conversions are approximate as there are slight variations between the avoirdupois and the troy systems of measurement, and also the conversion between metric and imperial units.


22. 6. Forty boys and twenty girls joined in a division level math quiz competition. At the awarding ceremony, 20% of the boys and 10% of the girls won prizes. How many received prizes? B.9 C. 10 D. 11 A 8​


Answer:

Number of boys who joined = 40

Number of girls who joined = 20

Number of boys who won prizes = 20% of 40 = 0.2 x 40 =8

Number of girls who won prizes = 10% of 20 = 0.1 x 20 = 2

Total number of students who won prizes = 8 + 2 = 10

Therefore, the answer is C. 10.
