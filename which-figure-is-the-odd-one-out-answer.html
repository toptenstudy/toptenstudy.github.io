30. in plant cell, nucleus and red blood cells, which one is the odd one out and why?​


Answer:

Nucleus

Explanation:

Plant cells are the basic unit of life in organisms of the kingdom Plantae. They are eukaryotic cells, which have a true nucleus along with specialized structures called organelles that carry out different functions.

A type of blood cell that is made in the bone marrow and found in the blood. Red blood cells contain a protein called hemoglobin, which carries oxygen from the lungs to all parts of the body.

The nucleus is an organelle found in eukaryotic cells. Inside its fully enclosed nuclear membrane, it contains the majority of the cell's genetic material.

So we can see here that nucleus is not a cell but plant and red blood cells are. Instead nucleus is an organelle, which is a part of both cells.
