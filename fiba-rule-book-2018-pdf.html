16. In a crowd of 16 500 people who watched the FIBA-Asia finals , 45% are women . How many men watched the FIBA-Asia finals?


Answer:

9,075 men

Computation:

Multiply 16,500 by 45% or 0.45 to determine the number of women that watched the FIBA-Asia finals.

16,500 × 0.45 = 7,425

Subtract 7,425 from 16,500 to determine the number of men that watched the FIBA-Asia finals.

16,500 - 7,425 = 9,07545% of 16500=7425
16500-7425=9075..
Therefore 9075 are the total number of the men who watched the FIBA-Asia finals.
