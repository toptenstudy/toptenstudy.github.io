16. 5. A 100-gram ball and a 200-gram ball are connected by a rod with length of 60 centimeters. The axisof rotation is located at the center of the rod. If the mass of the rod is ignored, what is the momentof inertia of the balls about the axis of rotation?​


Answer:

I = 0.027 kg.m²

Explanation:

Let:

m = mass

d = distance between the object and the axis of rotation

I = moment of inertia

For the 100-gram ball:

m = 100 g = 0.1 kg

d = (60/2) = 30 cm = 0.3 m

I = md² = (0.1)(0.3)²

I = 0.009 kg.m²

For the 200-g ball:

m = 200 g = 0.2 kg

d = (60/2) = 30 cm = 0.3 m

I = md² = (0.2)(0.3)²

I = 0.018 kg.m²

The moment of inertia of the balls is

I = 0.009 + 0.018

I = 0.027 kg.m²    (answer)
