25. The temperature inside the freezer is about 6.70 0C. If I place a balloon in the freezer that initially has a temperature of 27 0C and a volume of 0.75 liters, what will be the volume of the balloon when it is fully cooled by the freezer?​


Given:

[tex]V_{1} = \text{0.75 L}[/tex]

[tex]T_{1} = \text{27.0°C + 273.15 = 300.15 K}[/tex]

[tex]T_{2} = \text{6.70°C + 273.15 = 279.85 K}[/tex]

Unknown:

[tex]V_{2}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]\frac{V_{1}}{T_{1}} = \frac{V_{2}}{T_{2}}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = V_{1} × \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}[/tex]

[tex]V_{2} = \text{0.75 L} × \frac{\text{279.85 K}}{\text{300.15 K}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{V_{2} = \text{0.70 L}}[/tex]
