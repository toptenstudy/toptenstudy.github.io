24. Robert charges Php 5 for an echo bag and Php 2.75 for each pound of vegetables picked at the garden. Write an equation in y= mx +b form for the total cost of x pounds of vegetables from the garden.


Answer:

y = 2.75x + 5

Step-by-step explanation:

Let y be the total cost of everything Robert will buy in the vegetable garden, and as described by the question, let x be the amount of vegetables he will buy in pounds.

We are given the format of a line equation in slope-intercept form: y = mx + b. Since we already have a hint, we just need to figure out which given number goes in m and which goes in b.

Let's focus on the statement "... Php 2.75 for each pound of vegetables..." This statement gives the answer away. We need to multiply 2.75 into x, and the only variable multiplied to x is m. Thus, m = 2.75.

Now, Robert shall use an echo bag no matter how much vegetables he will buy. Using this logic and the process of elimination, we can conclude that b = 5.

Substituting these two numbers into the format, we have an equation: y = 2.75x + 5.

Therefore, the answer to this question is y = 2.75x + 5.
