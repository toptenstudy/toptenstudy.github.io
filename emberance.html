3. Research about the physics explanation why and how certain people certain people can walk on live ember​


ANSWER:The ability to walk on live embers is a well-known phenomenon, but the physics behind it is not well understood. The basic principle is that air has a higher thermal conductivity than most solids, so when air passes over a heated surface, it can carry away heat and prevent the surface from becoming too hot for the person walking on it. Additionally, the air can also act as a buffer between the person and the hot surface, further protecting from the heat. In some cases, the air can even carry away enough heat to cool the surface enough for the person to walk on it without getting burned. This phenomenon is known as thermal convection.
