8. The grade six pupils at Sumulong Elementary School went on an outing to the National Museum, accompanied by their parents. There were six times as many children as parents. If 245 people went on this outing. how many were parents?​


PROBLEM

The grade six pupils at Sumulong Elementary School went on an outing to the National Museum, accompanied by their parents. There were six times as many children as parents. If 245 people went on this outing. how many were parents?

SOLUTION

Let P = number of parents in an outing

C = number of children in an outing

Equation (1) : Number of children = 6 Number of parents

C = 6P

Equation (2) : Number of parents + Number of children = 245

P + C = 245

Substitute 6P to C in an equation (2):

P + 6P = 245

7P = 245

P = 35

CHECK

The number of children is 6(35) or 210.

The total number of people is 35 + 210 = 245.

ANSWERThere were 35 parents in an outing.
