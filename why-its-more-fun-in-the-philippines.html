Answer:

1) Sagada, Mountain Province - Have a cave tour full of crystals and some coffins of the natives in the Lumiang Cave and hike along the road to reach the Bomod Ok Falls.

The area is very cold even during summers that you don’t need to open an aircon.

2) Underground River, Palawan Province - Have a boat tour of the underground river full of crystals. the river is very clean and clear.

3) Mount Pinatubo, Zambales Province - You will see how blue is the water on the crater of the volcano

4) Boracay - Enjoy the white sand beaches there.

5) Villa Escudero - have you ever heard about a restaurant by the waterfall? It’s here! As well as a coconut plantation, museum, performances - a great point to explore history, culture, and traditions of the Philippines.

6) Chocolate Hills of Bohol Province - while on the road, you may see the tarsier

7) Baguio City of Benguet Province - The modernized and cold summer capital of the Philippines

8) Puerto Galera - Just south of Manila is the charming coastal town of Puerto Galera, well-favored for its gorgeous beaches, excellent diving spots and wide diversity of marine species. The most popular beaches are White Beach and Sabang Beach, which also offer nearby shopping, dining, nightlife and hotel accommodation. Another popular attraction is the natural harbor of Muelle Bay with its rows of shops, restaurants and bars.

9) Subic Bay Area

10) Intramuros - The old walled city in Manila built during the Spanish colonial era. You will be able to see the Fort Santiago, Spain’s oldest military garrison in the Philippine’s capital and some very old churches like Manila Cathedral and San Agustin Church.

Why is it more fun in the Philippines?

Because the majority of people are not materialistic jet set types. They are down to earth, friendly and fun loving people. The weather is always warm and you are never far from a beach or river.
