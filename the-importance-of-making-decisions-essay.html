“The Importance of Making Decision”

Every day is always different. We encounter different circumstances which need careful decisions. Who we are in the future is the result of the decisions we made in the past. That is why it is essential to take time in weighing things before we come up with a deep decision.  

Below are some steps I would like to share in making a decision.

Step 1: This step is very critical. Identify the nature of the decision you must make. Does it affect a lot of things around you? What are the impacts?  

Step 2: Fully explore lots of options and hear reflections from others.  We might acquire unsolicited advice from the people around us, but the decision lies within ourselves.  

Step 3: Identify the alternatives and possible actions to make. You have to think several times to gather information and construct new alternatives.  

Step 4: Weigh things base on the information and emotions you gathered and imagined. Evaluate whether this decision could resolve the problem.  

Step 5: Carefully choose among the alternatives after you have done weighing things. You may be able to foresee what the best one for you is.  

Step 6: It’s time to take the action. Get ready to implement the chosen alternative action.

Step 7: Review your decision and its consequences. This is the step when you think of the results. Does it solve?  

These steps are only based on my experience and not considered as a perfect formula for making the right decision. Just be strong in facing whatever consequences might happen after all.
