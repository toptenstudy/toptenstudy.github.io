Fractions:

The fraction halfway between 3/7 and 4/7 is 3.5/7 or 31/2/7.

To solve for the fraction halfway between 3/7 and 4/7, add the numerators of the two fractions then divide the sum by 2 such that:

3/7 + 4/7 = 7/2/7 then,

3.5/7 or 3 1/2/7

Notes:

The given fractions are similar since their denominators are the same.

In adding similar fractions, just add the numerators and copy the denominator.

The term halfway means the midway between the two given fractions that is why we divide the sum of the two numerators by 2.

The numerator of the fraction halfway between 3/7 and 4/7 can be expressed in decimal form which is 3.5 or fraction form which is 3 1/2.

Other examples of similar fractions are: 2/3 and 1/3, 5/9 and 7/9, 1/8 and 5/8.

8. The fraction halfway between 3/7 and 4/7. A. 1/8 B. 1/4 C. 1/2 D. 1/3.​


The fraction halfway or average of [tex]\frac{3}{7}[/tex] and [tex]\frac{4}{7}[/tex] is [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex].

In other words, what fraction is exactly in the middle of [tex]\frac{3}{7}[/tex] and [tex]\frac{4}{7}[/tex]? The fraction will not be smaller than [tex]\frac{3}{7}[/tex] and not greater than [tex]\frac{4}{7}[/tex].

The average of two numbers is half-way between them.

Halfway is an average of two fractions. The average number is a number that is exactly located at the same distance from the lowest and highest numbers on the line of numbers. that is, the number that is in the middle of the two numbers. The number that is precisely equal in distance from both the lowest and greatest number on the number line is known as the mean of all numbers.

The way to find the average of two numbers is to sum the numbers and divide them by 2.

[tex]\frac{\frac{3}{7}+ \frac{4}{7} }{2}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{\frac{7}{7} }{2}[/tex]

after that we can simplify the fraction by dividing 7 by 7.

[tex]\frac{\frac{7}{7} }{2}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex].

Then, we can conclude that the fraction halfway or average of [tex]\frac{3}{7}[/tex] and [tex]\frac{4}{7}[/tex] is [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex].

24. what number is halfway between 3/7 and 3/5 solution po pls​

[tex] \frac{3}{5} - \frac{3}{7} = \frac{21 - 15}{35} = \frac{6}{35} [/tex]
Divide the answer by 2
[tex] \frac{6}{35} \div 2 = \frac{6}{70} [/tex]
Now add the answer to the initial number 3/7
[tex] \frac{3}{7} + \frac{6}{70} = \frac{30 + 6}{70} = \frac{36}{70} = \frac{18}{35} [/tex]
Therefore 18/35 is halfway between 3/7 and 3/5
