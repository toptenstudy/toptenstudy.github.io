21. 1. What are the sustainable business practices of amazon based on the given case?


Amazon has made significant efforts to implement sustainable business practices across its operations to reduce its environmental impact. here are some of the sustainable business practices that Amazon has implemented in recent years:

Renewable Energy:
Amazon is committed to powering its global infrastructure using renewable energy, with a goal to reach 100% renewable energy by 2025.

Sustainable Packaging:
Amazon is working to reduce packaging waste by using more sustainable materials, designing products to be more compact, and implementing programs to incentivize customers to recycle.

Recycling:
Amazon has implemented recycling programs in its operations, such as recycling e-waste, cardboard, and plastic.

Green Building:
Amazon is constructing new buildings that meet green building standards, such as LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification.

Efficient Transportation:
Amazon is working to reduce emissions from its transportation by using more fuel-efficient vehicles, optimizing routes, and implementing programs to reduce delivery packaging.

These initiatives demonstrate Amazon's commitment to sustainability and contribute to the company's long-term success as a responsible business. While there is still room for improvement, Amazon's efforts to implement sustainable practices serve as an example for other businesses to follow.
