2. What is the scientific name of red rose


Answer:

Rosa

Explanation:

The Scientific name for a rose is called Rosa gallica or just Rosa.

Answer:

The scientific name for red rose is Rosa gallica. From genus Rosa and specie gallica.

Explanation:

Remember that there are rules to follow in writing the scientific name of  a plant.

Always capitalized the first letter of the Genus which is the first word or epithet of every scientific name of a plant specie. The specie (second word or epithet)  is in lowercaseYou can write it in two waysItalicized (for example Rosa gallica)Underlined (for example Rosa gallica)

Rosa gallica belongs to a family of Rosaceae, genus Rosa and specie gallica. It is commonly known as Rose or red rose.
