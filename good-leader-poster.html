Leadership and Followership

The message on the poster urges viewers to help save the environment. The most crucial social awareness that everyone should have in order to make a difference on Earth is environmental preservation. By preserving trees, native plants, water, raw materials, and electricity, we can protect the environment. Environmental degradation and global warming, we must diligently adhere to all available precautions by stopping pollution and reducing the effects of global warming, everyone should plant more trees around. This will promote natural resource conservation, greenery, reforestation, recycling of waste paper, and other natural products.

Effective leaders inspire, convince, and motivate others in addition to giving orders. Leaders draw out the expertise and abilities of a group, guide people toward a common objective and consensus, and inspire a commitment to get things done. A good follower stands up for the leader, has the confidence to inform the leader when he or she is doing something wrong or moving in the wrong path and supports and aids the leader when he or she is doing the right thing.

The first steps involve discovering how to take charge of one's own destiny, forging a solid moral foundation, and learning how to create objectives and maintain motivation. Building leadership qualities will increase a young person's self-assurance and capacity for independent thought. The leader must help his followers improve, while the followers must contribute to the success of their leader. Both roles are equally vital, demand responsibility, and interact with one another.
