Answer:

VALID GENERALIZATIONINVALID GENERALIZATIONINVALID GENERALIZATIONINVALID GENERALIZATIONVALID GENERALIZATIONVALID GENERALIZATIONVALID GENERALIZATIONINVALID GENERALIZATIONVALID GENERALIZATIONVALID GENERALIZATION

Explaination:

1.) Nature is a man's treasure. - VALID GENERALIZATION (pertains to the common belief that nature is a valuable resource)

2.) Mrs. Medina baked this sweet cake. - INVALID GENERALIZATION (a specific statement that doesn't apply to all situations)

3.) The students do their exercises very well. - INVALID GENERALIZATION (assumes that all students excel in exercises)

4.) Cats and dogs are my best friends at home. - INVALID GENERALIZATION (personal preference that doesn't apply to everyone)

5.) Humans are responsible to take care nature. - VALID GENERALIZATION (pertains to the responsibility of all humans to take care of nature)
