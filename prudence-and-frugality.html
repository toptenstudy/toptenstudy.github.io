Frugality means being thrifty or spend money or other material things wisely.  Prudence is the ability to use wisdom and careful thinking when making choices in life or responding to a particular situation. Frugality combined with prudence makes a person acquires a sound mind in everything that he or she will do. If all individuals on earth would apply these positive traits,  our community is a better place to live in.
