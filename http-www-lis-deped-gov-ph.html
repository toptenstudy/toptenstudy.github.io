20. Reasons why lis deped accounts are disabled


There are lots of reasons why a website has been disabled. 

Here are a few things to checked:

1. That the subscription was active and that the hosting and/or domain didn’t need renewing

2. That files existed on the server

3. That the DNS or nameservers weren’t pointing elsewhere.

4. The subscription of the domain and hosting has not yet paid or remain unpaid.

5 Too many users of the website.

6 The administrator set the website as disabled.

For DepEd LIS system, they always update their system from time to time for the security of all data in the system. For more reasons you may contact your LIS coordinator because they all the reasons why. They were notified by the Division LIS coordinator.
