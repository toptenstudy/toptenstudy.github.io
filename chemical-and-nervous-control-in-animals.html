3. Compare and contrast the chemical and nervous control in both plants and animals.


Answer:

In all animals, including humans, control and coordination occur with the help neurons and a chemical substance called hormones

A neuron is a nerve cell, the basic building block of the nervous system. The neuron is a specialized cell, which functions by transmitting information throughout the body.

Hormones are chemical messengers that are secreted directly into the blood by the specialized glands called the endocrine glands. There are different types of hormones, secreted by different glands and serve as messengers throughout the body.
