28. Do you think that practicing Corporate Social Responsibility is an obligation or an option? Why?​


I believe practicing corporate social responsibility is an obligation. A business always requires resources, most especially physical and human resources and without these, a business cannot successfully be created. Therefore, a business should learn how to take care of these certain resources and where they have come from in order to ensure the continuity of the business. For instance, the business should consider how it affects the environment in a negative manner because this is where these physical resources that the business has acquired have originated from. It's like a domino effect- no environment would lead to no resources and thus, leading to the decline of the business.
