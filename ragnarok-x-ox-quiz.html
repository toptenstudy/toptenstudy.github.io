There's no doubt Marvel's Thor makes me review my knowledge about Norse Mythology.  And maybe that's the reason why I love the movie. I like how Thor and Loki still got together (or end up as a team) even if they are each other's nemesis: The God of Thunder and the God of Mischief.  But I can't also deny the fact that Cate Blanchett (as Hela) and Tessa Thompson (as Valkyrie) stole mega-attention from viewers because of their girl-power.  They did very well.

Cliche it is but yes, it is a story with a moral lesson in the ending. It's like, the superhero movies must-have, so expect that.

This is one of the films that I will watch over and over again but not get sick. Well, because I'm a Marvel's fan as well as a mythology-junkie.
