22. Basic concepts in Social Studies and their effect on Filipino’s Self-Reliance, Peace and Conflict Resolution, Social Problems, Growth and Development, and Human Rights.​


There are several basic concepts in social studies that can have an impact on various aspects of society, including self-reliance, peace and conflict resolution, social problems, growth and development, and human rights. Some of these concepts include:

Culture: Culture refers to the shared values, beliefs, customs, behaviors, and practices of a group or society. Understanding different cultures can help promote self-reliance by fostering an appreciation for diversity and encouraging people to learn from one another. It can also help facilitate peace and conflict resolution by promoting understanding and respect for different cultural practices and values.

Power and authority: Power refers to the ability to influence or control others, while authority refers to the right to make decisions or exercise control. Understanding the dynamics of power and authority can help individuals and communities navigate conflicts and work towards resolution. It can also be important in addressing social problems, as imbalances of power can contribute to social inequalities and injustice.

Governance: Governance refers to the systems and processes by which a society is governed and decisions are made. Understanding how governance works can help individuals and communities participate in decision-making processes and advocate for their needs and interests. It can also be important in promoting growth and development, as effective governance can help create a stable and supportive environment for economic and social progress.

Human rights: Human rights refer to the fundamental rights and freedoms that are believed to be inherent to all human beings. Understanding and protecting human rights can be critical in promoting self-reliance, as it helps ensure that individuals have the freedom and opportunity to pursue their own goals and interests. It can also be important in promoting peace and conflict resolution, as respect for human rights can help create a more just and equitable society.
