23. 1. Does procrastination have a significant effect on the academic standing of students?​


Answer:

Yes, procrastination can have a significant impact on a student's academic standing. Procrastination can lead to decreased productivity, decreased quality of work, increased stress and decreased motivation, which can negatively affect grades and overall academic performance. It is important for students to develop effective time-management strategies and to understand the root causes of their procrastination in order to overcome it and succeed academically.
