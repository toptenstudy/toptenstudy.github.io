4. There are 18 Davaoenos who participated in a seminar. this is 18% of the total number of the participants. how many attended the seminar?


Answer:

100

Step-by-step explanation:

We need to convert 18% to fraction first.

18% = 0.18 in decimal form0.18 = 9/50 in fraction form

So let x be the total number of participants.

[tex]18 = \frac{9}{50} x[/tex]

To eliminate 9/50, we should multiply both sides  to its reciprocal

[tex]18×\frac{50}{9} = (\frac{9}{50} × \frac{50}{9}) x[/tex]

100 = x

Hence, there are a total of 100 participants who attended the seminar

30. In social situation where students are attending a seminar, are there behaviors or interaction which are pleasing or irritating to others? explain your answer.​


It is stated there that they are attending a seminar. usually seminars are educational and are conducted to bring awareness to a specific issue. So therefore the audience is EXPECTED to LISTEN to the speaker. To answer your question, it is pleasing to others, especially the speaker, the organizers, your teacher if you listen. It is irritating if you're talking to a seatmate or a friend about a topic that is not related to the seminar. Giving a speech is a hard task and requires careful preparation, therefore one should show respect by listening and absorbing the knowledge presented to you by the speaker and allow yourself to comprehend and learn from his speech.
