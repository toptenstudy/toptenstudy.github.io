22. TORTA RECIPE:8 cups APF6 cups sugar1 cup oil2 cups water4 pieces eggs2 tablespoon vanilla1 teaspoon salt6 table spoon baking powderMeasurementAbbreviationSubstitutionIngredientsAll Purpose FlourWaterWhite sugarOilEggsVanillaBaking powderSalt​


Answer:

Ingredients

1 cup sugar 1/2 cup water10 pieces egg yolks 1-1/2 Tablespoons baking powder 3 cups all purpose flour 1/2 teaspoon anise powder 1/2 cup butter, melted 1/2 cup vegetable oil 1 can 300 ml. condensed milk

How to make it

1.) Boil sugar and water for 5 mins. to make a syrup. Set aside to cool.

2.) Combine flour, baking powder, & anise powder in a bowl. Set aside.

3.) Combine melted butter, oil, & prepared syrup together.

4.) Whisk in egg yolks, & condensed milk .

5.) Stir in flour mixture.

6.) Pour batter into lined torta or muffin molds.

7.) Bake in a preheated oven at 350degrees F for 22 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.

8.) If you like, brush the top of baked torta with melted butter and sprinkle with granulated sugar.

9.) Serve & enjoy.
