17. the art of making design or patterns on the walls or floors of houses


Answer:

The art of making designs or patterns on the walls and floors of houses is called

Its Rangoli

Rangoli the art of creating designs or patterns on the walls or floor of a building using finely ground white powder and various colors. Rangoli patterns are used by many Indian subcontinent households to decorate their home's courtyard.

Rangoli patterns are formed on the floor or a tabletop using materials such as powdered lime stone, dry rice flour, colored sand, quartz powder, flower petals, and colored rocks in this art form that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is a common practice in Hindu homes, but the colors are chosen during festivals and other important festivities because it takes time. It is traditionally prepared for Diwali or Tihar, Onam, Pongal, Sankranthi, and other Hindu festivals in the Indian subcontinent. It is mainly rendered during Diwali. Designs are handed on from generation to generation, preserving both the art style and the tradition.

Rangoli Designs may be plain geometric forms, god impressions, or flower and petal shapes (appropriate for the occasion), but they can also be very intricate designs created by a large group of people. Geometric patterns can also depict powerful religious images, because they are used in and around household shrines, as well as around yagna and yaga pits. In the past, simple designs were drawn around cooking areas to keep insects and pathogens at bay. Synthetic colors are a more recent addition. Other products include red brick powder and, in the case of flower rangolis, flowers and petals.

Imagination and new concepts have been introduced into Rangoli art over time. The hospitality and tourism sectors have also had an impact, and rangoli has become commercially viable in locations such as five-star hotels. Its conventional beauty, artistry, and significance have not faded.

The rangoli is known by different names depending on the state and tradition. Rangoli plays an important role in the daily life of a Hindu household, particularly in the olden days when no tiles were used for house flooring. They are normally made outside the main entrance's threshold in the early mornings after cleaning the place. The postures used to make a rangoli have traditionally been used as a form of exercise for women to straighten their spines. The rangoli symbolizes the happiness, positivity, and vibrancy of one's home. It is to welcome Lakshmi, the Goddess of Riches and Fortune. 18. make a Floor layout/plan of your house with emergency exit plan


Answer:

A fire safety plan helps you identify routes through a building or home in the event of a fire or other emergency. It outlines how people should escape a burning house or building or evacuate when a fire threatens the structure. You should also include items you'd want to take in the event of an evacuation and a meeting point or communication plan in the event you're separated. Creating a fire safety plan should be part of any fire pre-planning you do. Everyone in your office or family should be familiar with the plan and be prepared to follow the plan either if a smoke alarm goes off or you're requested to evacuate by authorities or emergency personnel.

What is an emergency evacuation plan?

An emergency evacuation plan shows you the safe routes you can take to exit a building during an emergency like a fire.

How often should you practice a fire escape plan?

You should practice your safety plan at least twice a year to make sure everyone knows the routes.
