26. A company x produces 120 bags of cement with 10 workers in 5hours and company Z produces 200 bags of cements with 18 workers in 8 hours. find how many bags of cement that will be producedby 20 workers in 10 hours


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

We can start by finding the production rate per worker per hour for each company:

Company X: (120 bags) / (10 workers) / (5 hours) = 2.4 bags/worker/hour

Company Z: (200 bags) / (18 workers) / (8 hours) = 1.39 bags/worker/hour

Next, we can use the production rate to estimate the total production of 20 workers in 10 hours:

Total bags produced by 20 workers in 10 hours for Company X:

(2.4 bags/worker/hour) * (20 workers) * (10 hours) = 480 bags

Total bags produced by 20 workers in 10 hours for Company Z:

(1.39 bags/worker/hour) * (20 workers) * (10 hours) = 278 bags

Therefore, if 20 workers work for 10 hours, Company X can produce approximately 480 bags of cement, and Company Z can produce approximately 278 bags of cement.
