To find it, we must see if there is any common variables, if there is then that's good! Now that we know there is a common variable, we must look at which variable has the lowest degree or exponent.

We can see that the first term, x^2 has the lowest degree or exponent.

21. The majority of us is/are ind favor​


Answer:

”Majority” is one of a few nouns in English that can be used either with a singular or a plural verb. These nouns are called collective nouns because they describe a collective (i.e. a group) of people or things. Their usage in English differs from most other languages: We use a singular verb if the whole group is understood as a single entity, and a plural verb if we are referring to all individuals who belong to the group.
