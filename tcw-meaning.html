1. TCWS #5

A Super Typhoon will affect the locality. DAMAGE TO STRUCTURE

2. TCWS #3

Storm surge possible at coastal. DAMAGE TO STRUCTURE: Moderate damage to medium-risk structures; Light damage to low-risk structures.

3. TCWS #1

PSWS # 1: Winds 30-60 kph -expected in at least 36 hrs; PSWS # 2: Winds >60-100 kph -expected in at least 24 hrs; PSWS # 3: Winds >100-185 kph -expected in at least 18 hrs; and PSWS # 4: Winds >185 kph -expected in at least 12 hrs.

4. TCWS #2

Storm surge is an abnormal rise of water generated by a storm, over and above the predicted astronomical tides.

5. TCWS #4

Tropical Cyclone Wind Signal #4 is that a few houses of first class material are partially damaged.
