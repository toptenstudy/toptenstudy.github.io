21. Jay's father is twice as old as Jay. In 20 years , Jay will be two-thirds as old as his father. How old is Jay now? What is the age of Jay's father now ?​


Answer:

Jay's father is twice as old as Jay. In 20 years , Jay will be two-thirds as old as his father. How old is Jay now? What is the age of Jay's father now?​

Answer:

Jay is 20 and his Father is 40

Solution:

Let f = Father's age now

Let j = Jay's age now

Given:

f = 2j

j + 20 = (2/3) x (f + 20)

By substitution:

j + 20 = (2/3) x (2j + 20)

j + 20 = (4/3) x j + 40/3

Multiply both sides by 3

3j + 60 = 4j + 40

j = 20

and

f = 2j

f = 40

Jay is 20 and his Father is 40
