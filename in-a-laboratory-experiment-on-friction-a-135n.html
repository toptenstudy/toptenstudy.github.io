1. n a physics laboratory, Namjoon and Seokjin used a force of 30N to push a 35kg cart to make it accelerate. While in motion, the cart experiences a frictional force of 12N. Compute the acceleration of the cart.


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The acceleration of an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{a = \frac{F_{net}}{m}}[/tex]

where:

[tex]F_{net} =[/tex] net force

m = mass

a = acceleration

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the net force

[tex]F_{net} = F_{applied} - F_{friction}[/tex]

[tex]F_{net} = \text{30 N} - \text{12 N}[/tex]

[tex]F_{net} = \text{18 N}[/tex]

Solving for the acceleration

[tex]a = \dfrac{F_{net}}{m}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{\text{18 N}}{\text{35 kg}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{a = \text{0.514 m/}\text{s}^{2}}[/tex]

Therefore, the acceleration of the cart is 0.514 m/s².
