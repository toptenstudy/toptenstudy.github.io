9. In a poster making contest, you are required that the rectangular poster must have a perimeter of 34 inches. You need to find the dimensions of the poster if the length is 2 inches more than twice the width.​


Given:

Perimeter of Rectangular Poster: 34 inches

Length: 2 inches more than twice the width

Formula of Perimeter of a Rectangle: 2L + 2WSolution:

1. Since the Perimeter of the Poster is given, we know that

                    2L + 2W = 34 inches

2. To find the length of the rectangular poster, let's make a formula based on the given. Thus,

                     L = 2 + 2W

3. Now, let's substitute the formula for length in the formula of the perimeter. Thus,

                     2 (2 + 2W) + 2W = 34

Cross multiply..

                     4 + 4W + 2W = 34

Combine like terms..

                     4W + 2W = 34 - 4

                     6W = 30

Remove the 6 in 6W by dividing it both side.

Now, we have..

                     W = 30/6

                     W = 5

4. We have the value of width which is 5. Now, let's find the value of the length by substitution.

                   2L + 2W = 34

                   2L + 2(5) = 34

                   2L + 10 = 34

Combine like terms..

                  2L = 34 - 10

                  2L = 24

Remove the 2 in 2L by dividing it both side.

                  L = 24/2

                  L = 12

5. Thus, the dimension of the rectangular poster is 12 (length) and 5 (width).

6. To check, substitute the value of length and width to the formula of perimeter.

                2L + 2W = 34

                2(12) + 2(5) = 34

                24 + 10 = 34

                34 = 34

12. criteria for judging dance contest


Answer:

This is an example of criteria for judging a dance contest:

•Choreography (Creativity, Artistry, Style & Originality) - 40%

•Execution - 10%

•Audience Impact- 10%

•(Timing & Coordination, Projection & Expression) - 30%

•Costume - 10%

Why is criteria for judging important?

•It is important for judges to know how to score and important for contestants to know what they should improve and focus on their performance.

#AnswerForTrees

In judging a dance contest, it's either you or the organizers should have a prepared criteria. However, here are the basic criteria for judging a dance contest:

A) Choreography - this includes the sequence of dance steps in a dance act or performance. This criteria must include the creativity, artistry, style, and originality of dance steps.

B) Execution - this includes the projection and expression, timing and coordination of each member during the performance

C) Costume - this is also a vital part in a dance act. Costumes make the performance come to life depending on what they are portraying/depending on the concept of the dance act/performance

D) Audience impact - audience's claps, chants and cheers is also part of the criteria because performances are made for an audience that is why their reaction matters as well.

Criteria for judging should be divided in percentages totaling 100 percent. For example, Choreography is 40 percent, Execution is 30 percent, Costume is 20 percent, Audience impact is 10 percent for a total of 100 percent.

Criteria matters for it to be fair to all the performances being judged.

In mathematics, the definition of perimeter is a path that encompasses/surrounds a two-dimensional shape. The formula to find the perimeter of rectangle is 2L + 2W or 2(L+W), where L is a length and W is a width of a rectangle.

In the given problem, first create a formula based on the given situation. Let's solve together.

The given based on the situation are:

Perimeter is 64 inch

Length is 5+2W, and the

Width is W

So, the formula is 2L+2W=P, then

2(5+2W) + 2W = 64, since L= 5+2W

Then simplify:

10+4W+2W = 64

6W + 10 = 64

6W = 54

W = 9

Therefore, the dimension of the rectangle is 9 by 23 inches..
