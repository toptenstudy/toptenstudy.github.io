25. Why do you choose BSHM course? ​


Answer:

As a general answer to this question, students may choose BSHM (Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management) course because:

1. Interest in the hospitality industry: Students who are interested in the hospitality industry, such as hotels, restaurants, events management, and tourism, may choose BSHM as it provides in-depth knowledge and practical skills in these areas.

2. Career opportunities: BSHM graduates can pursue various career paths such as hotel management, catering management, tourism, food and beverage management, and events management. These industries offer stable jobs with high salaries.

3. Personal growth: BSHM courses offer an opportunity for personal growth as it instills values such as leadership, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving.

4. International exposure: Many BSHM courses offer an international experience as the hospitality industry is global. Students can gain cross-cultural competence and understanding of different hospitality practices around the world.

5. Creative and dynamic learning: BSHM courses are designed to provide a dynamic learning experience with case studies, internships, and guest speakers. It encourages creativity and innovation, and also provides a platform for networking.
