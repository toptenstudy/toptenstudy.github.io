4. A certain power supply filter produces an output ripple of 100mV peak to peak and a vdc of 20V. the ripple factor is.


Answer: the ripple factor is 0.005

Explanation:

Given the data in the question;

we know that expression of ripple factor is;

r = Vr(pp) / Vdc

where Vr(pp) the peak to peak is ripple voltage ( 100mv = 0.1 V)

and Vdc is the dc value of the filter output voltage ( 20 V)

so we substitute our given values;

r = 0.1 / 20

r = 0.005

Therefore; the ripple factor is 0.005

15. The conscious perception of pain is often delayed because of the time it takes for the sensations to reach the cerebral cortex. Why would this be the case based on propagation of the axon potential?


Answer:

The conscious perception of pain is delayed because it takes time for the pain signals to be transmitted from the site of the injury to the brain. This is due to the fact that pain signals are transmitted through axons, which are long, thin fibers that extend from the cell body of a neuron. When an axon is stimulated, it generates an electrical signal known as an action potential, which travels along the axon and is transmitted to the next neuron in the pathway.

The speed at which an action potential travels along an axon depends on the diameter of the axon and the type of axon. Smaller axons and those with a thinner myelin sheath (a layer of insulation that surrounds the axon) tend to have slower conduction velocities. The axons that transmit pain signals are typically smaller and have a thinner myelin sheath, which contributes to the slower conduction velocity of these signals.
