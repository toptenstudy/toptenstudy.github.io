14. There are 100 pigs and chickens in a farm,all of Wich are healthy.if there are 340 legs in total, how many pigs are there​


Answer:

70 pigs

Step-by-step explanation:

Let:

x = number of pigs

y = number of chickens

x + y = 100 <--- equation 1

4x + 2y = 340 <--- equation 2

From equation 1:

y = 100 - x <--- equation 3

Substitute equation 3 in 2

4x + 2(100 - x) = 340

4x + 200 - 2x = 340

2x = 140

x = 70 <== answer

∴ there are 70 pigs in the farm

24. 1. In a farm there are chickens and pigs. Altogether, there are 70 legs and 25 heads of the pigs and chickens. How many chickens and pigs are there?​


Answer:

There are 10 pigs and 15 chickens

Step-by-step explanation:

let c be the chicken and p be the pigs

Creating an equation given from the problem.

Remember that their heads are only 1 so ,

c + p = 25

Remember also that the legs of a chicken is 2. and the legs of a pig is 4.

2c+4p = 70

Solving the system of linear equations:

c + p = 25

c = 25 - p (1)

2c+4p=70 (2)

Substitute c to the second equation and solve for p

2c + 4p = 70 2(25-p) + 4p = 7050-2p+4p = 7050+2p = 702p = 70-502p = 20 p = 20/2 p = 10

Again , substitute the value of p to the first equation

c = 25-pc = 25-10c = 15

Therefore , there are 10 pigs and 15 chickens

27. In Mr.Tan's farm,there are 48 animals.There are 12 more pigs than goats.How many pigs and goats are there?​


Answer:

There are 18 goats and 30 pigs.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let g be the number of goats and g + 12 be the number of pigs.

Find the number of goats:

[tex] \\ \tt{}g + (g + 12) = 48[/tex]

[tex]\\ \tt{}g + g + 12 = 48[/tex]

[tex]\\ \tt{}2g = 48 - 12[/tex]

[tex]\\ \tt{}2g = 36[/tex]

[tex]\\ \tt{}g = \frac{36}{2} [/tex]

[tex]\\ \boxed{ \large \boxed{ \tt{}g = 18}}[/tex]

There are 18 goats.

===============================

Find the number of pigs:

[tex]\\ \tt{} = g + 12[/tex]

[tex]\\ \tt{} = 18 + 12[/tex]

[tex]\\ \boxed{ \large \boxed{ \tt{} = 30}}[/tex]

There are 30 pigs.

===============================

Check to verify:

[tex]\\ \tt{}18 + 30 = 48[/tex]

[tex]\\ \tt{}48 = 48[/tex]

===============================
