5. Is a kind of speech that gives information and persuade while diverting the audience's attention to amuse and decrease tension that builds rapport?


This kind of speech is called a Persuasive speech which is meant to develop a good relationship with the audience while convincing them to somehow change their views. It can be used on charity events - convincing people why it's a good idea to make a donation. More commonly we see this kind of speech on television in the form of public service announcements for health, safety, and education. This can also be seen (or heard) on cartoons, animes, and TV dramas.

In some ways, it is also a kind of Motivational or Inspirational speech, since the aim of a Persuasive speech is to spark motivation and inspiration to act or do something.
