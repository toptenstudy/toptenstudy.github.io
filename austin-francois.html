18. Francois payard contribution in baking industry​


Answer:

Francois Payard is a renowned pastry chef and baker who has made significant contributions to the baking industry. Here are a few of his notable achievements:

World Pastry Champion: Payard won the World Pastry Championship in 1995, an incredibly prestigious award in the baking industry.

Patissier of the Year: He was named "Pastry Chef of the Year" by the James Beard Foundation in 1998.

Pastry Shops: Payard has opened several pastry shops throughout the world, including locations in New York City, Las Vegas, Tokyo, and Seoul.

Pastry Cookbook Author: Payard has authored several cookbooks on pastry, including "Simply Sensational Desserts," "Chocolate Epiphany," and "Payard Desserts."

French Pastry Techniques: Payard is known for his traditional French pastry techniques and for incorporating his unique flair to classic recipes.

Innovation: Payard has also innovated by creating new pastries and desserts, such as his signature "Payard macaron" and the "Marie Antoinette," a cake made with almond sponge, vanilla bean cream, and fresh raspberries.

30. Sino si Francois Quesnay


Si

Francois Quesnay

ang doktor ni Louis XIV. Ang kanyang iskema ay nagbibigay-daan na ang iba'tibang elemento ng ekonomiya ay magkakaugnay at sama-sama tulad ng mga blood vessels ng katawan. Pinag-aralan at pinag-isipan niya ang mga isyu ng kabuhayan at negosyo. Ngunit kadalasan,kalat-kalat at hindi pormal ang kanilang mga obserbasyon, kung kaya marami ring maling ideya anglumaganap. Itinuring na nagsisimula pa lamang ang sistmatiko at siyentipikong pag-aaral ng ekonomiks sa huling bahagi ng ika-18 siglo
