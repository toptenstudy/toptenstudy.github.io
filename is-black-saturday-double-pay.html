Since 12 friends pay PHP 83.40 for admission to go ice skating and they share the cost equally, notice that then we have to divide the total cost by number of people to get how much each person pay.

In another words, we are looking for next quotient.

Php 83.40÷12=?

Therefore,

Divide integers 83÷12=6

Write down 4 and divide integers 114÷12=9

Multiply 12.9=108. Then subtract.
