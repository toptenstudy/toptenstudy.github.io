6. waht artwork did ansel adams pertain when he said ,there is nothing worse tham a sharp image of a fuzzy concept​


Answer

Adam Ansel was referring to Photography when he quoted “There is nothing worse than a sharp image of a fuzzy concept.”

What Adam meant is that a 'sharp and crispy' photo that contains a lot of details doesn't automatically make a picture look good. He wanted to teach Photographers that you don't need High-end cameras to make a good photograph. For example, this shot taken by British photographer Pennie Smith is low-light, blurry, and grainy. Yet, the photo's composition conveys a strong emotion of Paul Simonon smashing his guitar.

4. The computer does not recognize keystrokes?

REASON: Verify the keyboard is plugged in securely.

TECHNIQUE: Disconnect the keyboard from the computer and reconnect it into the same port. If you have a USB keyboard, you may want to try a different USB port to isolate the issue.

5. The monitor screen is fuzzy?

REASON: A blurry monitor can occur for several reasons such as bad resolution settings, non-matching cable connections or a dirty screen. This can be frustrating if you are unable to read your display properly.

TECHNIQUE: Often the easiest way to fix a monitor blurry is to go into your device's settings. On a Windows PC, click on Advanced scaling settings under Display in Settings. Toggle the switch that reads Let Windows try to fix apps so they're not blurry. Restart and cross your fingers that this fixes the problem.
