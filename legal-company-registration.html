8. Guest arrives and approaches the Front desk for registration. His company has made guaranteed reservations for him but the secretary failed to send the letter of authorization. Will you register him under a company account? What will you do or say?​


A letter of authorization is a requirement that should be considered before agreeing to a request.

A letter of authorization grants a power that enables a certain party to do a particular act which has been agreed between the two. This type of letter is formal, and is usually issued by the party who holds the authority to do so.

In the case of the guest who has made a reservation such as above, in which a letter of authorization is missing, then I would not register the said person under a company account since that would be considered breaking the rule. I would instead demand for the letter and apologize for the inconvenience.

A legal document which summarizes the rights and privleges of bondholders as well as the obligations and commitments of the issuing company is called

ANSWER:

a legal document specifying the rights and obligations of both the issuing firm and the bondholders. It is designed to address all matters related to the bond issue, such as collateral, and call provisions.

An indenture is a legal contract between the issuer and the bondholder. If the bond is called, investors are paid any accrued interest defined within the provision up to the date of recall
