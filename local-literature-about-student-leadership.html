12. FOCUSING THE ROLES AS A STUDENTS. • HOW THEY CONTROL BEING A LEADERSHIP (AS A STUDENT)?​


"STUDENTS LEADERSHIP TIPS"

As a student, there are several ways to take on a leadership role and control it effectively. Here are some tips:

Set a positive example: Be a role model for other students by showing dedication, hard work, and a positive attitude towards learning. Lead by example and inspire others to follow your lead.

Communicate effectively: Effective communication is key to being a good leader. Communicate clearly, actively listen to others, and provide feedback when necessary.

Build relationships: Forge strong relationships with your peers, teachers, and other members of the school community. This will help you establish credibility as a leader and allow you to collaborate with others effectively.

Take initiative: Don't wait for someone else to take the lead. Identify opportunities to make a positive impact and take action. This could be anything from organizing a fundraiser to starting a club or initiative to benefit your school or community.

Be responsible: As a leader, it's important to be responsible and accountable for your actions. Take ownership of your mistakes, and work to rectify them. This will help you earn the trust and respect of others.

Remember, being a leader isn't about having all the answers or being the best at everything. It's about inspiring and empowering others to achieve their full potential and make a positive impact.
