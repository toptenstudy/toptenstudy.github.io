4. Explain the possible meaning/s according to your own interpretation.(Duterte in the Center of Triangle, Elito “Amang Pintor” Circa,1920​


Answer:

In art, triangle represents stability and power.  In the Philippines, a triangle could mean a lot. It may symbolize liberty, equality and fraternity. It could also represent the hierarchy in the government. The title of Circa’s art is “Duterte in the Center of the Triangle”, which could mean that the president has the power to control the country. The image of the crocodile may represent the corrupt officials that has been charged during Duterte’s administration. The eagle may also signify the free-spirit of the President.

Explanation:

“Duterte in the Center of Triangle” is a masterpiece by Amangpintor (Elito Villaflor Circa). He is known advocate of Contemporary Indigenous Philippine Art. Famous for his paintings using indigenous human hair and blood fluids.  

According to Circa, he was impressed with the performance of the president and made him inspired to create the painting. His painting of President Duterte depicts several images of the president. The portrait also includes the presidential seal. The highlight of the masterpiece is the image of President clutching a crocodile by snout.

10. 3. What is an ange whose vertex lies on a circle and its two side are Chords of a circaA Central and Inscribed angle Obtuse angle D. Right angle​


Answer:

inscribed angle

Explanation:

An angle whose vertex lies on a circle and whose sides intercept the circle (the sides contain chords of the circle) is called an inscribed angle. The measure of an inscribed angle is half the measure of the arc it intercepts.
