It was used to obtain the numeric value of a string containing numbers characters.

26. STAR is a rhombus with a diagonal RT.If the m∠STR = 3x − 5 and m∠ART = 21, what is m∠RAT?​


PROBLEM:

STAR is a rhombus with a diagonal RT. If the m∠STR = 3x − 5 and m∠ART = 21, what is m∠RAT?

[tex]\blue {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

SOLUTION:

• Since diagonals bisect vertex angles, [tex] \rm\angle STR \: \: \cong \angle ART[/tex]. This means that angles STR and ART measures the same. No need to solve for the value of x since we already that angle STR measures 21° also.

[tex]\red {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

• Angle ART is just a half of angle SRA. Thus, angle SRA measures 42°.

[tex]\large\tt \angle ART×2=\angle SRA[/tex]

[tex]\large\tt 21 \degree× \: 2=\angle SRA[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt \angle SRA = 42 \degree[/tex]

[tex]\red {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

• Angles SRA and RAT are supplementary since they are what we call the consecutive angles. Thus, they add up to 180°.

[tex]\large \tt \angle SRA +\angle RAT= 180°[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt 42 \degree +\angle RAT= 180°[/tex]

[tex] \large\tt \angle RAT= 180° - \: 42 \degree[/tex]

[tex] \huge \boxed{\large\rm \angle RAT= 138 \degree}[/tex]

[tex]\red {\overline{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: }} [/tex]

ANSWER:The measure of angle RAT is 138°.
