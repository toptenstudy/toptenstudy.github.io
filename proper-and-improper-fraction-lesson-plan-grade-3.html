-is a fraction whose numerator is equal to or greater than its denominator.

10. What is Proper Fraction and Improper Fraction?​


Proper Fraction

a fraction in which the numerator is less or of lower degree than the denominator

Improper Fraction

a fraction whose numerator is equal to, larger than, or of equal or higher degree than the denominator

✍AnswerThe proper fraction is a fraction that are less than one,and the numerator (the top number) is less than the denominator (the bottom number).The improper fraction is a fraction with a numerator that is greater than or equal to the denominator.

12. how to learn proper fractions, improper, mixed fractions and how to turn mixed fractions into improper fractions


Proper fraction is a fraction that has a denominator that's greater than the numerator.

Ex.
[tex] \frac{5}{8} \: \: \: \: \frac{3}{4} \: \: \: \: \frac{14}{17} [/tex]
Improper fraction is a fraction that has a denominator that's less than the numerator.

Ex.
[tex] \frac{14}{3} \: \: \: \: \frac{36}{5} \: \: \: \: \frac{41}{2} [/tex]
Mixed fraction is a fraction that consists a whole number and a fraction.

Ex.
[tex]3 \frac{7}{5} \: \: \: \: 9 \frac{5}{9} \: \: \: \: 10 \frac{1}{2} [/tex]
To turn mixed fractions to improper fractions, multiply the denominator to the whole and add the numerator to the product.

Ex.
[tex]5 \frac{7}{3} = (5 \times 3) + 7 = \frac{22}{3} [/tex]

29. How to change proper fraction to improper fraction.​


Answer:

To turn a mixed number into an improper fraction, multiply the whole number with the denominator of the proper fraction. Add the numerator of the proper fraction to this product to obtain the numerator of the improper fraction. The denominator of the improper fraction will be the same as the denominator of the proper fraction.
