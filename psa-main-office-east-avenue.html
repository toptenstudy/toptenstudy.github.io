2. I went for a walk the other day.I went four avenues East (0.80miles),then twenty four street South (1.20miles),the one avenues West (0.20miles),and finally eight streets North(0.40miles)


1. The distance = 2.6 miles

2. The direction of displacement : 53.06 ° relative to due east

Further explanation

Distance is a scalar quantity that indicates the length of the trajectory that is traveled by an object within a certain interval. Distance has no direction, only has magnitude

Can be simplified distance = totals traveled

Displacement is a vector quantity that shows changes in the position of objects in a certain interval of time. Displacement has magnitude and direction

Can be simplified displacement = distanced traveled from starting point to ending point

He went four avenues east (0.80 miles), then twenty-four streets south (1.20 miles), then one avenue west (0.20 miles), and finally eight streets north (0.40 miles).

1. The distance = total miles traveled

The distance = 0.8 + 1.2 + 0.2 + 0.4 = 2.6 miles

2. resultant displacement (magnitude and direction relative to due east)

To find the magnitude of the displacement we use the phytagorean formula (attachment)

The adjacent: the horizontal distance = 0.8 east-0.2 west = 0.6 miles

The opposite side: the vertical distance = 1.2 south - 0.4 north = 0.8 miles

The displacement = D :

[tex]\rm D=\sqrt{0.6^2+0.8^2}=1~miles[/tex]

The direction of displacement is calculated using tangent

Tan θ = Opposite ÷ Adjacent.

[tex]\rm Tan~\theta=\dfrac{0.8}{0.6}=1.33\\\\\theta=53.06^o~relative~to~due~east[/tex]

17. sample of authorization letter for psa​


A letter of authorization is a document given by one person to another to act on their behalf. The person giving the letter is called the principal while the person being given the document is called the agent. The letter defines the specific task that the agent is supposed to carry out. The principal is legally responsible for the actions of the agent. The agent can be an individual or any other entity.
