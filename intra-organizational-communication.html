1. what does intra organizational conflict means?


Answer:

Intra-organizational conflict means conflict within the organization—for example, between different functions, or between managers and employees. Similarly, intra-organizational communication means communication within the organization—for example, between different teams, or between levels in the hierarchy.

15. How did intra personal and interpersonal communication help you during the community quarantine period? ​


Intrapersonal communication is helpful in keeping one’s mind sane and productive even during the community quarantine period.  It aids in the effective processing of the thoughts and emotions of an individual.  It is also necessary in creating relevant and healthy plans, and reflect on the quarantine experiences one has to go through for some time.

On the other hand, interpersonal communication is highly necessary during the community quarantine period because communication with other people make an individual better and more able in coping with the situation.  Through phone calls, video communications and the use of various social media platforms, people get to share their experiences with others.  It makes the burdens easier to bear knowing that others can relate to each other’s experiences.

28. How to have an effective organizational communication?


Answer:

1. Be aware of your audience

It’s essential that you have a clear understanding of who your audience is when you are engaging in any type of organizational communication.

2. Consider your tone

When it comes to business, how you communicate is as important as what you are trying to say.

3. Ensure clarity in all communication

Clarity is one of the most important aspects of any effective communication strategy.

4. Share relevant content with the right people

One of the most common symptoms of ineffective organizational communication is when employees don’t engage with the content or messages being sent.

5. Choose the right communication channel

With the advancements in technology and the variety of communication tools and apps available, it’s easier than ever to engage with your employees. Choosing the right communication channels to use is an important factor to consider.
