22. what is the axis of symmetry​


Answer:

A parabola symmetry axis is a line around which the parabola is symmetrical. The line of symmetry is vertical while the parabola is vertical. The resulting parabola and the associated symmetry axis are vertical when a quadratic function is graphed in the coordinate plane.

29. What is symmetry and the types of symmetry?​


Answer:

Objects are said to be symmetrical if their pre-image and image have the same size and shape, but are either mirror images of each other or one has been rotated to go in a different direction from the first. There are three basic types of symmetry: reflection symmetry, rotational symmetry, and point symmetry.
