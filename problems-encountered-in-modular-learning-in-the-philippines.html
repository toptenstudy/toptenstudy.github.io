Issues in english proficiency in modular learning in the Philippines

One of the educational developments sparked by the pandemic is modular distance learning. Teaching and learning have been put through an adjustment period as a result of the recent employment of this type of learning delivery. The pressing need for modular classes has been a special challenge in learning. It entails superior instructional design and organization. Most modules published in English pose additional problems and expectations on school learners because English is used as a key medium of instruction in education. As a result, through a qualitative design with a phenomenological perspective, this research aims to investigate the lived experiences of students' learning in responding to English-written modules. The study's participants were eight Grade 6 students who were chosen using a purposive sample technique and some inclusion criteria.

24. essay about The challenge I encountered in modular learning​


The pandemic taught us a lesson we learned the hard way. As a student myself who is used to face to face class it's kind of hard to adapt to the new normal which is modular. The most challenging part of modular learning is that I need to learn and study at my own pace. There are also a lot of distractions if you are doing modular because you tend to procrastinate or do the "mañana habit" because you feel that you have so much time left before the deadline. And not only that, there are some modules that are Self learning, which means that I need to utilize whatever resources I have to educate myself. And I find it overwhelming as a student because supposedly I should be the one learning not educating myself. I mean, how am I supposed to solve an algebra equation or a math problem myself without a proper discussion from a teacher. It may sound that I am complaining, (which I am but with the situation not directly intended to someone) but I also appreciate the effort of our teachers with this new normal. Modular learning may be challenging and overwhelming for me but I'll just use these challenges as a leverage to improve myself and learn to manage my time wisely. There may be mistakes and shortcomings along the way but everyday is a learning process.
