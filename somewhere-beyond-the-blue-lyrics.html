13. Select the phrase of word that is incorrect" They're on there way to somewhere far from here.a. Thereb. to c. somewhered. They're​​


In the given sentence, the word "there" was used incorrectly. It should be replaced with "their," which is a term used to describe something associated with people or things mentioned before or in the same sentence.

Let us analyze the given sentence. Here, we are tasked to determine which word was used incorrectly.

[They're] on [there] way [to] [somewhere] far from here.

"They're" is short for "they are," which is used to describe a single person or a group of persons. For example:

They are happy.They're part of the group.

"There" is used when referring to a particular place or area. For example:

I used to have lunch there.There she is!

"To" expresses motion, direction, purpose, or intention. For example:

The letter was sent to the driver.Let us go to the northwest corner.To those who are unaware...

"Somewhere" is used to describe an unknown location. For example:

He has got to be here somewhere.I have to find somewhere I belong.
