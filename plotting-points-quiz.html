PROBLEM

If justin scored 75% on a 20-point quiz what was his raw score?

ANSWER Justin's raw score is 15 points

FORMULA

[tex]percentage \: = \: \frac{n \times 100}{y} [/tex]

SOLUTION

[tex]75 \% \times 20[/tex]

Reduce the fraction 75/100 to lowest terms by extracting and canceling out 25.

[tex]\frac{3}{4}\times 20 [/tex]

Express 3/4 x 20 as a single fraction.

[tex]\frac{3\times 20}{4} [/tex]

Multiply 3 and 20 to get 60.

[tex]\frac{60}{4} [/tex]

Divide 60 by 4 to get 15.

[tex]x = 15[/tex]

ANSWER 15 POINTS

SOLUTION

First change 75% into a decimal which is 0.75.

Then multiply 0.75 x 20 is equals to 15
