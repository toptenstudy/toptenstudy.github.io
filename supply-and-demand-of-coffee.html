Answer:

The cost of the coffee is ₱8.

Explanation:

Let:

x + 17 = cost of the fries

2x = cost of the sandwich

x = cost of the coffee

x + 17 + 2x + x = 49

4x + 17 = 49

4x = 49 - 17

4x = 32

x = ₱8 <= cost of the coffee
