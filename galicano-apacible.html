16. Identify the adverbs in the sentences bye underlining it and write in the apace provided the types of adverbs used.​


Answer:

ANSWERS

ADVERB OF FREQUENCY 1. I never talk to strangers.

Never is the adverb of frequency and talk is the verb it modfies.

ADVERB OF DEGREE 2. I can’t drink my milk since it was extremely hot.

Extremely is the adverb of degree and hot is the adjective it modifies.

ADVERB OF TIME 3. I will call you later.

Later is the adverb of time and call is the verb it modifies.

ADVERB OF MANNER 4. Mr. Enriquez strongly disagrees in that argument.

Strongly is the adverb of manner and disagrees is the verb it modifies.

ADVERB OF PLACE 5. The ship sailed southward encountering huge waves.

Southward is the adverb of place and sailed is the verb it modifies.

====================

Adverbs modifies or describes a verb, an adjective or another adverb. It provides information on how, how often, when, where and what extent an action takes place.

Adverb of Manner answers the question "How?"

Adverb of Time answers the question "When?"

Adverb of Place answers the question "Where?"

Adverb of Frequency answers the question "How often?"

Adverb of Degree answers the question "To what extent?"
