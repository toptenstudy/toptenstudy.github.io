21. The product of two positive consecutive even integer is 168. Assuming the smaller integer to be x, frame an equation for the statement. A. x (x + 2) = 168 B. x2 = 168 C. 2x = 168 D. x (2x) = 168​


Answer:

A. x(x+2) = 168

Step-by-step explanation:

The product of two positive consecutive even integer is 168.

Smaller even integer: xSecond even integer, add 2: (x + 2)Product: 168Operation: Multiplication

Equation:

(x)(x+2) = 168

To solve for x, the first even integer:

x² + 2x - 168 = 0

Factor:

(x + 14)(x - 12) = 168

Equate each factor to 0:

x + 14 = 0

x = -14

x - 12 = 0

x = 12

Smaller even integer x: 12

Second even integer: 12 + 2 = 14

The consecutive even  integers are 12 and 14.

Check:

(12)(14) = 168

168 = 168  (True)
