MATERIALS USED AT HOME AND IN SCHOOL

1. PEN/PENCIL. It is useful because both teacher and pupil used it to take down notes, check papers and label things. It is harmful because its tip is usually sharp that it could bleed one’s skin or flesh. Sometimes, pupils fight and they use pen/pencil to harm others.Pen/Pencil is also used in studying at home or writing notes for reminders in the house.

2. PAIR OF SCISSORS. This is used to cut things inside the house and is usually used in art works in the school. It is harmful too because its blades are sharp that when one accidentally cut our skin, it could wound and bleed.

3. LAPTOP. We use it to connect to the internet and access to information that we need in our lessons or for personal interests only. We also use it to watch movies saved in a flash drive or just in any website.It is harmful because the screen has rays that may harm our eyes especially if we use it for a long time.

4. ALCOHOL. These days, we really need alcohol to constantly wash away every virus we may get from touching random things. This is why we carry alcohol in  the house and even when at school. It is harmful if we swallow it. The chemical composing it may poison us to death.

5. ELECTRIC FAN. We need it anywhere as long as there is electricity to soothe ourselves from hot surroundings. It is somehow harmful if while the propeller is turning and we accidentally or intentionally put our hands inside, it can cut our skin.
