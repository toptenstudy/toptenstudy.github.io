19. What is the mark down price at 20% off of Php 900? ​


What's 20 percent-off ₱900?

Replacing the given values in formula (a) we have:

Amount Saved = Original Price x Discount in Percent / 100. So,

Amount Saved = 900 x 20 / 100

Amount Saved = 18000 / 100

Amount Saved = ₱180 (answer).

In other words, a 20% discount for a item with original price of ₱900 is equal to ₱180 (Amount Saved).

Note that to find the amount saved, just multiply it by the percentage and divide by 100.
