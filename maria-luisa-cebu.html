5. The secret language by luisa igloria


The secret language by Luisa Igloria

This poem makes me realize how greatly we, Filipinos, are affected by the Americans or the colonialists. They have made a standard in which everyone follows. A representation of this is the part when the persona mentions coiling her hair and braiding it for the fair stranger. The effects of these foreigners to the persona, or us, are also very deeply rooted that even the way we talk is similar to theirs. But because of these effects on the persona, or us, it gives the feeling of incarceration. And that deep inside us, there still remains a longing for our true Filipino identities.
