19. On April 1, 2020, Cabadbaran company borrowed 5,000,000 and signed a 2-year note bearing interest at 12% per annum compunded anually. interest is payable in full at maturity


The interest will be payable fully in December 31, 2021 is 992,000.

The interest accrued by May 31, 2021 (1 year since April 1, 2020) =

= 5,000,000 x 12% = 600,000

Since the interest compounds yearly, the interest accrued by December 31, 2021 (Estimation on the year-end)

=  (5,600,000 x 12% x 7/12) + 600,000

= 392,000 + 600,000

= 992,000

COMPOUND INTEREST

Compound interest indicates that the interest earned will earn additional interest in the subsequent period. Therefore, the previously accumulated interest must be included in the interest computation for the second component.

Compound interest accelerates the growth of your wealth. Compound interest causes an amount of money to increase at a quicker rate than simple interest since you earn returns on your initial investment as well as rewards at the conclusion of each compounding period. This means you do not need to save as much money to achieve your objectives.
