The company decided to institutionalize its design process by building an innovation center. SELCO also added products that provided energy solutions beyond solar. Some within the company were hoping the company would go “deeper” and look at designing solutions for even poorer members of the Indian population. Others were hoping that the company would go “wider” and expand beyond its current geographical areas in Karnataka and Gujarat. 3. what is the entrepreneurial habits did Harish Hande exhibit in handling SELCO? Justify your answer.


SELCO is an Indian company based in Bangalore engaged in providing solar energy solutions to rural areas of India for a more sustainable and comfortable living standard. Harish Hande is one of the co-founders of SELCO.

What entrepreneurial habits did Harish Hande exhibit in handling SELCO?Tenacity

As with any company that is the pioneer in its field, its first problem will always be financing costs. Hande was able to seek out potential investors and persuade them to fund the company and allow it to scale up and improve its products.

Intrinsic motivation and drive

Harish Hande envisioned a better standard of living for the rural poor of India starting at what he knew best, energy and its unlimited source. The company's main focus was making sure that the Indians living at the poverty level would be given the basic need of electricity first. Profit was only the second goal of the company he founded.

A positive mental attitude

While solar energy addresses the issue of energy waste, pollution, and sustainability, he knew that the poor may not willingly embrace solar energy solutions due to their upfront cost. This did not stop him from moving forward with his plans. He thought of ways to help the poor afford solar energy products without compromising the quality of his own products and services.

A creative mindset

With the problem of having a target market that is not able to afford their products, Hande thought that simply selling to them will not suffice. The rural poor must be educated about the benefits of solar energy. Once they are aware of its benefits, they will then be presented with programs that will help them earn the capital they need to repay the costs. Hande not only sold to them, but he also helped them earn money so that they can afford premium products that will last a lifetime. This proves to be better than lowering the cost of solar energy products which can lead to decline in quality.

Heat transfer is an exchange of thermal energy between two objects. The rate of heat transfer depends upon the temperatures of each entity and the medium through which the thermal energy is being transferred. In cooking, heat transfer refers to heating your food items through a cooking appliance, such as a stove, fryer, microwave, or oven.

Heat transfer is a very important aspect of the cooking process. Heating food destroys potentially harmful bacteria and other microorganisms, which makes food safe to eat and easier to digest.
