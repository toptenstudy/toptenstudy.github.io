26. A foundation is holding a fund-raising campaign in a form of a raffle. A raffle ticket costs 120 php andthere are 5,000 tickets to be sold. The ticket drawn in the raffle will win for its holder the price of 100,000 php.Compute the expected profit or loss for joining this raffle.​


Answer:

-100

Step-by-step explanation:

x                                               P(x)                                x*P(x)

100,000-120=99,880               1/5000=0.0002            19.976

-120                                           4999/5000=0.9998    -119.976

ANSWER: 19.976-119.976=-100

Joining the raffle has an expected loss of 100 php (or -100).

120 PHP has to be deducted from the winning (100,000) to account for the amount you spent for the ticket. In case of loss, you have to account for the price of ticket as well; hence, -120.  

With 5000 tickets, you have a 0.0002 chance of winning and 0.9998 chance of losing.

Multiply the outcomes with their respective probabilities, then add the two outcomes.
