12. Mr. Golv is practicing his jiu-jitsu drill where he does 5 guard passes and 2 kimura arm locks. A guard pass takes G seconds, and a kimura arm lock takes K seconds. Which expressions can we use to describe the number of seconds it takes Mr. Golv to complete his jiu-jitsu drill 7 times? I need two answers.


Answer:

7(5G + 2K) or 35G + 14K

Step-by-step explanation:

Let G = guard pass in seconds

Let K = kimura arm lock in seconds

You ask for an expression to describe his drill in 7 seconds

So your expression will be:

7(5G + 2K)

= 35G + 14K
