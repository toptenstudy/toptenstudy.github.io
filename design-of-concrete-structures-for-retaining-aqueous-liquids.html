17. • How do aqueous solutions differ from gaseous, liquid, and solid solutions?​


Answer:

Aqueous solutions are solutions wherein water is the solvent while gaseous, liquid, and solid solutions are solutions wherein a gas, a liquid other than water, and a solid is the solvent, respectively.
