30. discuss budget preparation in the philippines​


Four phases comprise the Philippine budget process, specifically:

(1) Budget Preparation;

(2) Budget Legislation;

(3) Budget Execution; and

(4) Accountability. Each phase is distinctly separate from the others but they overlap in the implementation of the budget during the budget year.
