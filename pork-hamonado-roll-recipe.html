8. recipe of pork adobo and sinigang


Pork adobo

Ingredients

Pork

Garlic minced or crushed

Dried bay leaves (Laurel leaf)

Vinegar

Soy sauce

Peppercorn (Paminta)

Water

Salt

Procedure

First, combine the pork, soy sauce, and garlic then marinate for at least 1 hour

Next, heat the pot and put the marinated pork and cook it for a few minutes

Pour remaining marinade

Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour

Put in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes

Add salt

Sinigang

Ingredients

Pork

Kang-kong

Sitaw

Kamatis

Green chili

Oil

Water

Sibuyas

Gabi

Sinigang mix

Procedure

Initin ang kawali at ilagay ang oil

Gisahin ang sibuyas

Ilagay ang karne at lutuin hanggang maging "light brown" ang kulay

Ilagay ang mga sangkap at ihalo

Ibuhos ang tubig at pakuluan

Ilagay ang gabi at kamatis saka kumulo ng 40 minuto o hanggang lumambot ang baboy

Ilagay ang sinigang mix and chili

Ilagay ng sitaw (at iba pang gulay kung mayroon man) at kumulo ng 5 hanggang 8 minuto

Ilagay ang kangkong, patayin ang apoy, at takpan ang kaldero. Hayaang maluto ang kangkong gamit ang natitirang init sa kaldero.

26. 1.oven beef brisket. garnishes: 1. 2. 3. 2.pork spare ribs oven. garnishes: 1. 2. 3. 3.beef marcon dish. garnishes: 1. 2. 3. 4.pork hamonado dish. garnishes. 1. 2. 3.​


Answer:

Oven beef brisket1. Preheat the oven or smoker to 225 degrees F.

2. Trim the fat evenly across the top to 1/4-inch thick.

3. Season the brisket liberally with the seasoning rub. Cook in the oven or smoker until the internal temperature on an instant- read meat thermometer reaches 175 degrees F, about 6 to 8 hours.

Pork spare ribs oven

1. Add a liquid element, like olive oil or mustard, to allow the dry rub to stick.

2. Dry Rub – Add your favorite Dry Rub that will add flavor while the ribs smoke and caramelize.

Beef marcon dish

1. Cool the morcon in the sauce.

2. Chill overnight to make slicing easier and neater. Lift the morcon off the sauce.

3. Reheat the sauce in a small sauce pan until simmering.

Pork hamonado dish

1. In a large wok, add 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and saute until golden brown for 1-2 minutes.

2. Then add the pork rolls. Sear it on all sides until it becomes golden brown for about 5-8 minutes.Pour half of the marinade juices in the pan and let it simmer until sauce becomes thick in texture for  around 30-40 minutes.

3. Don’t forget to turn over the pork rolls from one side to another every 10-15minutes.When sauce becomes thick, separate the pork rolls  and transfer on a serving platter. Gently cut the string around the rolls and slice into serving pieces.
