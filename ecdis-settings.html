4. What appropriate scale chart from paper chart folio, Electronic Chart System (ECS) or Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS)​


The most appropriate scale chart for a paper chart folio, Electronic Chart System (ECS), or Electronic Chart Display and Information System (ECDIS) would depend on the specific application and needs of the vessel. Generally, a chart scale of 1:50,000 to 1:150,000 is recommended for coastal navigation, while a chart scale of 1:500,000 to 1:3,000,000 is suitable for offshore navigation. Electronic Chart Display and Information Systems (ECDIS) are capable of displaying charts of almost any scale.

13. What are the legal aspects and requirements of ecdis?


Answer:

The amended SOLAS regulation V/19 requires all newly built passenger ships of 500 gross tonnage and upwards, as well as newly built cargo ships of 3,000 gross tonnage and upwards engaged on international voyages to be fitted with ECDIS.
