My greatest philosophical belief in life is that we should treat anybody, with deepest respect and love. We're not born to create hatred. We are born for us to interact and correlate with other human beings, to give joy and inspiration, and to be a source of peace and hope to anyone who needs it. A life full of gratitude is a life worth-living. When you plant something that is negative, it will bear a negative result. Instead of delving into fights and arguments, learn to lower your pride and accept that you sometimes are not perfect and forgive someone you consider as an enemy. Loving your enemies is better than hating them. You will just get stressed about it.

Just be contented about your life, treat others the way you want to be treated and you will live a happy life.

2. What is digital identity?

A digital identity is your identity online. It involves all activities you make.

Examples are your browsing history, passwords, usernames, social media accounts, birth date, online purchases, the sites you visit regularly, the connections you make, download history and many more.

3. What is digital self?

A digital self is none other than yourself, but in an online platform. It is yourself on the internet. It is about how you portray yourself and how you interact with others, on the internet. For example, the profiles you make are your digital selves. Your Instagram account is your digital self.
