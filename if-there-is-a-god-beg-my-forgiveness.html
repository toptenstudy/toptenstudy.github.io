MAKE A PRAYER ASKING GOD FOR FORGIVENESS FOR ALL YOUR SINS AND EXPRESS YOUR WILLINGNESS TO FORGIVE OTHERS.

We only need to be saved one time. If we repent of our sins and accept Jesus as our personal savior, we will be saved once and always. We will be children of God forever, no matter what. But what about the sins we commit after we are saved? The answer is that God has already for given us for all sins we will ever commit. However, He does still want us to repent of the those sins and ask forgiveness. This not only helps us be more conscious of when we sin, but it also helps us learn not to sin. The first thing is to realize that not every weakness is a sin. If you study the New Testament, you will get a better idea of what is sin and what is not. An inappropriate thought is not a sin unless you dwell on it or act on it. Being tempted to sin is not a sin unless you act on it. With time, the Holy Spirit will teach you what is sinful and what is not. For me, I believe it’s always good to ask forgiveness every time I approach God in prayer. That way, if there's anything between God and me that shouldn’t be there, I will have laid it at God’s feet where he says he will wipe it away. Then I can approach God sinless. In the end, I believe the Holy Spirit will guide you in this. If you pray often and study the Bible, I believe the Holy Spirit will lead us to act exactly how God wants us to. And always remember, God already knows our desires, needs and weaknesses even before we pray. And according to the New Testament, God doesn’t want mindless repetition nor rituals when we pray. So approach him as a father and confidant. He will do the rest.
