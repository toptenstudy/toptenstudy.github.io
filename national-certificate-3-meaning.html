13. what is certificate of inspection mean?


Certificate of inspection. A document certifying that merchandise (such as perishable goods) was in good condition at the time of inspection, usually immediately prior to shipment. Pre-shipment inspection is requirement for importation of goods into many developing countries.

14. Why learners must continuously reflect on their study skills,study strategies and study styles for the attainment of the National Senior Certificate


Answer:

Learners must continuously reflect on their study skills, study strategies and study styles for the attainment of the National Senior Certificate in order to:

1. Pass assessment for learning

Teachers make professional judgement on learners’ performance based on the quality of their works. The quality of work depends on how learners receive and use that knowledge taught or shared to them by their teachers. Therefore, in order for the learners to deliver perfectly what they’ve learned for the past years and pass the assessment to attain the National Senior Certificate a student must continuously reflect on their study skills, strategies and styles. It also helps students not only to remember what has been taught but also to be able find improvements for their learning.  

2. Learn and avoid mistakes

There are study styles that doesn’t work for a certain person. For example, a student may easily learn things through reading but not in listening or vice versa. Therefore, to be able to catch up with the lessons a student must know what’s the most suitable study strategies for him to use in order to pass any exams in any subjects which leads to passing the National Senior Certificate.  Furthermore, it will help students not to repeatedly do the same mistakes which delays learning.

3. Open more opportunities

The higher your exam results the better opportunities you’ll have. Improving yourself by deeply reflecting on what’s the best method for you as a student to pass a certain subject resulting to high grades would open a path for a good employment, internships and admissions to higher college or universities.
