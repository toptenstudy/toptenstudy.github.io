Explanation:
Markup refers to the difference between the selling price of a good or service and its cost. It is expressed as a percentage above the cost. In other words, it is the premium over the total cost of the good or service that provides the seller with a profit.

13. In 2014, Gross profit of a company was Php2,000,000 while its expenses were Php450,000. In 2015, Gross Profit of the company was Php3,500,000 while expenses were Php830,000. If corporate income tax rate is 30%, what is the total net income from 2014 up to 2015?


Net income = Gross Profit - Expenses - Income tax
Income tax = (Gross Profit - Expenses) * Tax Rate

Using this formula, the net income for 2014 and 2015 is as follows:

2014
Net income = P2,000,000 - P450,000 - [(P2,000,000 - P450,000) * 30%] = P1,085,000

2015
Net income = P3,500,000 - P830,000 - [(P3,500,000 - P830,000) * 30%] = P1,869,000

Therefore, total net income from 2014 up to 2015 is P2,954,000.

28. Define and differentiate gross and domestic product and Gross national product​


DEFINITION

GDP

-GDP stands for Gross Domestic Product and measures the monetary value of all imports and services produced inside nations geographic borders during a specific period.

GNP

-GNP stands for Gross National Product and measures the monetary value of output and services produced by all citizens of a country both domestically and abroad. However, it does not include the output of foreign residents.

DIFFERENTIATE

GDP

-The strength of the country’s domestic economy.

GNP

-How the residents are contributing to the country’s economy.

29. Define and differentiate gross and domestic product and Gross national product​


Answer:

An Overview. Gross domestic product (GDP) is the value of a nation's finished domestic goods and services during a specific time period.
