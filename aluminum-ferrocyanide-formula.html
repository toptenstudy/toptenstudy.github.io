formula mass of Al2(SO4)3 is 342.3. The relative formula mass of an ionic compound is the ratio of the average mass of one formula unit of the ionic compound to the mass of 1/12 the mass of a carbon-12 atom. Thus, to obtain the formula mass of Al2(SO4)3, just add up the relative atomic masses of every atom in one formula unit.

29. How many formula units are there in 237.64 g of aluminum hydroxide?


Answer:

no. of formula units = 1.8347 × 10²⁴ formula units

Explanation:

Calculating the molar mass of Al(OH)3

molar mass = (26.98 × 1) + (16.00 × 3) + (1.008 × 3)

molar mass = 78.00 g

Calculating the number of formula units of Al(OH)3

no. of formula units = 237.64 g × (1 mol / 78.00 g) × (6.022 × 10²³ formula units / 1 mol)

no. of formula units = 1.8347 × 10²⁴ formula units
