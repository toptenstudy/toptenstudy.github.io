27. In dealing with customer complaints, what is the first thing you do for guests for them to explain their problems which is causing the complaint? Select one: a. Answer b. Ask c. Understand d. Acknowledge


Answer:

the correct answer is the letter C Acknowledge

Explanation:

When dealing with customer complaints, it is important to first acknowledge the guest's concern. This means showing empathy and understanding towards their situation. Once the guest feels heard and understood, you can then ask them to explain their problem in more detail. This allows you to gather all the necessary information to address their complaint effectively. Therefore, the correct answer is C - Acknowledge.
