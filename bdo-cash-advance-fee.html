17. The business recorded an advance collection. The entry includes: a.Debit Unearned Incomeb. Credit Cash c. Debit Service Incomed. Debit Cash ​


The entry for recording an advance collection would be:

a. Debit Unearned Income

a. Debit Unearned Incomeb. Credit Cash

This is because when a business receives an advance payment from a customer, it is considered as unearned income as the business has not yet provided the goods or services for which the payment was made. Therefore, the cash received would be credited, and the unearned income account would be debited to reflect the liability owed to the customer.
