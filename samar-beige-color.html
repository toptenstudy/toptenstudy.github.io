A baníg (pronounced [bɐˈnɪɡ] buh-NIG), is a traditional handwoven mat of the Philippines predominantly used as a sleeping mat or a floor mat. Depending on the region of the Philippines, the mat is made of buri[1] (palm), pandanus or sea grass leaves.
