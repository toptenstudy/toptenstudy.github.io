11. The hula hoop moves around your body in a circular motion. What force pulls the object downward?


Answer:

GRAVITY

Explanation:

Friction between the hoop and the hula hooper's clothes and the air will slow the hoop's spinning down. However, friction also helps to keep the hula hoop up on the hula hooper's body while the force of the hula hoop's mass pulls it down (this downward force is due to gravity).
