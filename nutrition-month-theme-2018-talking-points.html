25. Slogan for theme of 2017 nutrition month


 “Healthy Diet, Gawing Habit - For Life!”, is the theme, quote, or nutrition month slogan 2017  of our nation-wide nutrition awareness month.

This theme of 43rd nutrition month of the country aims to promote healthy diet among Filipinos as well as spread health awareness in the whole archipelago. (If you’re looking for slogan for nutrition month English or slogan about nutrition 2017 in English, this answer might help.)

July 1 to July 31, 2017 is the annual campaign for Philippine’s Nutrition Month pursuant to the Presidential Decree No. 491 - Nutrition Act of the Philippines organized and implemented by the National Nutrition Council (NNC).

With the theme “Healthy Diet, Gawing Habit – for Life! aims to promote healthy diets that would contribute to decrease the incidences of overweight, obesity and non-communicable diseases; to teach the Filipinos how to better distinguish between the healthy foods and unhealthy foods for better nourishment choices; and to encourage the  country’s food industries to produce, make, sell, and offer healthier food options to the consumers. The food industry includes farmers, producers, distributors, and food establishments.

29. opening remarks speech about nutrition month 2018


Opening remarks for nutrition month 2018 / Talking points nutrition month 2018:


Good day, everyone!


I welcome you all to our celebration for 2018’s nutrition month!


The National Nutrition Council or NNC for short was actually the national agency or council that was tasked to help encourage, promote, and support greater awareness of the significance of nutrition among Filipinos in our country, Philippines.  


In fact, the Nutrition Month theme this 2018 is “Ugaliing magtanim, Sapat na nutrisyon aanihin!” and this might be the best slogan for a nutrition month celebration.

 

The said theme or slogan for nutrition month here in the Philippines apparently aims to improve the Filipino’s healthy food consumption as well as their food security. Also, this year’s objective is for the citizens to be self sufficient as much as possible especially with updating knowledge when it comes to backyard gardening and agriculture.  


I would also want to add the fact that this theme or slogan of nutrition month perhaps advocates protecting our Philippine’s ancestral lands as well as its peoples, the indigenous people like the Lumads, Aetas, etc.


For more information about the NNC, this council was created by Presidential Decree 491 during 1974 and it is acting as the country’s highest policy making as well as coordinating body on nutrition in our country. Such organization is chaired by the secretary of health and the board must be composed of ten government organizations. The organizations are the following: Department of Health, Department of Agriculture, Department of Interior and Local Government, Department of Trade and Industry, Department of Labor and Employment, Department of Social Welfare and Development, Department of Science and Technology, Department of Budget Management, National Economic Development Authority and most of all Department of Education.  


So here we are, celebrating nutrition for the whole month with an advocacy to not just be healthy Filipinos but also wise when it comes to agriculture and livelihood.
