26. The children needed flaglets for their Anti-drug rap contest. they made 3 flags of 3/4 metre of cloth. what part of a meter was used for a flaglet?helps ... patulongtopic: dividing a fraction by a whole number.​


The children needed flaglets for their Anti - drug Rap contest. they made 3 flags of 3/4 metre of cloth. What part of a meter was used for a flaglet?

Solution:

[tex]\frac{3}{4}[/tex] ÷ 3 = [tex]\frac{3}{4}[/tex] × [tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex]

= [tex]\frac{3}{12}[/tex]

= [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex]

The answer is [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex]

Each flag was [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] meter.

→ to divide a fraction by whole number, you must multiply the dividend by the reciprocal of the divisor. Reduce the answer to lowest terms (if necessary).
