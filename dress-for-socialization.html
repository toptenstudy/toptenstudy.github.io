2. What are the 5 W's that need to be consider in the selection of dress materials to be sure that the wearer is socially, politically, and aesthetically comfortable?​


Answer:

The 5 W's that need to be considered in the selection of dress materials to ensure that the wearer is socially, politically, and aesthetically comfortable are:

Who: Consider the individual who will be wearing the dress. Their personal preferences, body type, cultural background, and age should all be taken into account.

What: Consider the occasion for which the dress will be worn. Is it a formal event or a casual one? Will it be worn during the day or at night? What is the dress code?

Where: Consider the location where the dress will be worn. Different climates and cultural norms may affect the choice of fabric and style.

When: Consider the time of year and the current fashion trends. Some fabrics and colors may be more suitable for certain seasons or occasions.

Why: Consider the purpose of the dress. Is it for personal expression or to conform to social expectations? Is it to make a political statement or to show support for a particular cause?

By considering these 5 W's, the selection of dress materials can be made in a way that ensures the wearer is socially, politically, and aesthetically comfortable.
