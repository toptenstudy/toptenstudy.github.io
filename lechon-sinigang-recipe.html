1. recipe of pork adobo and sinigang


Pork adobo

Ingredients

Pork

Garlic minced or crushed

Dried bay leaves (Laurel leaf)

Vinegar

Soy sauce

Peppercorn (Paminta)

Water

Salt

Procedure

First, combine the pork, soy sauce, and garlic then marinate for at least 1 hour

Next, heat the pot and put the marinated pork and cook it for a few minutes

Pour remaining marinade

Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour

Put in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes

Add salt

Sinigang

Ingredients

Pork

Kang-kong

Sitaw

Kamatis

Green chili

Oil

Water

Sibuyas

Gabi

Sinigang mix

Procedure

Initin ang kawali at ilagay ang oil

Gisahin ang sibuyas

Ilagay ang karne at lutuin hanggang maging "light brown" ang kulay

Ilagay ang mga sangkap at ihalo

Ibuhos ang tubig at pakuluan

Ilagay ang gabi at kamatis saka kumulo ng 40 minuto o hanggang lumambot ang baboy

Ilagay ang sinigang mix and chili

Ilagay ng sitaw (at iba pang gulay kung mayroon man) at kumulo ng 5 hanggang 8 minuto

Ilagay ang kangkong, patayin ang apoy, at takpan ang kaldero. Hayaang maluto ang kangkong gamit ang natitirang init sa kaldero.

22. maghanap ng isang recipe sa internet ng pagkaing pinoy sinigang na bangus​


Answer:

Sinigang Na Bangus Recipe

This is a recipe for sinigang na bangus

Course Fish

Cuisine FilipinoPrep Time10 minutes

Cook Time30 minutes

Total Time 40 minutes

Servings 3

Author Vanjo Merano

Ingredients

1. 2 lbs. bangus milkfish, cleaned and sliced

2. 1 40g pack Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix (Original)

3. 1 bunch fresh kangkong leaves

4. 12 pieces sitaw snake beans, cut into 2 inch pieces

5. 6 to 8 pieces okra

6. 2 pieces long green pepper siling pansigang

7. 1 medium tomato wedged

8. 1 medium yellow onion wedged

9. 2 ½ tablespoons fish sauce

10. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

11. 2 quarts water

Instructions

1. Heat a cooking pot. Pour water into it.

2. Add tomato and onion. Let boil

3. Add bangus. Cover and cook in medium heat between 8 to 12 minutes.

4.Add knorr sinigang sa sampaloc mix. Stir cook for 2 minutes.

5. Put the long green pepper into the pot. Add snake beans and okra. Stir cover and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.

6. Add fish sauce and ground black pepper stir

7. Put the kangkomg leaves into the pot. Cover the pot and turn the heat off. Let it stay in 5 minutes.

8. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.

9. Share and enjoy

Nutrition

Serving 3g
