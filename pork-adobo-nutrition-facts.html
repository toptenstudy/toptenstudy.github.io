23. 1. cook pork-chicken adobo​


Answer:

Here's a recipe for pork-chicken adobo:

Ingredients:

1 lb. chicken (cut into serving pieces)

1 lb. pork belly (cut into cubes)

1/2 cup vinegar

1/2 cup soy sauce

1 head garlic (minced)

1 onion (chopped)

3-4 bay leaves

1 tsp. whole peppercorns

1/2 cup water

2 tbsp. cooking oil

Instructions:

1. In a large pan, heat the cooking oil and sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.

2 Add the pork and chicken pieces to the pan and stir-fry for about 5 minutes or until lightly browned.

3. Add the vinegar, soy sauce, bay leaves, and peppercorns to the pan. Stir well to combine.

4. Pour in the water and bring the mixture to a boil. Once boiling, lower the heat and let it simmer for 30 minutes or until the meat is tender.

5. Stir occasionally to prevent the meat from sticking to the bottom of the pan.

6. Once the meat is tender, increase the heat and let the mixture boil for a few more minutes to thicken the sauce.

7. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preference. You can add more vinegar or soy sauce if desired.

8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy your delicious pork-chicken adobo!

29. how to Cook pork adobo


Ingredients are:
* 1 tablespoon oil
* 1 onion , peeled and sliced thinly
* 1 head garlic, peeled and minced
* 1-1/2 pounds pork shoulder or belly, cut into 2 inch cubes
* 1 cup vinegar
* 1/2 cup soy sauce
* 1 cup water
* 2 bay leaves
* salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a pot over medium heat , heat oil. Add onions and garlic and cook until limp. Add pork and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned and juices run clear
2. Add vinegar and bring to a boil, uncovered and without stirring, for about 3 to 5 min. Add soy sauce, water and bay leaves . Continue to boil for about 2 to 3 min. Lower heat, cover and continue to cook until meat is tender and sauce is reduced. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve Hot..

Paraan ng Pagluluto:

1. Igisa ang bawang hanggang sa medyo brown na ito saka ilagay ang karne ng baboy, lagyan ng asin at paminta ayon sa iyong panlasa, sangkutsain ito ng 3 minuto.

2. Ilagay ang toyo at suka, hintaying kumulo saka haluin, dahil kung hahaluin ito na hindi pa kumukulo ang suka nakakaapekto ito sa texture ng karne.Pakuluin ng 5 minuto.

3. Ilagay ang tubig, ituloy ang pagpapakulo, hanggang sa malapit ng lumambot at haluin paminsan minsan. Kapag malambot na ay patayin ang apoy, hanguin ang carne at patitisin ang sabaw. Ilagay sa isang tabi ang kalderong may sabaw.

4. Mag-init ng mantika, prituhin ang adobo hanggang sa maging medyo brown. Kapag tapos na ang prosesong ito ay ibalik sa kaldero na pinagkuhaan nito, sa katamtamang init ng apoy ay isalang ito hanggang sa tuluyan ng lumambot ang karne at lumapot ang sabaw ng adobo.
