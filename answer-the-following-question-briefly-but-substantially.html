6. Answer the following questions substantially.(pls. Explain your answer briefly) 1. What benefits do we get from electromagnetic induction? 2. How are generators useful during power outages?​


Answer:

1. What benefits do we get from electromagnetic induction?

The chief advantage of electromagnetic induction is the ability to convert mechanical energy directly into electrical energy. Virtually any source of mechanical energy can be used, provided there is enough available. Since many other forms of energy can be converted to mechanical energy, it is a very versatile means of producing electricity. That versatility also enables us to place the generator in a convenient spot and bring energy to it (as with a diesel-driven generator) as well as place it at the energy source (as with a hydroelectric dam).

The primary advantage of using an electromagnet over a permanent magnet is that you can control its magnetic field. You turn on and off the device's magnetic field, and with many electromagnets, you can even control the power of the magnetic field.

1. Social studies is an interdisciplinary academic field that deals with the study of human society and social relationships. It encompasses a range of subjects including history, geography, political science, economics, sociology, psychology, and anthropology. The goal of social studies education is to help students develop a sense of civic responsibility, understand the complexities of society, and prepare them to participate in a democratic society.

2. The features of Philippine social studies include:

A focus on Philippine history, culture, and government

An examination of the relationships between individuals, communities, and society as a whole

An exploration of the ways in which Philippine society has been shaped by its past and by external forces

A study of the social, economic, and political issues facing the Philippines today, such as poverty, inequality, and political corruption

An emphasis on the development of critical thinking skills, so that students can analyze and evaluate information and form their own opinions.
