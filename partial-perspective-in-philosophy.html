14. what is holistic perspective and partial perspective??


No matter what subject or field that is being pertained in this question, a holistic perspective is a wide point of view (POV) of an individual toward a certain issue or topic. This perspective may also be considered to be a “strategic” POV wherein all factors are taken into consideration.

 

Meanwhile, a partial perspective is the POV wherein only a certain number of factors are taken into consideration and not everything, which are considered non-essential, are taken into account.
