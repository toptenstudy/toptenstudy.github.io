4. 5. In a classroom, there are nine 15-watt fluorescent lamps and five 150-watt electric fans operating for 8 hours on a regular school day. How many KW are consumed in one school week? A 35.4 KW B. 354 kW c. 3540 kW D. 35400 kW​


Answer:

A. 35.4kW

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:
We multiply five 150watt electric fans and nine 15 watt lamps:
150 * 5 = 750      15 * 9 = 135

Now we add them both: 750 + 135 = 885

Then we multiply it to the 8 hours: 885 * 8 = 7080

Then we multiply by the number of school days which is 5:

7080 * 5 = 35400

And then we convert it to kilowatts
= 35.4kW

26. 1. A 24-V storage battery is charged by a current of 12 A for half in hour. How much power is required to change the battery at this rate? a. 0.12 kWb. 0.18 kWc. 0.22 kWd. 0.29 kW​


Answer:

P = V×I

P = 24V × 12A

P = 288 Watts

Convert 288 Watts (W) to kilowatts (kW) by dividing to 1000.

288 ÷ 1000 = 0.288 or 0.29kW is the power required to change the battery.

Therefore, the answer is letter D. 0.29kW
