IV. Problem Solving.

1.) Ten items purchased in a supermarket weigh 15 kg. Two of the items weigh 1 730 g. What is the total weight in kilograms of the other eight items?

Given:

10 items = 15kg

weight of 2 items = 1730g

Solution:

1730g = 1.73 kg

1kg = 1000 g

1730g x 1kg / 1000g = 1.73kg

Total weight of other 8 items = 15kg - 1.73kg

Total weight of other 8 items = 13.27kg

2.) There are 24 sakto bottles of coke in a pack. If each bottle contains 0.2 L, how many fluid ounces (fl. oz.) of coke does the pack contain? (1 mL = 0.03 fl. oz).

Given:

24 bottles of coke in a pack

each bottle = 0.2L

1 ml = 0.03fl oz

Solution:

24 bottles x 0.2L / 1 bottle = 4.8L

1 L = 1000ml

4.8L x 1000ml / 1L = 4800 ml

4800ml x 0.03 fl oz / 1 ml = 144 fl oz

How many fluid ounces (fl. oz.) of coke does the pack contain?

The pack of coke contains 144 fl oz
