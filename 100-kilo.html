14. Patulong po samga magaling sa math bukas na po kasi ipapasa Mrs . Reyes paid a total amount of not more than 800 pesos for two kilos of meat (m) and three kilos of fish (f). Suppose a kilo of meat cost 250 pesos . What will be the maximum cost of a kilo of fish ? A. f _> 100B . f <100 C. f>100 D. f _< 100 (SHOW YOUR SOLUTION )​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving linear equation.

Given

not more than 800 pesos for two kilos of meat

kilo of meat cost 250 pesos

Solution

Let

meat (m)

kilos of fish (f)

Mathematical equation

Total cost = Cost of fish × number of kilo of fish + cost of meat × number of kilo of meat

Note more than → ≤

m = 2

kilo of meet = 250

f = 3

let x is the cost of kilo of fish

250m + fx ≤ 800

250(2) + 3x ≤ 800

3x ≤ 800 - 500

3x ≤ 300

x ≤ 100

Answer

D. f ≤ 100
