16. in your own word summarize philippine clean water act of 2004 , republic act no.9275​


Answer:

The Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004, Republic Act No. 9275 aims to protect the country's water bodies from pollution by regulating the discharge of wastewater from industries, commercial establishments, and households. It establishes standards for the quality of water and sets penalties for violators. The law also mandates the creation of a wastewater management system and the establishment of a Water Quality Management Fund to support its implementation. Overall, the act seeks to ensure the sustainable use and conservation of the country's water resources for the benefit of present and future generations.


17. summarize Philippine clean water act of 2004 , Republic Act No.9275 and give example ​


Answer:

The Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004, Republic Act No. 9275, is a law that aims to protect and preserve the country's water resources by regulating the discharge of pollutants into bodies of water. It seeks to promote sustainable development by ensuring that the country's water resources are used in a manner that will not harm the environment.

The law establishes a National Water Quality Management Fund that will finance the implementation of the act, as well as the development and implementation of programs and projects aimed at promoting the conservation and protection of the country's water resources. It also provides for the creation of a Water Quality Management Area that will be responsible for the development and implementation of a water quality management plan for a specific body of water.

Under the law, all industries, commercial establishments, and households are required to install wastewater treatment facilities to ensure that the discharge of pollutants into bodies of water is minimized. The law also mandates the establishment of a water quality monitoring system that will ensure that the quality of the country's water resources is maintained.

An example of the implementation of the Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004 is the rehabilitation of the Pasig River. The government, in partnership with various stakeholders, has implemented measures to reduce the pollution of the river, such as the relocation of informal settlers and the strict enforcement of environmental laws. The efforts have resulted in a significant improvement in the water quality of the Pasig River, which has led to the revival of the river's ecosystem and the restoration of its economic and recreational value.

27. ano Ang naidudulot Ng pagsunod sa batas na Philippine clean water act​


Answer:

Ang pagpapatupad ng Philippine Clean Water Act ay may malaking epekto sa kalagayan ng kalikasan, kalusugan ng tao at kabuhayan ng mga Pilipino. Ang mga sumusunod ay ilan sa mga naidudulot nito:

1. Proteksyon sa kalikasan: Ang pagpapatupad ng batas na ito ay naglalayong maprotektahan ang ating mga ilog, lawa, dagat at iba pang mga yamang-tubig mula sa polusyon. Sa pamamagitan ng pagbabawal sa mga negosyo at industriya na mag-abono, magtapon ng basura, kemikal at iba pang mga bagay na makakasama sa kalikasan, nababawasan ang pagkasira ng mga yamang-tubig.

2. Kalusugan ng tao: Sa pamamagitan ng paglilinis ng mga yamang-tubig, nababawasan ang mga sakit at karamdaman na maaaring idulot ng maruming tubig. Ang mga tao ay hindi na magkakaroon ng mga sakit na maaaring makuha sa pag-inom ng maruming tubig tulad ng cholera, hepatitis A, at typhoid fever.

3. Kabuhayan ng mga Pilipino: Dahil sa malinis na tubig, mas maraming mga mangingisda, magsasaka, at iba pang mga industriya ang makakapagtrabaho ng maayos. Ang mga produkto ng paglilinis ng tubig tulad ng malinis na isda, gulay at iba pa ay mas malusog at mas mabenta kaysa sa mga produkto na galing sa maruming tubig.

4. Pagsunod sa batas at pagpapakita ng disiplina: Sa pamamagitan ng pagpapatupad ng batas na ito, ang mga negosyo at industriya ay hinihikayat na sumunod sa mga patakaran at magpakita ng disiplina sa kanilang pagtatapon ng basura, kemikal, at iba pang mga bagay na maaaring makasira sa kalikasan at kalusugan ng mga tao.
