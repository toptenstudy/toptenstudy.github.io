1. factors that affect solubility of vinegar and water​


Answer:

Solubility is the extent to which a solute is able to dissolve in a solvent under certain conditions. Factors that affect solubility are the quantity of solute and solvent, temperature, the intermolecular forces in the solute and solvent and other substances that may be in the solvent.
