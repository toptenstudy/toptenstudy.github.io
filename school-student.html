Answer:

The total number of students in the school is 900.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's assume that the total number of students in the school is "x".

We know that "5%" of the students are scholars, which can be expressed as:

5% of x = 45

To solve for "x", we can start by converting 5% to a decimal by dividing by 100:

0.05x = 45

Then we can solve for "x" by dividing both sides by 0.05:

x = 45 / 0.05

x = 900

Therefore, the total number of students in the school is 900.

23. At the beginning of the school year, the number of students in the band was the same as the number of students in the choir. Throughout the school year, the number of students in the band decreased by 8, and the number of students in the choir decreased by 4. At the end of the school year, the number of students in the choir was 25% more than the number of students in the band. How many students were in the band at the beginning of the school year? At the beginning of the school year, __________ students were in the band.


Answer:

24 students

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number of students in the band and in the choir at the beginning of the school year.

At the end of the school year,

No. of students in the band = x - 8

No. of students in the choir = x - 4

x - 4 = (1 + 0.25)(x - 8)

x - 4 = 1.25(x - 8)

x - 4 = 1.25x - 10

10 - 4 = 1.25x - x

6 = 0.25x

=> x = 24 students
