28. Mr. John, Ms. Chen and Mr. Tan are Partners in Business. Business profits are being shared 3:2:1 respectively. The total business profits to be divided by the three individuals is Php300,000. How much will Ms. Chen receive?


SOLUTION:

3x + 2x + x = 300, 000

6x = 300, 000

[tex]\frac{6x}{6}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{300, 000}{6}[/tex]

x = 50, 000


Mr. John => 3x => 3(50, 000) => Php150, 000

Ms. Chen => 2x => 2(50, 000) => Php100, 000

Mr. Tan => x => 1(50, 000) => Php50, 000


[tex]ANSWER: Php100, 000[/tex]
