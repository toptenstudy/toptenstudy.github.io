Interviewee: Please tell me more about it, Sir Mike.

Interviewee: I can handle that, Sir.

Interviewee: I studied accounting at [name of the school and include grades and achievements]. I am an excellent team player and enjoy working with people. After all, teamwork brings better results.

Interviewee: I've been looking for jobs that could further improve my skills and discover other potentials.

Interviewer: Anything else?

Interviewee: I also hope that it will help me grow in my field.

Interviewer: What are your strengths? Why should I hire you?

Interviewee: I am good at [skill] and cooperate with my team

*This is like selling your skillsets. Tell them what you are good at.

Interviewee: On certain occasions, Yes. I don't mind as long as it is according to the labor law standards.

Interviewer: Do you still have any questions for me?

Interviewee: What do you like best about working here?

*Never say no to this question. Always have a question prepared. It shows that you are sincere in working for the company.

Interviewee: Nice meeting you too, Sir Mike, and thank you for your consideration on this interview. OR Thank you for considering my application.
