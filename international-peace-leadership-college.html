4. vividly differentiate internal and external peace?​


Answer:

Such classifications are choices of the individual. There is no exact definitions for internal or external peace. What is happening around us, such as war, conflict or clashes, may be taken as external disturbance. How do we feel about it?. If we are not bothered about the external happenings, it may add to our internal peace. There are many other things, which will result in negative emotions, such as anger, fear or worry, which may also disturb our peace. But, anger, worry, or fear are generally, due to lack of proper understanding of the situation. Many people are able to win over it, through our wisdom or knowledge. If the external factors affect our mind, internal peace may be in question, but if we are able to absolve all negative factors, in the right sense, internal peace, or peace of mind, may not be affected.
