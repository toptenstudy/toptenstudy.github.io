Why do you want be an entrepreneur?

I want to be an entrepreneur because I believe I have the required talents and attributes to succeed. I have an eye for detail, a strong desire to solve problems, and the capacity to think critically and creatively. I am determined to succeed and am willing to take risks and work hard to reach my objectives. I am also committed to make a positive difference in the world. As an entrepreneur, I would be able to build something that would make a difference in people's lives. My goal is to use my abilities and knowledge to benefit others, and I believe that entrepreneurship is the best way to do so. Entrepreneurship, in my opinion, is a wonderful approach to get experience and develop talents. As an entrepreneur, I'd be able to learn and grow while acquiring useful expertise in a range of fields, including business, finance, marketing, and technology. This experience will provide me with a unique viewpoint that will be useful in whichever career I choose to pursue. At last, I want to be an entrepreneur because I believe it is an excellent method to achieve financial independence. Being an entrepreneur gives me control over my own life and finances, as well as the ability to make a living doing something I am passionate about. This is a fantastic opportunity for me to design a lifestyle that I am proud of.

21. define entrepreneur,entrepreneurial and entrepreneurship?​


entrepreneur

a person who organizes and operates a business or businesses, taking on greater than normal financial risks in order to do so.

entrepreneurial

characterized by the taking of financial risks in the hope of profit; enterprising.

entrepreneurship

the activity of setting up a business or businesses, taking on financial risks in the hope of profit.
