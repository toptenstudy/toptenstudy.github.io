​

Explanation:

To identify if a definition is formal or informal, first we need to know what is a definition. A definition is an expression of meaning or significance of a word or a phrase. These meaning can often be found in dictionaries.

In differentiating a formal definition from an informal one, we must understand that a formal definition contains the term to be defined, the class of object or concept that the term belongs to, and the differentiating characteristics that distinguishes it from the other class.

Ex. Biology (term) is a branch of scientific study (class) primarily concerned with the study of living organisms (differentiating characteristics).

Informal definitions, on the other hand, uses known words or examples to explain an unknown term. These definitions may be synonyms or antonyms introduced by or, in other words, or like.

Ex. Freedom, also referred to as liberty or independence, is a state people reach when they are free to think and do whatever they please.

David continued to fight for Corpuz case and got a favorable judgment. Corpuz refuse to pay David contending that since David refuse the first check given by him, he gave his services gratuitous. Question Is the service of David gratuitous? Explain your answer.​


Answer:Yes it is valid. While there was no express agreement between petitioner Corpus and respondent David as regards attorney’s fees, the facts of the case support the position of respondent David that there was at least an implied agreement for the payment of attorney’s fees.Payment of attorney’s fees to respondent David may be justified by virtue of the innominate contract of facio ut des (I do and you give which is based on the principle that “no one shall unjustly enrich himself at the expense of another.”Where one has rendered services to another, and these services are accepted by the latter, in the absence of proof that the service was rendered gratuitously, it is but just that he should pay a reasonable remuneration therefor because ‘it is a well-known principle of law, that no one should be permitted to enrich himself to the damage of another.

30. which of the following is not an effect of living out the beatitudes? a. teach us the final end to which god call us b. gratuitous gift of god c. confront us with decisive choices concerning earthly goods d. use of earthy goods in keeping with the law of mannonsense report​


Answer:

Many Christians can name the Ten Commandments and the Apostle’s Creed, but what are the Beatitudes? The Beatitudes are a set of teachings and blessings that Jesus gave in the Sermon on the Mount in the Gospel of Matthew. The messages found in the Beatitudes describe the foundation of the Christian faith. The purpose of the Beatitudes is to inspire Christians to live according to the traits Jesus describes. Some of these acts are simple, and some are grand, but they all form the cornerstone of the ideal Christian lifestyle. Therefore, living out the Beatitudes’ examples is very important for a Christian.
