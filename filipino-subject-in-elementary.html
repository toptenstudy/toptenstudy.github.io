14. Why english in elementary an important subject?


Because if they are taught in an early level, the students learn to adapt the language, the sound, the grammar. As they grow older, they become more effective and well-equipped to the language.

Fluency in English is an advantage when these students apply for work after they graduate. Hence, teaching them early is introducing the basic concepts to integrate their minds in the language.
