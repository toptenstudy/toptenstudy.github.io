14. If you perform 40 joules of work lifting a 10 N box from the floor to a shelf, how high is the shelf?


Answer:

4m

Step-by-step explanation:

1 Joules = 1 N-m

= 40 Joules x 1 N-m / 1 Joules

= 40 N-m

Distance from floor to shelf

= 40 N-m / 10 N

= 4m
