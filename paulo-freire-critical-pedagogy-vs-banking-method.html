Answer:

ABSTRACT Paulo Freire, a Brazilian philosopher, aims to liberate people. In order to achieve this aim, he offers problem-posing education. According to this method, designed as an alternative to traditional education models, education should not be provided through one-sided imposition by teachers.

Paulo Reglus Neves Freire was a Brazilian educator and philosopher who was a leading advocate of critical pedagogy. He is best known for his influential work Pedagogy of the Oppressed, which is generally considered one of the foundational texts of the critical pedagogy movement.

4. Allied banking corporation vs lim sio wan


Allied Banking Corporation vs Lim Sio Wan

On November 14, 1983, Lim Sio Wan deposited PhP 1,152,597.35 in the Allied Banking Corporation bank as money market placement.

On December 5, 1983, Christina So, pretended to be a representative of Lim Sio Wan and withdrew the PhP 1,152,597.35 amount. Christina So deposited it in the account of Filipinas Cement Corporation (FCC) in Metrobank.

Lim Sio Wan went to Allied Banking Corporation bank and said she did not know a certain Christina So and did not authorize her to withdraw her money.

Lim Sio Wan sued Allied Bank. Allied Bank sued Metrobank and Metrobank sued Filipinas Cement Corporation (FCC).

In the end, Allied Bank ended up paying back Lim Sio Wan and all of the damages.

14. how does paulo freire define oppression ​


Freire defines oppression as an act of exploitation, violence, and a failure “to recognize others as persons.” Not only do oppressors commit violence against the oppressed by keeping them from being fully human, they often stereotype oppressed people as “violent” for responding to oppression.
