Answer:

The number of permutations with identical objects is

[tex]N=\frac{r!}{r_{1}!r_{2}!r_{3}!...r_{k}!}[/tex]

In this problem, [tex]r=8+4+2=14[/tex], [tex]r_{1} =8[/tex], [tex]r_{2} =4[/tex] and [tex]r_{3} =2[/tex]

Therefore, the answer is:

[tex]N=\frac{14!}{8!4!2!} =45045[/tex]

19. Weiss threw a softball high in the air as Yang looked down from her two-storey home to see how high she threw it. Weiss threw the ball high enough to reach the floor of the second storey. If Weiss threw the ball at an initial velocity of 30 m/s, and an initial height of 1.22 m, with one storey equivalent to at least 3.05 meters, how high will the ball fly after five (5) seconds?​


Answer:

V^2=U^2+2*a*x

V=0 (as it is at max height)

U=30ms^-1 (initial speed)

a=-g /-9.8ms^-2 (as it is moving against gravity)

x is the variable you want to calculate (height)

0=30^2+2*(-9.8)*x

x=-30^2/2*-9.8

x=45.92m

Explanation:

With these questions it's best to just memorise the suvat equations and either draw or imagine the actions involved, that was you can tell what piece of given information translates to which variable. For example; I know that I am looking for max height, so when a ball is at its highest point, it can't be moving up anymore (thus V = 0). I also know that it is moving upwards against gravity, so gravity will be decelerating the ball (therefore a=-g). In a paper, it may as for assumptions with this question, a good answer would be no air resistance or movement due to any other external forces. I hope I helped :)

23. Is mezzanine applicable for two storey house?


Answer

A Mezzanine can be built in any structure as long as it doesn't violate the National Building Code of the Philippines.

Explanation

According to the National Building Code of the Philippines (NBCP) Republic Act (RA) 6541, Mezzanine floors shall have a clear ceiling height not less than 1.80 meters above and below it. Meaning, a floor should have a ceiling height of at least 3.70 to 4 meters to build a Mezzanine following Philippine standards.

Additional Information

A Mezzanine is an open space platform between the floor and the ceiling. It is usually seen in industrial structures, warehouses, malls, and modern townhouses. This is because a Mezzanine can help add more space on a floor level.
