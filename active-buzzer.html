13. What parts of buzzer?​


Answer:

The buzzer consists of an outside case with two pins to attach it to power and ground. Inside is a piezo element, which consists of a central ceramic disc surrounded by a metal (often bronze)vibration disc. When current is applied to the buzzer it causes the ceramic disk to contract or expand.

29. energy transformation of buzzer​


A buzzer converts electrical energy into sound energy. When an electric current passes through the buzzer's coil, it produces a magnetic field that attracts and repels a metal disk. This disk vibrates and produces sound waves that travel through the air. Therefore, the energy transformation of a buzzer is from electrical energy to sound energy.
